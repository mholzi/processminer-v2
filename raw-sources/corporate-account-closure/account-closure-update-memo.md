## Purpose

This memo records an operational update to the Corporate Account Closure process following the Q2 controls review. It supersedes the relevant figures in the Detailed Process Document where they differ.

## Residual balance disbursement — callback threshold

Following the Q2 controls review, the callback verification threshold for residual balance disbursements has been lowered to EUR 100,000 equivalent. Any disbursement at or above EUR 100,000 now requires a callback confirmation of the destination account with the client before the funds are released. This tightens the previous arrangement and brings the Corporate Account Closure process into line with the bank's revised payments-verification standard.

## Unchanged items

The memo confirms the following are unchanged from the baseline DTP:

- The 4-eyes closure approval control (a separate Closure Approver authorises every closure).
- The standard closure SLA of 10 business days for cases with no exception.
- The eight-step process flow, from receive-and-log through confirm-and-archive.

## Action

Operations to update the desk procedures and the Client Lifecycle Workflow Tool configuration to reflect the EUR 100,000 callback threshold by month-end.
