## 1. Purpose

This memo records a single service-level change to the Debit Card Replacement
process, effective immediately. All other steps, controls, exceptions and
roles in the baseline Detailed Process Document are unchanged.

## 2. Change

The card bureau moved to a faster production and courier-handover arrangement
in May 2026. As a result, the **card delivery service level has been shortened
from 5–7 business days to 4–6 business days** from the date the replacement
card is ordered.

This is the only metric affected. The "card block applied" and "replacement
card ordered" service levels are unchanged.
