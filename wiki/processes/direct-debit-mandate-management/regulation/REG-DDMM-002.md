## What it requires
Counterparties to payment transactions must be screened against EU and UN consolidated sanctions lists before processing; confirmed matches must be frozen and reported to the competent authority.

## Why it applies
The process involves debtor and creditor identities on each mandate; engaging — even inadvertently — with a sanctioned party constitutes a violation of EU sanctions law.

## How it is met
CP-DDMM-002 screens debtor and creditor names automatically at PS-DDMM-003; EX-DDMM-002 freezes the mandate and escalates confirmed hits to Compliance and Financial Crime.
