## What it requires
Debtor personal data — name and IBAN — must be processed on a lawful basis, held only as long as necessary, and subject to documented retention and deletion procedures.

## Why it applies
The mandate register holds debtor name and IBAN for the lifetime of the mandate and beyond; GDPR governs the lawful basis for that storage and the maximum retention period.

## How it is met
CP-DDMM-005 reviews dormant mandates quarterly; the retention and purge period is not yet defined — PG-DDMM-003 records this as an open gap.
