## What it requires
Banks must maintain adequate internal controls, segregation of duties, and operational risk management for payment processes; operational risks must be identifiable and monitored.

## Why it applies
The mandate management process is a regulated payment operation; MaRisk/KWG impose the internal-control and operational-risk obligations that this process's dual-control, reconciliation, and screening controls exist to satisfy.

## How it is met
CP-DDMM-003 enforces dual-control and segregation of duties on bulk uploads; CP-DDMM-004 provides daily operational reconciliation; defined escalation paths to the Payments Ops Lead cover residual operational risk.
