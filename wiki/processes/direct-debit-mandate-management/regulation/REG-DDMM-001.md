## What it requires
Mandates must carry a valid UMR, Creditor Identifier, and debtor IBAN; R-transactions must be handled per scheme-defined reason codes and timelines; creditors must be confirmed on successful registration.

## Why it applies
The bank registers and maintains SDD mandates on behalf of corporate creditors who collect under the SEPA scheme; every mandate must be scheme-compliant to support a valid collection.

## How it is met
CP-DDMM-001 validates mandate data at registration; PS-DDMM-007 handles R-transactions per scheme codes; PS-DDMM-006 returns a scheme-required confirmation to the creditor.
