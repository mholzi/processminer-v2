## What it requires
SDD collections must rest on a valid mandate representing the debtor's authorisation; debtors hold an unconditional 8-week refund right for authorised SDD collections; R-transaction handling obligations must be fulfilled.

## Why it applies
The bank holds the mandates authorising corporate creditors' SDD collections; a mandate held incorrectly or not cancelled when invalid undermines the debtor's PSD2 refund rights and the bank's liability position.

## How it is met
CP-DDMM-001 ensures mandate data is scheme-valid; PS-DDMM-007 handles R-transactions including refund-related reason codes (MD01/AC04); PS-DDMM-005 maintains an accurate active mandate register.
