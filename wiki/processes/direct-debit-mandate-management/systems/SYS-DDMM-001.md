## Purpose
Online channel through which corporate creditor clients submit, amend, and cancel mandate requests and receive registration confirmations.

## Role in this process
Receives mandate requests in Step 1 and returns type-specific confirmations in Step 6. Also the creditor-facing status display for in-flight and exception-state requests (EX-DDMM-002 and EX-DDMM-004 show as 'pending / in progress') and return channel for EX-DDMM-001 reason-coded rejections.
