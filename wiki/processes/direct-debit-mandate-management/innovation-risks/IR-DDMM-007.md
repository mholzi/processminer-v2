## The risk
An AI-assembled sanctions context pack (II-DDMM-005) frames the adjudication before the Compliance officer begins their review. If the assembly logic omits or down-weights a relevant signal — an alias, a linked entity, a recent designation — the officer may anchor on the pack and miss it.

## Likelihood & impact
Moderate likelihood; automation bias is well-documented when AI summaries are presented alongside raw data. Impact is MEDIUM to HIGH depending on the nature of the missed signal: a missed sanctions hit exposes the bank to regulatory breach and reputational damage.

## Mitigation
The context pack is presented as supplementary context only — not a decision recommendation. Raw data sources remain directly accessible and must be reviewed independently. Assembly logic, signal coverage, and omission rates are audited periodically by Compliance; officers trained explicitly on anchoring risk.
