## The gap
OAF-DDMM-001 requires reconciliation discrepancies to be evidenced to closure. TD-DDMM-010 shortens the batch sync interval, reducing discrepancy frequency, but no decision defines the evidencing procedure the audit finding requires.

## Resolution
Define and document a mandatory closure-evidencing procedure within CP-DDMM-004: each reconciliation discrepancy must have a recorded resolution action and sign-off before the daily cycle closes. Implement as a mandatory MMS step and confirm with Internal Audit that the approach satisfies OAF-DDMM-001.

## Status
OPEN. Requires a targeted process decision and Internal Audit confirmation of the evidencing procedure.
