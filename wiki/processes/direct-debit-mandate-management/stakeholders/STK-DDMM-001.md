## Who they are
Internal Financial Crime team, sitting within the wider Compliance & Financial Crime function but operationally separate from the Sanctions Compliance team.

## Stake in the process
Engaged when a confirmed sanctions hit (EX-DDMM-002) may also imply money-laundering activity or trigger a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) obligation. Their involvement determines whether a SAR is filed and whether law enforcement is notified.

## Engagement
Pulled in by Compliance on a confirmed sanctions hit (EX-DDMM-002 path only). No standing involvement in routine mandate registration or R-transaction handling.
