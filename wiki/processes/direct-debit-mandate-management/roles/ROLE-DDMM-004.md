Compliance is accountable and responsible for adjudicating potential sanctions hits flagged during the sanctions screening step.

## In this process
Accountable for sanctions hit adjudication in Step 3. Consulted in Step 7 only when R-transaction resolution surfaces a sanctions or financial-crime concern. Financial Crime is a separate team engaged only on confirmed hits (EX-DDMM-002 path).
