## Responsibility
The Payments Ops Lead is accountable for mandate data validation outcomes, registration outcomes, and R-transaction resolution, and is the escalation point for technical failures and exceptional situations in the process.

## In this process
Accountable for mandate data validation outcomes in Step 2, registration outcomes in Step 5, and R-transaction resolution outcomes in Step 7. Receives escalations on MMS registration failures (E-4) and coordinates with IT support on resolution and retry. Informed of confirmed sanctions hits (EX-DDMM-002) as frozen mandates are operationally significant.
