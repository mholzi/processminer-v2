## Responsibility
The corporate creditor client is accountable for submitting accurate and complete mandate requests to the bank via the Creditor Portal.

## In this process
Initiates the process by submitting new mandate requests, amendments, and cancellations via the Creditor Portal. Receives registration confirmations in Step 6. Is notified during R-transaction resolution in Step 7 for MD01 (reversal), MD02 (re-present request), and AC04 (new mandate required) — not for SL01, where the restriction is applied silently.
