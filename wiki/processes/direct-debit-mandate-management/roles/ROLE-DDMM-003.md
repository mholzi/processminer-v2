## Responsibility
A senior Payments Operations clerk designated as checker for a given bulk batch on a rotating basis — never the same person who submitted that batch (segregation of duties). Provides independent second-line review of bulk mandate uploads.

## In this process
Reviews and approves bulk mandate files above 50 mandates in Step 4, providing the dual-control sign-off before registration. At Step 7, consulted on MD02 and AC04 resolutions — the reason codes that mutate a live registered mandate record; MD01 and SL01 are handled by the Mandate Clerk solo.
