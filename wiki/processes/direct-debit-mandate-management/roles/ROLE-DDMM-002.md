## Responsibility
The Mandate Clerk is the primary human actor in the process, performing mandate data validation, dual-control review for bulk uploads, MMS registration, and R-transaction resolution.

## In this process
Performs mandate data validation in Step 2. At Step 3, conducts first-line triage of potential sanctions hits before routing to Compliance; clean items pass informationally. Reviews bulk files in Step 4, registers mandates in MMS in Step 5, and resolves R-transactions in Step 7. Step 6 is automated.
