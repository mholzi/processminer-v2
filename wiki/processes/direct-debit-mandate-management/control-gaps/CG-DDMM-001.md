## The gap
No documented retention period exists for mandate data in the MMS. GDPR Art. 5(1)(e) requires a defined storage-limitation schedule; its absence means data may be held beyond legitimate purpose. The dormancy review in CP-DDMM-005 also lacks a defined threshold, leaving the control unauditable.

## Risk
Regulatory breach under GDPR Art. 5(1)(e); supervisory fines and data-subject complaints. The absent dormancy threshold also prevents consistent application and independent audit of CP-DDMM-005.

## Remediation
Define and document a retention period for mandate records (active life plus statutory minimum post-cancellation); apply the same threshold as the dormancy trigger in CP-DDMM-005 and close PG-DDMM-003.
