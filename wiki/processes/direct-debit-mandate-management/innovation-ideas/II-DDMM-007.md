## The idea
Replace the fixed 50-mandate batch threshold with a risk-based routing model assessing each batch by total value, creditor risk tier, and request type. Routine low-risk batches receive a lighter spot-check; higher-risk batches get full dual-control. Extend the checker pool with a lighter certification track for routine work, removing dependency on a single scheduled checker.

## Expected benefit
Eliminates the scheduling bottleneck by broadening the eligible checker pool and making review workload proportional to risk. Provides a compliance-aligned path to extend four-eyes coverage to single mandates on a risk basis, addressing CG-DDMM-003 without mandating review on every trivial record.

## Feasibility
Requires MMS to expose risk-scoring attributes for batch routing and an updated certification framework for the broader checker pool. Risk-scoring criteria must be agreed with Compliance and Risk. PG-DDMM-001 threshold question is resolved as part of this initiative.
