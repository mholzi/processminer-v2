## The idea
When MMS routes a potential sanctions hit to Compliance, automatically assemble the triage context pack — mandate history, creditor profile, screening match details, watchlist source, and any prior Compliance decisions for the same party — and attach it to the escalation record.

## Expected benefit
Eliminates the repetitive manual context-gathering cycle and may reduce sanctions adjudication time. Compliance receives a richer pack than current manual assembly produces, reducing back-and-forth requests for additional context.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity — requires integration between MMS, the Sanctions Screening Engine, and Compliance workflow. The data already exists across these systems; the gap is automated assembly and presentation. Dependency: Compliance must agree on a standard pack format before build.
