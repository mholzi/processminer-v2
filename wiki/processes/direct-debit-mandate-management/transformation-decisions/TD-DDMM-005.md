## The decision
Replace the fixed 50-mandate batch threshold with a Compliance-approved risk-based routing model in MMS; create a lighter checker certification track for routine spot-checks; extend four-eyes coverage to high-risk single-mandate registrations.

## Options considered
- Retain fixed threshold and add a second Checker (no risk calibration, no single-mandate coverage)
- Risk-based MMS routing with two review tracks and broader checker certification (chosen)
- Waive dual-control on single-mandate registrations as disproportionate (non-compliant — rejected)

## Rationale
Adding a second Checker at the fixed threshold leaves CG-DDMM-003 open and does not calibrate control to risk. Waiving dual-control on singles is non-compliant. Risk-based routing is the only option that resolves the bottleneck and the compliance gap simultaneously under a single Compliance-approved model.
