## Description
The sanctions screening step returns a confirmed hit against the debtor or creditor name, indicating a potential match with a sanctioned party.

## Handling
The mandate is frozen and escalated to Compliance and Financial Crime. No portal notification is sent to the creditor — tipping-off rules prohibit disclosing a sanctions investigation, so the mandate appears only as 'pending / in progress' in the portal with no reason given. Registration is blocked until Compliance clears the parties.

## Impact
Registration is blocked indefinitely pending investigation. Collections are frozen for the affected debtor. A confirmed hit may also trigger regulatory reporting obligations and a wider review of the creditor relationship.
