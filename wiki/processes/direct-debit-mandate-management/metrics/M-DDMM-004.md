## Definition
Compliance adjudication window from the point the Mandate Clerk routes a potential hit to Compliance (post first-line triage) to the adjudication decision (cleared or confirmed hit). Clerk triage time is counted in M-DDMM-001.

## Current reading
No measured actual is available; the SLA target is within 1 business day.

## Why it matters
Adjudication delays block mandate registration and may prevent the creditor from initiating collections in a timely manner.
