## Description
Out-of-band support route through which creditors escalate queries, chase delayed registrations, or request clarification on rejection reason codes via their RM or a Payments service desk email.

## Usage
Activated when the portal's pending / in-progress status gives insufficient detail — which is always, since the portal shows no reason-level information. All creditor queries about process status are relayed indirectly to Payments Operations through this route.
