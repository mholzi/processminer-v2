## Description
Self-service web application through which corporate creditors log in to submit single mandate requests, smaller bulk files, and view the status of in-flight mandates.

## Usage
Used for the large majority of mandate submissions — single mandates and smaller bulk batches. Provides the only real-time status view available to the creditor throughout the registration process.
