## Finding
The annual Internal Audit review (Feb 2026) found that the daily MMS / Payment Hub reconciliation (CP-DDMM-004) is performed but discrepancies are not tracked to closure with documented evidence — there is no discrepancy log showing each item was investigated and resolved. The control operates but cannot be evidenced.

## Recommendation
Implement a structured reconciliation discrepancy log with mandatory sign-off for each item; retain records for audit purposes in line with MaRisk operational-risk documentation requirements.
