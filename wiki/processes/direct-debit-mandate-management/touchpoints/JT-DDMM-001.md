## What the client does
The creditor logs into the Creditor Portal, completes the mandate submission form — entering UMR, CI, debtor name, IBAN, mandate type and signature date — and submits the request.

## What the bank does
The Creditor Portal receives and queues the request; the creditor sees an immediate acknowledgement confirming receipt and a request reference.

## Experience
Familiar for repeat users with established creditor data; format rules for UMR and CI can catch first-time submitters. No in-form guidance — data-entry errors only surface after submission, at validation.
