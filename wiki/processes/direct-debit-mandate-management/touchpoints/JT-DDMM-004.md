## What the client does
The creditor logs into the portal to check the status of a submitted mandate and sees pending / in-progress. If they need more detail, they contact their RM or the Payments service desk.

## What the bank does
The portal displays a binary status label — pending or in-progress — with no reason, timeline, or estimated completion. Queries relayed via RM or service desk are forwarded to Payments Operations.

## Experience
The creditor has no visibility into why a mandate is pending or how long it will take. Opacity is especially acute during sanctions screening, where the reason for the hold cannot be disclosed. Uncertainty is high and trust is tested.
