## What the client does
The creditor receives the registration confirmation in the Creditor Portal — the mandate is now active — and can begin scheduling direct debit collections.

## What the bank does
The portal delivers a system-generated confirmation with content tailored to request type — new registration, amendment, or cancellation — closing the processing loop.

## Experience
A high-value moment — the creditor can finally act on the mandate. The confirmation is unambiguous and type-specific. Relief is marked after what may have been an opaque wait, particularly when the SLA approached its limit.
