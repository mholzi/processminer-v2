## The trend
Banks are deploying rules-based and AI-assisted workflow orchestration tools to automatically prioritise operations work queues by SLA proximity, risk profile, and item value. Manual queue scanning is being replaced by dynamic, configurable routing that surfaces the highest-priority items automatically.

## Relevance
The DDMM process relies on Mandate Clerks scanning an undifferentiated arrival-order queue every shift, causing silent SLA breaches and misallocation of effort. Intelligent queue prioritisation is a direct structural fix for this systemic cost.

## Evidence
Industry reporting indicates material reductions in SLA breaches where banks have adopted intelligent queue orchestration; specific figures are indicative only and require verification in a source-innovation pass before being treated as cited benchmarks.
