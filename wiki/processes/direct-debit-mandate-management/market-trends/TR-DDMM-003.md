## The trend
Financial institutions are deploying AI and ML to reduce false-positive rates in sanctions screening and to automate assembly of triage context packs for compliance officers, materially reducing manual adjudication time per alert.

## Relevance
The DDMM process generates repetitive manual context-gathering work when Compliance adjudicates potential screening hits. AI-assisted pack assembly would convert a reactive, clerk-intensive workflow into a structured, near-automated hand-off.

## Evidence
High false-positive rates in rule-based sanctions screening and measurable analyst time reductions from AI-assisted triage are commonly cited in compliance technology literature; all figures here are illustrative and require verified sources before use.
