## The gap
The DTP did not document the dual-control rejection loopback. When the Checker rejects a batch, it returns to the Mandate Clerk for correction; creditor-supplied discrepancies are split out via EX-DDMM-001. The correction procedure is not yet documented.

## Impact
Without a documented correction procedure, clerks handling rejected batches may follow different steps, leading to inconsistent handling and potential re-work loops.

## Next step
Document the full correction procedure: what the Mandate Clerk does on receiving a rejected batch and how the decision to split out creditor-supplied discrepancies to EX-DDMM-001 is determined.
