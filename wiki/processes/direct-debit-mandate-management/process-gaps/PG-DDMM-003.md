## The gap
The document does not specify how long cancelled or dormant mandates must be retained in MMS before they can be purged, leaving the data lifecycle undefined.

## Impact
Without a documented retention period the process cannot comply with any applicable record-keeping obligations and the mandate register may accumulate stale records indefinitely.

## Next step
Define and document a retention period for cancelled and dormant mandates (check SEPA scheme rules and internal data governance policy), and define the dormancy threshold that feeds CP-DDMM-005.
