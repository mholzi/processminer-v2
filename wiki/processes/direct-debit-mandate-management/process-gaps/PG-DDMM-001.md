## The gap
The 50-mandate threshold that triggers the dual-control check is confirmed as a fixed hard rule — not risk-based and not varying by creditor or file type. Resolved during foundational run (CP-DDMM-003 review).

## Impact
Without clarity the process could not be correctly documented or consistently applied; a risk-based threshold would require different governance and monitoring than a hard rule.

## Next step
No current-state action required. Whether the fixed threshold should become risk-based (e.g. by creditor risk score or mandate value) is a target-state design question.
