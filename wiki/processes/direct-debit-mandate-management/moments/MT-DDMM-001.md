## The moment
A first-time creditor submits their first mandate via the Creditor Portal; unfamiliar UMR and CI format rules may silently fail at validation, making the first interaction a test rather than a welcome.

## Why it matters
First impressions shape the creditor's long-term confidence in the portal and, by extension, in the bank's operations team. A rejection on the first submission — especially one the creditor could not have anticipated — primes frustration for every subsequent interaction.

## Design implication
Inline format validation and contextual tooltips on UMR and CI fields would catch errors before submission, converting what is currently a silent post-submission failure into a guided first experience.
