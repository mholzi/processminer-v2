## The moment
The creditor checks the portal repeatedly and sees only pending / in-progress — no reason, no timeline, no contact. The wait is most damaging when the hold is at sanctions screening and disclosure is prohibited.

## Why it matters
A creditor with a live collection campaign dependent on the mandate has time-sensitive business exposure during the wait. The inability to estimate completion or escalate directly erodes confidence in the bank's operational reliability, even when processing is progressing normally.

## Design implication
Even without disclosing a hold reason, the portal could show an estimated completion range and confirm active processing. A proactive SLA-risk notification would contain creditor anxiety without exposing sensitive operational detail.
