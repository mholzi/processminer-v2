## What it checks
Verifies that each incoming guarantee application contains all mandatory fields: beneficiary details, guarantee amount and currency, wording type, validity period, and underlying commercial contract reference.

## Control activity
Trade Finance Operations analyst reviews the application against a standard completeness checklist before logging it in the Trade Finance System. Incomplete applications are returned to the client or relationship manager with a request for missing information before processing begins.

## Risk addressed
Prevents downstream rework caused by an application being partially processed before a missing field is discovered, and ensures the sanctions screening and credit check steps receive a complete data set.

## Timing
Performed once at intake, immediately upon receipt of the application, before any downstream step is initiated.
