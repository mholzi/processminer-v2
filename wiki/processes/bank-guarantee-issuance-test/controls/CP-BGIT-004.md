## What it checks
Confirms that bespoke guarantee wording has been reviewed and signed off by the Legal team before the application advances to issuance. Applies only to bespoke-wording guarantees; standard wording is not subject to this control.

## Control activity
The Legal team reviews the bespoke wording draft submitted by the Trade Finance Officer and provides written sign-off. The application does not proceed to sanctions screening until sign-off is received.

## Risk addressed
Risk of issuing a guarantee with wording that creates unintended legal exposure or does not accurately reflect the underlying commercial obligation.

## Timing
Performed during Step 3 (Wording Review), before the application advances to sanctions and compliance screening.
