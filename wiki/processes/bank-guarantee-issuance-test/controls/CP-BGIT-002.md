## What it checks
Verifies that the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country are not subject to sanctions before issuance proceeds.

## Control activity
The Compliance Analyst screens the beneficiary and beneficiary country using the Sanctions Screening Tool; the screening result is attached to the application. A screening hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.

## Risk addressed
Risk of issuing a guarantee in favour of a sanctioned party, breaching EU sanctions regulations.

## Timing
Executed for every application at Step 4 before issuance approval.
