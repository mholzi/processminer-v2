## What it checks
Verifies that cash collateral has been received and blocked before issuance of a partially-secured guarantee can proceed.

## Control activity
The Trade Finance Officer confirms receipt and blocking of cash collateral for partially-secured guarantees. Issuance cannot proceed until this confirmation is recorded.

## Risk addressed
Risk of issuing a guarantee without adequate security cover for partially-secured instruments.

## Timing
Executed at the Collateral Confirmation step, before issuance approval at Step 6.
