## The journey
Contour connected banks and corporates on a shared blockchain for documentary trade, offering real-time guarantee and LC processing with a paperless, multi-party workflow. Founded by a bank consortium (HSBC, Standard Chartered, others), it wound down in Oct 2023, was acquired by Xalts in Feb 2024, then by XDC Ventures in Oct 2025.

## Relevance
Contour's lifecycle — promising blockchain-native client experience, consortium collapse through funding failure, two acquisitions — benchmarks the network-effect risk for shared-infrastructure guarantee digitisation. Its partial recovery also signals continued demand for interoperable, real-time guarantee platforms among large corporates.
