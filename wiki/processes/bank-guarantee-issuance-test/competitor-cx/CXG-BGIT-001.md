## The journey
Corporate clients submit guarantee applications via HSBC Trade Solutions; eligible standard-wording guarantees receive fully automated STP processing with zero human intervention, reducing issuance from 3–4 business days to potentially minutes. First live transactions completed December 2023 for KPMG Qatar (tender bond) and Russell Finex Ltd.

## Relevance
HSBC's sub-day STP issuance directly challenges Deutsche Bank's 3-business-day target. Ranked #1 global trade finance provider by Euromoney 2026, HSBC defines the speed expectation that corporate clients increasingly treat as standard, particularly for straightforward standard-wording guarantees.

HSBC GBM Insights press release (Dec 2023); GTR confirming KPMG Qatar STP guarantee; Euromoney Trade Finance Survey 2026 global #1 ranking.
