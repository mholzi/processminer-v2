## The journey
Corporate clients access guarantees through the Centric portal, managing the full lifecycle — issuance, amendment, monitoring, release — in a single interface. In November 2024, BNP Paribas piloted a SWIFT-ICC API standard transmitting guarantee requests from corporate ERP in real-time without manual intervention.

## Relevance
Voted Euromoney's Best Trade Finance Bank in Europe 2025, BNP Paribas's API-first guarantee pilot directly benchmarks Deutsche Bank's SWIFT transmission step. It sets the standard for straight-through processing that eliminates manual handoffs between the client's ERP and the bank's issuance system.
