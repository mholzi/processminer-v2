## The journey
Corporate clients instruct guarantees directly from Komgo's multi-bank Konsole portal; ICC-SWIFT API standards automate instruction receipt at Standard Chartered and trigger accelerated digital issuance. The bank completed the first live ICC-SWIFT API guarantee transaction in January 2026 and plans progressive global rollout.

## Relevance
Standard Chartered's multi-portal, API-native connectivity model — where clients instruct from their own trade platform without a bank-specific UI — contrasts with single-portal approaches. Real-time interoperability directly addresses corporate pain points around multiple log-ins and fragmented guarantee visibility.
