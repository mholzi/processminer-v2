## The journey
Corporates access the UC Trade Finance Gate via SSO through UniCredit's Corporate Portal. They issue, monitor, amend, and release domestic and international guarantees in one system, with real-time status notifications on all open deals. EBICS multi-banking support allows data exchange with other banks.

## Relevance
UniCredit's single-platform consolidation — all guarantee types, real-time status, SSO, and multi-bank EBICS connectivity — illustrates the CX standard for pan-European corporates managing guarantees across multiple jurisdictions, a segment that closely overlaps with Deutsche Bank's corporate client base.
