## The journey
Corporates apply for guarantees and SBLCs via Trade Channel, J.P. Morgan's global online trade portal; real-time transaction status (received, in process, settled) is available 24/7, with mobile-app approval workflows eliminating wet signatures and accelerating the four-eyes approval step for corporate treasury teams.

## Relevance
Trade Channel's 24/7 real-time status and mobile approval chain mirror Deutsche Bank's approval and transmission steps and signal client expectations. Ranked #1 in trade digital transformation by Coalition Greenwich 2024, it confirms that always-on visibility and paperless approvals have become table stakes.
