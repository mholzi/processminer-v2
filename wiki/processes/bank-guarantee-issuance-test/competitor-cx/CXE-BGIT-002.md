## The journey
Corporates apply for and track guarantees via ING's InsideBusiness portal, 24/7. Integration with Komgo eliminated email threads and paper applications, delivering an end-to-end digital workflow with automated data integration into ING's backend systems, reducing manual errors and turnaround times.

## Relevance
ING's Komgo-powered journey directly targets the same pain points as Deutsche Bank's process: back-and-forth communication and slow review cycles. Live credit-line visibility and no manual handoffs set a clear bar for the guarantee CX, particularly against DB's email-based Legal handoff (PG-BGIT-002).

Komgo case study on ING (2024): end-to-end digital workflow; tangible efficiency gains and reduction in manual errors confirmed.
