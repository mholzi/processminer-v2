## The journey
Corporates submit guarantee applications via RIVO's cloud portal. AI parses email-based requests, extracts instrument type, beneficiary, value, expiry and wording terms into structured data, and forwards instructions to the bank. Real-time status updates and multi-bank visibility replace separate portals and email chains.

## Relevance
RIVO's AI-to-structured-data conversion targets the manual wording-review and data-entry steps that extend Deutsche Bank's 3-day SLA. DZ BANK's 2024 deployment for guarantee issuance acceleration is a direct peer-bank benchmark for DB's own upgrade path.

## Evidence
Surecomp press release Nov 2024: AI commitment to 30% processing-time reduction; DZ BANK RIVO live deployment for guarantee issuance.
