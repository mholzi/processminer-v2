## The moment
The client clicks submit and receives a portal confirmation that the application has been received and is under review.

## Why it matters
This is the client's first signal of what to expect. Without a visible SLA or turnaround estimate, the client cannot judge whether the process is on track, making every subsequent day of silence a potential source of anxiety.

## Design implication
The confirmation screen should display an estimated turnaround based on wording type and pre-approved facility status — standard within limit versus bespoke or limit referral — giving the client a realistic SLA to track against.
