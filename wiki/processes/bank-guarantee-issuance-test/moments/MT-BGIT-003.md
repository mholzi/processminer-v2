## The moment
The client's bespoke-wording application is with Legal for review, and the portal shows only a generic in-progress status with no indication of when Legal sign-off will come.

## Why it matters
Bespoke guarantees are typically for higher-value or time-sensitive commercial contracts. An SLA-free Legal review running into multiple business days creates direct commercial risk for the client and erodes confidence in the bank's trade finance competence.

## Design implication
The portal should explicitly show that the application is at Legal review and, once VG-BGIT-002 is resolved, display a timer. The client should receive an immediate notification when Legal sign-off is given.
