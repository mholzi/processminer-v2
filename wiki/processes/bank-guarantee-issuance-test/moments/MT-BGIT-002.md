## The moment
After submitting, the client notices days have passed with no visible portal progress — the application is silently parked awaiting a Credit team limit review.

## Why it matters
A silent stall is the highest-impact negative moment in the process. The client infers something is wrong, calls the RM, and starts to question the bank's competence. PP-BGIT-001 is the most common source of SLA breach.

## Design implication
When an application is parked at Step 2, the portal must push a proactive notification to the client explaining the reason and the expected resolution timeline — eliminating the need to chase through the RM.
