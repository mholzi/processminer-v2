## The trend
Banks are replacing batch-based sanctions screening with AI-powered real-time monitoring, with specialist vendors integrating directly with SWIFT's messaging network to screen every guarantee instruction at the point of issuance rather than as a separate sequential step.

## Relevance
Deutsche Bank's sanctions screening step (PS-BGIT-004) operates as a manual control within the issuance workflow. Real-time RegTech integration at SWIFT level could shift screening to a continuous background check, reducing cycle time without reducing compliance coverage.

## Evidence
RegTech market CAGR 19.2% (2026–2036), reaching $116.7 billion by 2036; GSS and SWIFT announced automated guarantee screening integration in October 2024.
