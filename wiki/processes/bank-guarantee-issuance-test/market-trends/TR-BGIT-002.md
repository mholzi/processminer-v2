## The trend
Banks are deploying AI agents that autonomously validate guarantee wording against ICC rules, extract structured data from email instructions and route exceptions to human reviewers — targeting 75% reductions in per-transaction processing time. 45% of banks were live on AI trade finance tools by end-2025.

## Relevance
Both the bespoke wording Legal handoff and the manual completeness check at intake are candidates for agentic automation. AI agents can pre-validate completeness and flag wording deviations before human review, compressing the multi-day bottlenecks documented in PP-BGIT-001 and PP-BGIT-002 to minutes.

## Evidence
44% of finance teams will use agentic AI in 2026, a 600% increase from 2025; banks targeting this area report ~75% turnaround reduction — from ~2 hours to ~30 minutes per transaction.
