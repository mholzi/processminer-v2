## The trend
ICC and SWIFT published the first open-API industry standards for bank guarantees and standby LCs in October 2025, enabling structured, machine-readable corporate-to-bank guarantee instructions without SWIFT membership. Seven banks are already implementing the standard; Standard Chartered completed the first live production transaction in January 2026.

## Relevance
The standard directly addresses this process's intake bottleneck: corporate clients submit structured, validated guarantee instructions via API rather than through a portal form or email, eliminating manual re-keying, incompleteness errors and the email-only handoff documented in PG-BGIT-002.

## Evidence
Seven banks implementing C2B Guarantee APIs as of October 2025; Standard Chartered executed the first ICC-SWIFT-compliant live guarantee transaction in January 2026.
