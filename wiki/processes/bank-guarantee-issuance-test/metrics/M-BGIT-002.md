## Definition
The total number of bank guarantee instruments issued per calendar month, measured from application receipt to SWIFT MT760 dispatch.

## Current reading
Approximately 180 guarantees per month on a twelve-month rolling average, with a November–December peak approximately 40% above the mean, driven by commercial contract closings. Volume growing approximately 8% year-on-year.

## Why it matters
Volume drives staffing and system-capacity planning. The seasonal peak means the target 70% STP rate must be achievable at 250+ guarantees per month, not just at the base volume, or the peak will require disproportionate manual headcount.
