## What happens
For standard-wording guarantees, Step 3 is a no-op — the application proceeds directly to sanctions screening without TFO review. For bespoke wording, the TFO emails the draft to the Legal team for review and sign-off before issuance can proceed.

## Inputs
- Application with wording type identified in Step 1
- Standard guarantee wording template or bespoke wording draft

## Outputs
- Standard-wording application cleared to proceed
- Bespoke-wording application with Legal sign-off

## Why it matters
Ensures guarantee wording is legally sound before issuance; bespoke wording without Legal review would expose the bank to contractual risk.
