## The gap
There is no committed SLA for how quickly the Legal team must review and sign off bespoke guarantee wording. Review turnaround is informal and unmonitored.

## Impact
Bespoke-wording applications face variable and unpredictable delays at Step 3, directly causing the delay pattern documented in PP-BGIT-002 and risking breach of the 3-business-day end-to-end target.

## Next step
Agree and publish a Legal review SLA for guarantee wording (e.g. 1 business day for standard bespoke; 2 for complex), with escalation to Head of Legal if breached.
