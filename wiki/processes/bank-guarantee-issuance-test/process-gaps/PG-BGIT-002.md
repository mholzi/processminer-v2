## The gap
The bespoke wording handoff from the Trade Finance Officer to the Legal team is handled by email with no system-supported workflow. There is no tracking, no automatic routing, and no record in the Trade Finance System.

## Impact
Without a system workflow, there is no audit trail for the handoff, no automatic escalation if Legal does not respond, and no visibility into outstanding Legal reviews.

## Next step
Integrate the Legal review workflow into the Trade Finance System so that bespoke wording review requests, responses, and sign-offs are tracked and visible end-to-end.
