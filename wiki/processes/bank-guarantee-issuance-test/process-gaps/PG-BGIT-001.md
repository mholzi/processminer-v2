## The gap
There is no formal SLA governing how quickly the Credit team must respond when an application is parked at Step 2 for limit review. The informal expectation is 1–2 business days, but this is not enforced.

## Impact
Applications can stall indefinitely awaiting Credit team decisions, creating the most common source of delay in the guarantee issuance process and risk of breaching the 3-business-day end-to-end target.

## Next step
Define and publish a formal Credit team response SLA for guarantee limit reviews, with an escalation path if the SLA is breached.
