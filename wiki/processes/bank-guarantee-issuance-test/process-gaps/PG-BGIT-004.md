## The gap
The process tracks only step-level SLAs — the credit team response window and the Legal review target — but has no end-to-end issuance cycle-time metric. The three-business-day commitment to the client cannot currently be measured or reported automatically.

## Impact
Without an end-to-end cycle-time metric the process owner cannot confirm whether the three-business-day commitment is being met in practice, cannot identify which step is the systemic bottleneck, and has no baseline to demonstrate improvement after transformation investments are made.

## Next step
Define an end-to-end cycle-time metric anchored to application receipt timestamp and SWIFT dispatch timestamp, instrument the Trade Finance System to capture both events, and add the metric to the monthly management report.
