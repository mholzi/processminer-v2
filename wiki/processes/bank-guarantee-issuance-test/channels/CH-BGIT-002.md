## Description
An assisted channel in which a Deutsche Bank Relationship Manager submits or chases a guarantee application on behalf of a corporate client.

## Usage
Used when clients prefer RM-mediated entry or when they contact the RM to chase a stalled application. RMs act as the client's escalation point when the portal provides no proactive status update.
