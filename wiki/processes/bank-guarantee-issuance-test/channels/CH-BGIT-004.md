## Description
SWIFT is the messaging network through which the executed guarantee instrument is delivered to the beneficiary's bank via MT760 message. Not a client-facing channel.

## Usage
Used at the final step of every guarantee issuance to transmit the instrument to the beneficiary's bank. The corporate applicant is not directly on SWIFT — delivery confirmation reaches them via the portal or an RM callback.
