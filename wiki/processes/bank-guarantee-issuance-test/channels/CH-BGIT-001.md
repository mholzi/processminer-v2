## Description
Deutsche Bank's primary self-service web application through which corporate clients submit guarantee applications, track status and receive completion notifications.

## Usage
Used for all guarantee applications — the Corporate Portal is the sole intake channel. Relationship Managers also enter applications via the portal on behalf of clients who prefer assisted submission.
