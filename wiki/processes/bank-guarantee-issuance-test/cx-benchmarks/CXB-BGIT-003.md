## The benchmark
Coalition Greenwich's 2024 survey of 479 large European corporates (€500M+ revenue) found approximately 40% intended to shift trade finance banking relationships, with turnaround time cited as a critical competitive factor for locking in pricing during the inflationary period.

## Relevance
For Deutsche Bank's guarantee process, this signals that delays at PS-BGIT-002 (credit stall, PP-BGIT-001) and PS-BGIT-003 (Legal review, PP-BGIT-002) are client retention risks, not just operational inefficiencies. The ~8% YoY volume growth amplifies the cost of each lost relationship.

## Evidence
Coalition Greenwich 2024 European Trade Finance Insights: 479 corporate interviews, 12 European countries, March–August 2024.
