## The benchmark
Digitized trade finance processing can reduce turnaround from a typical 36 hours to approximately 3 hours through straight-through processing, AI-assisted document extraction, and automated approval workflows — an 87% reduction against the prevailing manual baseline.

## Relevance
Deutsche Bank's 3-business-day SLA for standard guarantees is the floor in a market where fully digitized peers process in hours. Known pain points at credit check (PP-BGIT-001) and Legal review (PP-BGIT-002) push actual turnaround further above the benchmark.

## Evidence
J.P. Morgan Trends in Trade 2024: "digitized processes reduce turnaround from 36 hours to 3 hours" (87.5% reduction).
