## The move
ING integrated Komgo's Konsole API with its InsideBusiness corporate portal so that structured guarantee instructions from corporate clients flow automatically into ING's back-office trade finance system with no re-keying, eliminating media breaks across the issuance workflow.

## Relevance
ING's integration removes the intake-to-back-office media break causing PP-BGIT-003 in this process. ING was named 2025 Most Innovative Bank for Trade Finance globally — the competitive bar corporate clients now hold peers against.

## Evidence
Komgo case study confirmed reduced turnaround times and elimination of manual errors; ING awarded Most Innovative Bank for Trade Finance 2025 by GTR.
