## The move
Finastra launched Assist.AI (February 2025), a GenAI assistant embedded in its Trade Innovation platform providing real-time, context-aware guidance on guarantee wording, compliance requirements and documentation workflows — reducing reliance on specialist Legal expertise for complex guarantee text decisions.

## Relevance
Deutsche Bank routes bespoke wording questions to Legal by email, creating multi-day delays. Finastra-hosted banks resolve wording queries in-platform instantly — a direct parallel to the Legal handoff gap in PG-BGIT-002 and the delay pain point in PP-BGIT-002.
