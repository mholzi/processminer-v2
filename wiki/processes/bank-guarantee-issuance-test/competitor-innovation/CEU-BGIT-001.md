## The move
BNP Paribas completed a live pilot in November 2024 using the ICC-SWIFT open-API standard for bank guarantees, letting corporate client Vanderlande submit guarantee requests in real time via Surecomp's Rivo platform with no manual intervention and no SWIFT membership required.

## Relevance
The pilot replaces the email intake channel between corporate and bank with structured API data, eliminating the completeness-check gap and the email-only handoff documented in PG-BGIT-002 — both gaps that Deutsche Bank still carries.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas Head of Digital Trade Channels called it 'a significant industry milestone'; GTR and Surecomp confirmed completion November 2024 with no manual intervention from instruction to issuance.
