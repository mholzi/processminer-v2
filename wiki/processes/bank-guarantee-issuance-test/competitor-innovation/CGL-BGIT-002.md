## The move
Standard Chartered executed the world's first bank guarantee transaction conforming fully to ICC-SWIFT API standards in January 2026, processed via Komgo's Konsole for a global energy company — automating intake, issuance and SWIFT delivery through a single interoperable API call.

## Relevance
Standard Chartered's production deployment demonstrates the ICC-SWIFT standard is live and enterprise-ready; banks not yet on the standard face an accelerating mandate gap as corporates embed API-native guarantee workflows into their ERP and treasury systems.

## Evidence
Standard Chartered press release dated 15 January 2026 confirmed the first production transaction; progressive rollout to all markets is planned. SC co-developed the ICC-SWIFT standard.
