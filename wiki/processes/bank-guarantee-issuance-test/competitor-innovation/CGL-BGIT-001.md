## The move
HSBC's Trade Solutions platform processes standard demand guarantees end-to-end via straight-through processing, cutting typical issuance from 3–4 business days to minutes with zero manual intervention — deployed globally across 2024 and 2025.

## Relevance
HSBC's STP model collapses the credit-check, wording-review and SWIFT-delivery steps that cause PP-BGIT-001 and PP-BGIT-002 into a single automated flow, creating a turnaround benchmark corporate clients will apply to every guarantee relationship.

## Evidence
HSBC announced the first STP guarantee transactions in December 2023 — Tender Bond for KPMG Qatar and Advance Payment guarantee for Russell Finex UK — with global rollout confirmed 2024–2025.
