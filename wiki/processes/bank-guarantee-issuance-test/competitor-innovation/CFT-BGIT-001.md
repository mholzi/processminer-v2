## The move
Komgo's GTK platform gives corporate clients a single portal to submit and track guarantee requests across all their banking partners via ICC-SWIFT API standards, eliminating per-bank portal logins and manual re-keying. Standard Chartered went live via GTK in January 2026.

## Relevance
Corporates on GTK arrive with validated, API-ready instructions that any connected bank receives cleanly. Deutsche Bank's manual portal intake creates a fragmented experience — and the completeness gap in CG-BGIT-001 — compared with GTK-connected competitors.

## Evidence
Komgo named Euromoney's most innovative trade finance software provider 2025; GTK product page confirms ICC-SWIFT API standard integration and multi-bank live deployments.
