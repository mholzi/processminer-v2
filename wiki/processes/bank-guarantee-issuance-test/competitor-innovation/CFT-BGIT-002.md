## The move
Surecomp shipped an AI Email Reader in RIVO v3.2 (October 2024) that automatically parses unstructured email-based guarantee applications — extracting legal terms, conditions, expiry and transferability clauses into structured data — targeting a 30% reduction in processing time.

## Relevance
Deutsche Bank's Legal handoff for bespoke wording is email-only with no automated parsing — the identical bottleneck Surecomp targets. Banks running RIVO convert email instructions to structured transactions in hours; Deutsche Bank's manual Legal review and re-keying remain a liability.

## Evidence
Surecomp press release (November 2024) and Finextra coverage confirm AI Email Reader live in RIVO v3.2, with a 30% processing-time reduction target and explicit focus on guarantee wording workflows.
