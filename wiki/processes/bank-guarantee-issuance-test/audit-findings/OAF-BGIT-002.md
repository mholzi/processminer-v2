## Finding
The external audit conducted in 2025 flagged the absence of a documented Service Level Agreement between Trade Finance Operations and the Legal team for bespoke-wording review as a process-control weakness. Without a formal SLA, there is no enforceable standard for Legal's review turnaround, no escalation trigger, and no basis for measuring whether the control is operating effectively.

## Recommendation
Establish and document a formal Legal SLA for bespoke-wording review, with a defined turnaround target, escalation path, and monthly reporting to the process owner.
