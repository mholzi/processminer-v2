## What the client does
Contacts their Relationship Manager by phone or message to ask where their application is, after noticing days have passed with no visible portal progress.

## What the bank does
The RM calls internally to establish which step the application is stalled at and relays a manual status update to the client.

## Experience
Having to chase through the RM signals a failure of proactive communication. Clients lose confidence in the process, and RMs absorb avoidable effort — the most common friction pattern for the credit-check stall documented in PP-BGIT-001.
