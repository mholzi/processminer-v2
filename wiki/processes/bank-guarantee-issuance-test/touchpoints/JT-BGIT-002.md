## What the client does
Returns to the Corporate Portal to check application progress. No push notification is sent when an application stalls at credit review or enters Legal review.

## What the bank does
The portal shows the current process step, manually updated by the TFO. No automated SLA countdown or proactive notification is generated.

## Experience
Clients must actively poll the portal for status. When an application is parked at credit review with no explanation or timeline, the experience is opaque and anxiety-inducing — one of the process's highest-friction moments.
