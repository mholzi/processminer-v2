## What the client does
Completes the guarantee application form in the Corporate Portal — beneficiary details, amount, currency, validity period, wording type and contract reference — and submits.

## What the bank does
The Trade Finance Officer receives the application in their queue and performs a manual completeness review before advancing it.

## Experience
The portal form gives no completeness validation before submission, no real-time credit headroom check, and no confirmation of expected turnaround time. The submit action feels like sending a form into a black box.
