## The gap
Step 6 (Guarantee Generation and Delivery) has no documented control verifying that the Trade Finance Officer generates only approved guarantees. No TFS check at the generation step is explicitly documented.

## Risk
Without a documented system enforcement at generation, it is unclear whether the TFS prevents generation of an unapproved or modified guarantee instrument.

## Remediation
Confirm and document whether the Trade Finance System enforces at generation that a valid TFM approval record exists; if so, document it as a control.
