## The gap
Step 1 (Application Intake) has no system or procedural control enforcing that incomplete applications cannot advance. The Trade Finance Officer performs a manual completeness check with no blocking mechanism.

## Risk
An incomplete application may advance into downstream steps, causing rework, delays, and potential errors in guarantee wording or facility utilisation.

## Remediation
To be documented. SME has flagged this as a known gap for future remediation; no control is being drafted at this time.
