## The risk
The C2B API standard is not yet widely adopted by corporate ERP systems. If the bank invests before clients have ERP connectivity, the digital channel goes underutilised and the manual portal path must run in parallel indefinitely, increasing cost instead of reducing it.

## Likelihood & impact
Likelihood is moderate — only a handful of European banks have live C2B connections and ERP adoption lags. If the risk materialises, the API yields no STP uplift for two to three years and a dual-channel model creates ongoing governance overhead for Trade Finance Operations.

## Mitigation
Pilot with two or three high-volume corporate clients who have confirmed ERP connectivity before committing to full rollout. Define a minimum critical-mass threshold — for example, ten connected clients — before decommissioning the portal intake path.
