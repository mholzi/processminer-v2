## Description
The beneficiary or beneficiary's country is flagged against the sanctions list during the Compliance Analyst's screening at Step 4.

## Handling
The application is suspended pending Compliance investigation. Issuance cannot proceed until the investigation concludes.

## Impact
Suspends the application pending investigation, preventing guarantee issuance until Compliance concludes its review.
