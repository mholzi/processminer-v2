## Description
The client's available guarantee facility limit is insufficient to cover the requested guarantee amount.

## Handling
The application is parked and routed to the Credit team. The Credit team may grant a temporary one-off uplift, initiate a permanent facility increase (a separate process), or decline. On decline, the application is formally rejected and the client is notified through the Corporate Portal.

## Impact
This is the most common reason for delay in the process.
