## Responsibility
Handles guarantee facility limit decisions when a client's available limit is insufficient to cover a new application: may grant a temporary one-off uplift, recommend a permanent facility increase (a separate process), or decline.

## In this process
Engaged when the credit and facility check at Step 2 reveals insufficient available limit. The Credit team reviews the parked application and either grants a temporary uplift, initiates a permanent facility increase, or declines. On decline, the application is formally rejected and the client is notified through the Corporate Portal.
