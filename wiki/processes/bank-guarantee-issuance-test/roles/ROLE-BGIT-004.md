## Responsibility
Responsible for sanctions and compliance screening of beneficiaries before guarantee issuance.

## In this process
Screens the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country against the sanctions list at Step 4, and attaches the screening result to the application. A screening hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.
