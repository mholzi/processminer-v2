## Responsibility
Provides additional sign-off authority for bank guarantee issuances above EUR 2 million, and for all partially-secured (collateral-backed) guarantees regardless of amount.

## In this process
Required to approve issuance at Step 5 for guarantees above EUR 2 million, or for any partially-secured (collateral-backed) guarantee regardless of amount, in addition to the Trade Finance Manager's approval.
