## Responsibility
Responsible for application intake, completeness checking, credit and facility verification, guarantee generation, and delivery to the beneficiary.

## In this process
Performs application intake (Step 1), credit and facility check (Step 2), and guarantee generation and delivery (Step 6). At Step 3, the TFO forwards bespoke wording to the Legal team by email; for standard-wording applications, Step 3 requires no TFO action.
