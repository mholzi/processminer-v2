## The gap
The credit limit pre-check advisory reduces Credit team referrals but does not eliminate them. When a referral still occurs, there is no formal Credit team response SLA, leaving the stall pattern (PG-BGIT-001) partly intact in the target state.

## Resolution
Establish a formal Credit team response SLA for guarantee limit reviews — for example, one business day — monitored via the TFS credit referral dashboard. The SLA should be agreed between Trade Finance and Credit and documented in a service-level agreement. The TFS pre-check display should surface pending referrals against the SLA to the TFO.

## Status
Open — requires agreement with Credit team leadership. PG-BGIT-001 is the underlying As-Is gap.
