## The gap
The in-TFS wording workflow requires a committed Legal review SLA, which does not exist today. Without an agreed SLA, the workflow timer cannot be configured and the escalation path has no measurable baseline.

## Resolution
Negotiate and agree a formal Legal review SLA for bespoke guarantee wording — for example, one business day for standard bespoke and two for complex cases — and configure the TFS workflow timer accordingly. The SLA must be documented in the Legal service agreement, with escalation path defined to Head of Legal.

## Status
Open — requires negotiation with Legal leadership before TD-BGIT-003 can be operationalised.
