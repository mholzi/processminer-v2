## What it requires
Bank guarantees classified as credit substitutes carry a 100% credit conversion factor under CRR Annex I; the resulting off-balance-sheet exposure drives a risk-weighted asset calculation and capital allocation under Article 111.

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank issues guarantees creating contingent liabilities classified as off-balance-sheet items under CRR Part Three, Title II; each issuance increases the bank's risk-weighted assets and requires corresponding capital.

## How it is met
CP-BGIT-003 (facility limit check) ensures issuance stays within the pre-approved credit facility; capital adequacy against issued guarantees is managed through the bank's CRR regulatory reporting framework.
