## What it requires
Institutions must apply documented creditworthiness assessment, governance policies, and appropriate sign-off authorities to credit facilities; these standards extend to guarantee facilities as contingent credit exposures.

## Why it applies
Guarantee facilities extended to corporate clients are credit exposures under EBA/GL/2020/06; the credit check at Step 2 and issuance approval at Step 5 must meet its governance and assessment standards.

## How it is met
PS-BGIT-002 validates facility adequacy; CP-BGIT-001 and CP-BGIT-003 enforce documented credit policy at issuance. Ongoing facility monitoring alignment with EBA/GL/2020/06 is to be confirmed with the Credit team.
