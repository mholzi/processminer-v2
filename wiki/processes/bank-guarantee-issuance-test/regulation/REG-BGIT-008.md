## What it requires
Institutions must apply enhanced due diligence and monitor for trade-based money laundering indicators; beneficiary screening must consider trade finance-specific ML/TF risk factors beyond standard AML CDD.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantee issuance is a trade finance product; EBA/GL/2021/02 identifies trade finance as a higher-risk sector requiring risk factor assessment beyond the general AMLD obligations in REG-BGIT-001.

## How it is met
CP-BGIT-002 (sanctions screening) addresses beneficiary-level AML/TF risk at Step 4. Coverage of trade-based ML indicators (complex routing, mismatched beneficiary) is to be confirmed with Compliance.
