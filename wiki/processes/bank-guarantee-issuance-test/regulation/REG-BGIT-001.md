## What it requires
The process is subject to anti-money-laundering and sanctions obligations, including EU sanctions regulations and AML directives.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantees are issued to third-party beneficiaries who may be subject to sanctions; the process therefore brings the bank within the scope of EU sanctions regulations and AML directives.

## How it is met
The Compliance Analyst screens every beneficiary and beneficiary country using the Sanctions Screening Tool at Step 4; the screening result is attached to the application.
