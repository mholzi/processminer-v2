## What it requires
Guarantee issuance (Garantiegeschäft) is a regulated credit business activity under KWG §1; only BaFin-licensed credit institutions may issue bank guarantees commercially on behalf of clients.

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank's corporate guarantee issuance constitutes Garantiegeschäft under §1 KWG; operating this process is conditional on, and must remain within, a valid German banking licence.

## How it is met
Deutsche Bank holds a full KWG banking licence; guarantee issuance operates within the licensed credit business scope. CP-BGIT-001 enforces delegated authority limits at each individual issuance decision.
