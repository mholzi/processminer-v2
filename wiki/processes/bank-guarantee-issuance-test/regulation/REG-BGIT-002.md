## What it requires
Bank guarantees issued on demand terms must follow the ICC Uniform Rules for Demand Guarantees (URDG 758).

## Why it applies
Where a guarantee is issued on demand terms, URDG 758 applies.

## How it is met
Standard templates incorporate URDG 758 by reference (clauses 1, 4, and 12); the Trade Finance System enforces template selection for standard wording. For bespoke wording, Legal confirms URDG 758 conformity as part of its sign-off at Step 3 (CP-BGIT-004).
