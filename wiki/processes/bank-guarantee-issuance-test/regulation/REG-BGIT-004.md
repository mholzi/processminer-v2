## What it requires
MaRisk BT 1 mandates segregation of duties between front-office (market) and independent risk/approval functions for credit business, and a four-eyes principle (Vier-Augen-Prinzip) for all credit decisions including guarantee issuance.

## Why it applies
Guarantee issuance (Garantiegeschäft) is in-scope credit business under MaRisk BT 1; the four-eyes rule and function separation requirements apply to each issuance decision at Deutsche Bank.

## How it is met
CP-BGIT-001 (four-eyes issuance approval) enforces TFM sign-off on every guarantee, with Head of Trade Finance co-signing above EUR 5 million, satisfying the BT 1.1 function separation requirement.
