## What it requires
DORA requires credit institutions to maintain an ICT risk management framework, report major ICT incidents, and ensure resilience of critical systems including critical third-party ICT providers such as SWIFT.

## Why it applies
The guarantee process depends on the Trade Finance System (SYS-BGIT-002) and SWIFT (SYS-BGIT-004); both are in-scope ICT systems, and SWIFT qualifies as a critical third-party ICT provider under DORA.

## How it is met
DORA compliance for SYS-BGIT-002 and SYS-BGIT-004 is managed at infrastructure and vendor-management level; process-level resilience requirements are to be confirmed with IT/Operations.
