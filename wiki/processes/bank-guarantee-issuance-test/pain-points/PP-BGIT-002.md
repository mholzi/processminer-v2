## Description
Bespoke wording requirements add unpredictable delay to the issuance process because Legal review has no committed turnaround.

## Impact
The absence of a committed Legal review turnaround makes end-to-end cycle time unpredictable for bespoke-wording guarantees.

## Root cause
Legal review of bespoke wording has no committed turnaround time, creating an open-ended dependency in the process.
