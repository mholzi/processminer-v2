## Description
When the Trade Finance Officer returns an incomplete application through the Corporate Portal with a missing-info comment, there is no formal SLA governing how quickly the client must respond or when the TFO escalates.

## Impact
Incomplete applications can stall indefinitely at intake with no escalation trigger, causing unpredictable delays before the issuance workflow even begins.

## Root cause
No formal follow-up SLA has been defined for incomplete application returns; the process relies on the client responding without a defined deadline or escalation path.
