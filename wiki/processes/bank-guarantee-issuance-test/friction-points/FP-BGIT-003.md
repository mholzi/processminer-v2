## Description
When a bespoke-wording application is with Legal, the client sees only a generic in-progress status in the portal. There is no indication the delay is Legal-driven, no SLA and no expected return date.

## Root cause
The Legal review is conducted outside TFS by email (PG-BGIT-002), leaving no real-time status data to surface in the portal.

## Client impact
The client cannot differentiate between a normal processing delay and a Legal-driven hold. For time-sensitive bespoke guarantees, the lack of visibility creates commercial risk and reputational concern about the bank's responsiveness.
