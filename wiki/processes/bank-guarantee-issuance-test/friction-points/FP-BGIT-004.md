## Description
The handoff from the TFO to Legal for bespoke wording review happens entirely by email with no client-facing status update. From the client's perspective, the application simply stalls without explanation.

## Root cause
The bespoke wording handoff has no system workflow (PG-BGIT-002); because it is email-based, no portal status transition is generated and the client remains uninformed.

## Client impact
Clients with bespoke-wording applications experience the process as a black box from Step 3 onward. The invisible handoff distinguishes bespoke from standard applications in the worst possible way — adding opacity where the commercial stakes are highest.
