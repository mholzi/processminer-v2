## Description
After submitting a guarantee application, the client sees no SLA countdown or progress indicator in the portal. The 3-business-day end-to-end target is not communicated; clients cannot tell whether their application is on track or at risk.

## Root cause
No SLA timer or progress display has been built into the portal; the process depends on the TFO manually updating a status field.

## Client impact
Without a visible SLA countdown, clients cannot plan against their underlying commercial commitment. The absence turns routine applications into anxious waits and drives avoidable RM calls as the deadline approaches.
