## The gap
It is not defined whether a partial release against a single request is permitted (PG-FR-003) — the question bites when the limit check finds a shortfall at validation and when Treasury funding is only partly available at the liquidity step.

## Resolution
No target-state element addresses this. It needs a policy decision by the Head of Payment Operations on whether partial releases are allowed and how a limit shortfall and partial funding are each handled; only then can it be designed into the process.

## Status
Open — not addressed in the current target design. Awaiting a partial-release policy decision before it can be designed.
