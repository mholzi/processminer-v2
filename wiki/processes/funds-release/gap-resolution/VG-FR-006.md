## The gap
The two detective controls — the daily held-vs-released reconciliation and the monthly audit-log completeness review — have no documented finding-resolution workflow: no investigation timeline and no formal escalation threshold for a break or an incomplete log (CG-FR-003).

## Resolution
No target-state element addresses this. Closing it needs a documented finding-resolution workflow for both controls — an investigation timeline, criteria separating timing differences from genuine breaks, and an escalation threshold to the Operations Team Lead — a Control & Compliance specialist task.

## Status
Open — not addressed in the current target design. Falls to the Control & Compliance specialist to design the finding-resolution workflow.
