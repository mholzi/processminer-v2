## The gap
STP-eligible clean releases receive a system-applied first- and second-line approval with no human dual-control and no compensating control (CG-FR-001), and the STP path itself is not represented in the documented process spine (PG-FR-005) — the fastest-growing volume is the least controlled.

## Resolution
TS-FR-002 makes the STP path an explicit, documented spine branch and gives it a real-time automated-control layer — rules validation, post-release monitored sampling and an anomaly-hold. STP releases gain a genuine, continuously-monitored compensating control and an auditable record.

## Status
Designed in the target state (TS-FR-002); not yet built. Highest-severity work-stream, and the compensating-control design has no peer precedent (IR-FR-001).
