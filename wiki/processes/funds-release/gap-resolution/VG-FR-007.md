## The gap
The second-line approver bench is just two nominated approvers with no defined backup (PG-FR-002). TS-FR-004's agentic pre-screening eases each review but does not widen the bench — a structurally thin bench can still stall a release when approvers are unavailable.

## Resolution
Partly eased in the target state — TS-FR-004 makes each 4-eyes review faster and more focused — but the structural fix is an open decision: whether to name the Operations Team Lead as approver of last resort or a dedicated backup, and to expand the nominated bench beyond two.

## Status
Partly addressed by TS-FR-004; the bench-widening decision remains open with the Head of Payment Operations.
