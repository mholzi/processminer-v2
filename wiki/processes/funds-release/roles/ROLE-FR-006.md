## Responsibility
The Operations Team Lead supervises the operations release team and resolves second-line approval exceptions — taking parked items when an approver is unavailable or the 4-eyes principle would be breached.

## In this process
It owns the handling of EX-FR-004: a parked item is escalated to it, and it reassigns the second-line approval to another eligible Operations Approver. Where both nominated approvers are absent the reassignment target is undefined (see PG-FR-002).
