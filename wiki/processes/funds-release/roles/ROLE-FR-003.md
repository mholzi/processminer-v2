## Responsibility
The Operations Approver provides the independent second-line authorisation of a non-STP or exception release under the 4-eyes principle. For STP items the second-line approval is system-applied and the Operations Approver does not act.

## In this process
On a non-STP or exception item it performs a substantive 4-eyes review, re-checking the validation result, screening disposition, Treasury funding confirmation and beneficiary details, and must not be the first-line analyst. It then authorises the release or routes a not-granted item back by reason to PS-FR-004, PS-FR-003 or PS-FR-005.
