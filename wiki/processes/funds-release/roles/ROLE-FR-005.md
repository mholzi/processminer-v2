## Responsibility
Treasury is accountable for confirming that funding is available for releases at or above the fixed EUR 5,000,000 threshold, with non-EUR amounts FX-converted at the release-day rate.

## In this process
For amounts at or above the threshold it confirms funding availability for the value date — a point-in-time check, not an earmark (per PP-FR-001). The confirmation must land before the 14:00 CET cut-off to hold same-day value. Where funding is unavailable the release is deferred and rescheduled.
