## Responsibility
Confirms that funding is available for the value date on releases at or above the Treasury threshold.

## In this process
Accountable and responsible for confirming funding availability on releases at or above the EUR 5,000,000 threshold; where funding is unavailable the release is deferred and rescheduled.
