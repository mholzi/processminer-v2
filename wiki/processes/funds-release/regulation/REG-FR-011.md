## What it requires
Personal data must be processed lawfully, only for specified purposes, kept no longer than necessary, and kept secure, with data subjects retaining defined rights over their data.

## Why it applies
Each release captures and screens the personal data of named beneficiaries and individual contacts, and retains it in the workflow audit trail — processing that GDPR governs.

## How it is met
Beneficiary data captured at PS-FR-001 serves the defined release purpose; lawful retention, security and access are governed by the systems and data-protection framework — to be confirmed with the SME.
