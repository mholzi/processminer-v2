## What it requires
Obliged entities, including credit institutions, must apply customer due diligence and screen transactions and counterparties for money-laundering and terrorist-financing risk, detecting and reporting suspicious activity under a single, directly applicable EU rulebook.

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank is an obliged entity; releasing funds against a corporate facility is a transaction the AMLR requires it to monitor and screen for financial-crime risk under harmonised EU rules that replace divergent national regimes.

## How it is met
Automated AML and sanctions screening (CP-FR-002) at PS-FR-003 screens each release; aligning the existing GwG-based controls to the AMLR before its July 2027 application is to be confirmed with the SME.
