## What it requires
A payment service provider must offer a Verification of Payee service that matches the payee's name against the IBAN before a euro credit transfer is authorised, to mitigate misdirection and fraud.

## Why it applies
Each release executes a euro credit transfer to a named beneficiary on an IBAN; the Regulation, in force since October 2025, brings such transfers within the Verification of Payee duty.

## How it is met
No process step verifies the beneficiary name against the IBAN today; beneficiary details are captured at PS-FR-001 but not payee-matched — a compliance gap to confirm with the SME.
