## What it requires
Resident credit institutions must report granular, loan-by-loan credit and credit-risk data on instruments granted to legal entities, including the drawn and undrawn amounts and changes to the outstanding balance.

## Why it applies
Each release is a drawdown that moves committed funds from the undrawn limit into the outstanding balance of a corporate credit facility — a change in instrument data AnaCredit requires the bank to report.

## How it is met
No process control maps directly; AnaCredit reporting is fed from the facility and core banking systems downstream of the release — to be confirmed with the SME.
