## What it requires
Foreign trade and payments transactions must comply with the embargoes and restrictive measures the Ordinance gives domestic effect, and cross-border payments above the threshold must be reported to the Deutsche Bundesbank.

## Why it applies
A funds release executes a payment, often cross-border, for a German credit institution; the AWG and AWV are the national framework that enforces sanctions and imposes payment-reporting duties on such transactions.

## How it is met
Sanctions screening (CP-FR-002) at PS-FR-003 detects embargo breaches; the Bundesbank cross-border payment report is a back-office obligation — to be confirmed with the SME.
