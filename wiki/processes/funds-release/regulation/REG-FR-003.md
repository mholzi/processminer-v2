## What it requires
That initiating and authorising a payment are performed by different people, and that a documented internal control framework governs the activity.

## Why it applies
Funds release authorises and executes outgoing payments — an activity BaFin's MaRisk payment-processing requirements explicitly cover.

## How it is met
Through the four-eyes control CP-FR-003 at second-line approval and the daily reconciliation control CP-FR-005.
