## What it requires
Funds and economic resources must not be made available, directly or indirectly, to or for the benefit of any person or entity designated under EU restrictive measures, and such assets must be frozen.

## Why it applies
A release pays funds to a beneficiary, potentially via a beneficiary bank or correspondent, any of which could be a designated party — directly engaging the prohibition on making funds available.

## How it is met
Sanctions screening (CP-FR-002) at PS-FR-003 checks the beneficiary and beneficiary bank against EU lists; a confirmed match is frozen and the release blocked at EX-FR-002.
