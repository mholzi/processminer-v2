## What it requires
A euro credit transfer must reach the beneficiary's bank within the regulated execution time, and the value date applied must reflect when the funds actually move.

## Why it applies
A funds release is a euro credit transfer to a beneficiary; the value date and same-day cut-off it carries fall under the EU payment-execution rules.

## How it is met
The 14:00 CET cut-off at PS-FR-005 and PS-FR-007 governs same-day value; no dedicated control tests execution-time compliance — to be confirmed with the SME.
