## What it requires
That the bank retains a complete, reconstructable record of who approved and who executed each transaction.

## Why it applies
A released payment is a booked transaction that must be evidenced for internal audit and regulatory inspection.

## How it is met
Through CP-FR-006, the monthly audit-log completeness review covering all actors and timestamps on each release.
