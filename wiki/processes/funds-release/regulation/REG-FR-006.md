## What it requires
A credit institution must measure and monitor its credit exposures and not extend credit beyond approved limits, including the committed undrawn portion of facilities that it must capitalise and control.

## Why it applies
Each release is a drawdown against a corporate credit facility; releasing more than the available undrawn limit would create an unauthorised, uncapitalised credit exposure for the bank.

## How it is met
The automated limit check (CP-FR-001) at PS-FR-002 verifies the amount against the available undrawn limit; it is point-in-time and not re-run at execution — see CG-FR-002.
