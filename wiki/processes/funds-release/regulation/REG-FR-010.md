## What it requires
Accounting vouchers, business letters and the records underlying each booking must be kept complete, immutable and machine-readable for the statutory retention period and made available to tax and audit authorities.

## Why it applies
Each release posts an accounting movement and produces a workflow item with an audit trail; these are business and accounting records a German credit institution must retain and keep verifiable.

## How it is met
The complete audit trail produced at PS-FR-008 and the audit-log completeness review (CP-FR-006) support the record; retention-period archiving is a systems obligation — to be confirmed with the SME.
