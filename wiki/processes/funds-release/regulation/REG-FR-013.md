## What it requires
A credit institution must hold a liquidity buffer sufficient to cover its net liquidity outflows over a 30-day stress period, maintaining a liquidity coverage ratio of at least 100%.

## Why it applies
A funds release is a liquidity outflow; large releases in particular draw on the bank's funding and must be manageable within its liquidity buffer and coverage ratio.

## How it is met
Treasury's funding-availability confirmation for releases at or above EUR 5m (CP-FR-004) at PS-FR-005 links each large release to liquidity management; firm-wide LCR monitoring sits with Treasury.
