## The trend
Banks are replacing siloed payment systems with composable, cloud-based payment hubs that orchestrate queuing, validation, approval and posting on one platform, integrate with core and treasury systems, and automate straight-through processing.

## Relevance
Funds Release runs across four loosely-joined systems with a manual Treasury re-key and an undocumented STP path; a payment-hub architecture would unify orchestration, close integration gaps and make the automated path explicit.

## Evidence
Santander's PagoNxt-built Payments Hub runs millions of transactions daily as cloud SaaS; J.P. Morgan and Capgemini cite composable cloud payment hubs as the route to automated STP and lower operating cost.
