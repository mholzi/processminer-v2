## The trend
As payments move to instant, irrevocable rails, the window to catch errors and fraud before settlement collapses. Banks are responding by pushing automated controls — duplicate detection, pre-submission validation and real-time matching — to the point of intake rather than relying on after-the-fact reconciliation.

## Relevance
Funds Release has no duplicate-request check at receipt, so a repeated drawdown can be released twice and is caught only by the next day's reconciliation. On faster rails that detective control would settle the error before it is even found.

## Evidence
The 2025 AFP Payments Fraud and Control Survey reports 79 percent of organisations faced attempted or actual payments fraud; the EBA has assessed fraud risk on instant transfers as up to ten times that of standard transfers.
