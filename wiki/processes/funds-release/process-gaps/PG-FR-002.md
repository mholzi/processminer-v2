## The gap
The second-line approver bench is just two nominated Operations Approvers, and it is unclear who acts as backup when both are absent. At ~120 releases a day, with the 4-eyes rule further shrinking the eligible pool per item, a two-person bench is structurally fragile.

## Impact
With no defined backup and only two nominated approvers, a release can stall at the 4-eyes step whenever both are unavailable — a frequent risk on a thin bench.

## Next step
Decide whether the Operations Team Lead is the second-line approver of last resort, or name a separate dedicated backup — and expand the nominated approver bench beyond two.
