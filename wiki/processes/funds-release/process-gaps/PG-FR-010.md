## The gap
M-FR-002 sets only a 'same business day' target for manual releases, with no intra-day turnaround target. STP releases have a 2-hour target (M-FR-001); manual releases have no equivalent.

## Impact
A manual release can take all day and still meet target, so there is no measurable expectation of intra-day speed and no early signal of a building backlog.

## Next step
Define an intra-day turnaround target for manual releases, proportionate to the 2-hour STP target.
