## The benchmark
Net Promoter Score is the standard yardstick for banking client loyalty, with published 2025 financial-services benchmarks letting a bank position its score against peers. Corporate transaction-banking relationships are increasingly judged on this experience measure.

## Relevance
Funds Release captures no client-experience measure — there is no effort score or NPS on the release journey — so the process cannot tell whether its close-only confirmation meets, or trails, the benchmark its competitors actively track.

## Evidence
CustomerGauge publishes 2025 financial-services NPS benchmarks covering 27 banks, the reference set institutions use to compare client-loyalty performance against peers.
