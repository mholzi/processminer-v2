## The benchmark
Real-time payment rails have become the baseline corporate expectation: more than 70 countries now run real-time payment systems, and faster payments are treated as an expectation rather than a premium, with payment speed a stated priority for mid-sized businesses.

## Relevance
Funds Release relies on a same-day model with a 14:00 CET cut-off for same-day value. As instant settlement becomes the norm, that cut-off-bound timing increasingly reads as slow to corporate clients accustomed to real-time rails.

## Evidence
J.P. Morgan's 2025 cross-border payments trends report notes that faster payments are now an expectation and that more than 70 countries have adopted real-time payment systems.
