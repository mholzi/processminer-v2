## The move
Santander runs a cloud-native SaaS payments hub built by its PagoNxt unit that processes millions of transactions daily, and has piloted agentic AI in payments — completing with Mastercard Europe's first live end-to-end payment executed by an AI agent.

## Relevance
Santander pairs unified payment orchestration with autonomous execution; Funds Release, run across four loosely-joined systems with manual re-keys, faces a peer compressing exactly the orchestration and execution steps it still performs by hand.
