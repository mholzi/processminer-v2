## The move
Deutsche Bank has built out real-time instant payment processing for corporate and institutional clients, offering round-the-clock euro instant credit transfers and integrating Verification of Payee into its corporate cash-management proposition ahead of the EU regulatory deadlines.

## Relevance
Funds Release runs to a two-hour STP target and a 14:00 CET cut-off for same-day value. A close European peer offering corporates round-the-clock instant release sets a service expectation that this process's batch-and-approve model does not yet meet.

## Evidence
Deutsche Bank reported SEPA Instant payment volumes up 27 percent year on year in January 2025, with corporate real-time payment volumes 13 percent higher.
