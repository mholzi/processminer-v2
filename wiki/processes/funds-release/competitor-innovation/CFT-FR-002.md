## The move
Quantifind provides AI-powered sanctions and risk screening that adds context from unstructured data to score potential hits, cutting false positives sharply while preserving detection of genuine matches.

## Relevance
PS-FR-003 sends every potential hit to Compliance for manual adjudication; a screening engine that resolves most false positives automatically would shrink the adjudication queue and accelerate clean releases.

## Evidence
Quantifind's AI-powered sanctions screening reported a 90%+ false-positive reduction while maintaining genuine-match detection during the Russia–Ukraine sanctions surge.
