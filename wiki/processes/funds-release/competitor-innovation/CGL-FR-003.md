## The move
HSBC has worked since 2021 with fintech Silent Eight to automate labour-intensive compliance decisions — sanctions screening and transaction-alert adjudication — and is scaling AI across workflows toward automating up to 90% of certain data and analytics tasks.

## Relevance
PS-FR-003 routes every potential sanctions hit to Compliance for manual adjudication; a global peer automating that exact adjudication step exposes a competitive gap in screening throughput and cost for Funds Release.

## Evidence
Silent Eight announced an expanded HSBC partnership (February 2024) implementing Automated Alert Closure for Transactions, automating investigation and resolution of screening alerts in real time.
