## The risk
The innovation ideas are interdependent: the STP control layer (II-FR-008) depends on the documented STP path (II-FR-003), the automated Treasury feed (II-FR-002) depends on a SYS-FR-004 API, and the metrics instrumentation (II-FR-006) depends on agreed definitions. One slipped dependency can stall the wider programme.

## Likelihood & impact
Likelihood medium — multi-system change programmes routinely slip on a single dependency. Impact medium: a stalled prerequisite delays dependent ideas, and a half-delivered transformation can leave the process less coherent than the As-Is.

## Mitigation
Sequence the roadmap so prerequisites land first — documentation before control, definitions before instrumentation; track dependencies explicitly; and design each idea to deliver standalone value so a slip degrades rather than blocks the programme.
