## The idea
Introduce an agentic-AI triage layer that classifies and logs every off-happy-path item — including the suspended or under-review facility bounce that today leaves no record — routing each to the right owner as a tracked exception.

## Expected benefit
Every deviation becomes a measurable, auditable exception, so suspended-facility releases surface in exception metrics, and routine triage moves off analysts to focus them on judgement-heavy cases.

## Feasibility
Higher effort and risk — it needs an agentic platform, a clean exception taxonomy and human-in-the-loop guardrails; depends first on deciding whether the suspended-facility bounce should raise a logged exception at all.
