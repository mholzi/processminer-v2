## The idea
Give the second-line approver an agentic-AI assistant that pre-assembles each item — validation result, screening disposition, funding confirmation and beneficiary checks — and flags anomalies, so the scarce 4-eyes review is faster and more focused.

## Expected benefit
Each substantive 4-eyes review takes less time, so the thin two-person approver bench can clear the daily volume with less strain, reducing the risk of a release stalling when approvers are unavailable.

## Feasibility
Higher complexity — the assistant must not itself perform the approval or it breaches 4-eyes; depends on the PG-FR-002 decision to widen the bench and on strict human-in-the-loop boundaries.
