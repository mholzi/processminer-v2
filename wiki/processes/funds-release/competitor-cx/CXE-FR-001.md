## The journey
BNP Paribas runs corporate financing and daily banking through Centric, a single digital portal that gives corporate clients self-service access to cash management, trade finance and FX, and digitalises bank interactions so clients transact and request services online rather than only through a relationship manager.

## Relevance
Funds Release has no client-facing portal at all — the front office submits a drawdown through an internal payments workflow tool and is only an informed party afterwards. A European peer offering self-service requests and visibility sets a digital-experience bar this process does not meet.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas CIB describes Centric as a one-stop digital portal whose self-servicing capabilities speed up client interactions across cash management, trade finance and FX.
