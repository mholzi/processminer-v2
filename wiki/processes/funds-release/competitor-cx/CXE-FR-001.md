## The journey
A corporate client banks through Centric, BNP Paribas's single modular e-banking platform that spans more than 45 countries. One sign-on covers cash management, trade finance and post-trade, and clients can confirm and net transactions in straight-through mode from the same portal.

## Relevance
This sets the expectation that a drawdown and its release ride one self-service portal where the client sees and acts on the item directly. In Funds Release the client's only window is a front-office confirmation at close (PS-FR-008), opening a visibility gap.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas CIB describes Centric as its clients' primary interface with the bank, built around single sign-on and modularity, with most services accessible in more than 45 countries.
