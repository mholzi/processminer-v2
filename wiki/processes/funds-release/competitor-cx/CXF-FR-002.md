## The journey
OakNorth Bank provides debt finance to fast-growth companies on a digital platform: once a facility is approved the client accesses funds immediately and monitors and manages the facility in real time, with instant credit analysis and live portfolio insight built in.

## Relevance
Real-time facility monitoring is precisely the visibility Funds Release lacks. A Funds Release client gets one confirmation at close and no live view of held versus released balances; OakNorth makes that view continuous and self-service for the client.

## Evidence
OakNorth's corporate materials and its CB Insights profile state that approved clients access funds immediately through its digital platform and monitor and manage their facility in real time.
