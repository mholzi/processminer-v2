## The gap
The two detective controls — the daily held-vs-released reconciliation (CP-FR-005) and the monthly audit-log completeness review (CP-FR-006) — have no documented finding-resolution workflow: no defined investigation timeline and no formal escalation threshold for a break or an incomplete log.

## Risk
A genuine discrepancy or an incomplete audit log can be misjudged or left unresolved without a defined timeline, weakening the detective controls meant to catch errors, unauthorised movements and audit-trail gaps.

## Remediation
Document a finding-resolution workflow for both controls — an investigation timeline, the criteria separating timing differences from genuine breaks, and a formal escalation threshold to the Operations Team Lead.
