## The gap
The four-eyes control depends on system role configuration, but no scheduled review confirms the first- and second-line approver populations remain genuinely disjoint.

## Risk
Role drift letting one person hold both approval rights, defeating the four-eyes control without anyone noticing.

## Remediation
Introduce a quarterly access review that re-tests approver role assignments and records the result as control evidence.
