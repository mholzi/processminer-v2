## Purpose
Platform used by Treasury to manage liquidity and funding; it includes a funding reservation/earmark capability.

## Role in this process
Treasury uses it to confirm funding availability for value dates on releases above the threshold. Its earmark capability exists but is unused, so confirmations are point-in-time only (CG-FR-004). Treasury manually re-keys the confirmation as a flag in SYS-FR-001 (PG-FR-008).
