## Purpose
System of record for corporate credit facilities; it supports an approval-time hold/reservation field on the available limit.

## Role in this process
Provides facility status and the available undrawn limit for validation (CP-FR-001). Its approval-time hold field exists but is unused, so the limit moves only on drawdown/posting (CG-FR-002). It is the system of record for held-fund balances in the CP-FR-005 reconciliation.
