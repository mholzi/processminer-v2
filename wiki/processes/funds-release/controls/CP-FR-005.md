## What it checks
That the balances of held accounts agree with the funds recorded as released.

## Control activity
A detective, manual control under which held versus released balances are reconciled and any discrepancy is investigated.

## Risk addressed
A release error or balance mismatch going undetected after funds have moved.

## Timing
Performed manually on a daily basis.
