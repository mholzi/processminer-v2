## What it checks
That the release is authorised by someone other than the person who performed first-line approval.

## Control activity
A preventive, manual control under which a second Ops Approver independently reviews and authorises the release and must not be the first-line approver.

## Risk addressed
A single person both preparing and releasing funds with no independent check.

## Timing
Performed manually on every release item at second-line approval.
