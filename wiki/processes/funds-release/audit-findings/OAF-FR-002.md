## Finding
Where a release was returned from second-line to first-line approval (the PS-FR-006 loopback), the audit sample showed the reason for rejection was recorded in only 12 of 21 cases. Without it, the bank cannot show why an approval was withheld and re-sought.

## Recommendation
Require a free-text rejection reason on every loopback and surface it in the release audit log.
