## Finding
Internal Audit's Q3 2025 review of funds release found that for 9 of 40 sampled releases above the EUR 5M threshold, Treasury funding confirmation could not be evidenced in the case file. The confirmation is given verbally or by email and is not consistently attached to the release record.

## Recommendation
Make capture of a Treasury funding-confirmation reference a mandatory, system-enforced field before a threshold release can be executed.
