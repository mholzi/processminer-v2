## Finding
Internal Audit observed that when Compliance adjudicates a sanctions or AML screening hit under CP-FR-002, the decision outcome is recorded but the decision timestamp is not always captured, weakening the audit trail for cleared hits.

## Recommendation
Capture an automatic timestamp and the adjudicator's identity on every screening-hit decision.
