## Finding
The Q4 2024 audit found that daily held-versus-released reconciliation breaks under CP-FR-005 were not always investigated within the two-day SLA — five breaks in the sample period remained open beyond five days. Remediation completed in Q1 2025 with a tracked break log.

## Recommendation
Track every reconciliation break in a dated log with an owner and an SLA timer, and report ageing breaks to the process owner weekly.
