## Definition
The time to release a clean straight-through item, from receipt of the request to execution. Straight-through means no human touch, not real-time: STP items run in scheduled intraday batch cycles.

## Current reading
No current performance is measured. The target is same business day, within two hours of receipt — the upper bound of the roughly hourly STP batch cycle, not a real-time latency.

## Why it matters
A tight straight-through turnaround keeps clean releases fast and frees analyst capacity for items that need manual handling.
