## Definition
SLA-clock time to resolve a release item that has hit an exception, from identification — measured with the EX-FR-001/002/003 SLA pauses applied, not elapsed wall-clock time.

## Current reading
No current performance is measured. The 1-business-day target applies only to EX-FR-001, EX-FR-004 and EX-FR-005; EX-FR-002 (terminal) and EX-FR-003 (deferred to a later value date) are out of scope for this metric.

## Why it matters
Prompt exception resolution limits how long funds remain held and keeps delayed items from breaching service levels.
