## Definition
The bank's internal operations cut-off after which a release can no longer be given same-day value. It is set ahead of the external settlement-rail deadlines to leave processing margin.

## Current reading
The cut-off is 14:00 CET — 14:00 CEST under daylight saving — and is the EUR / default cut-off. Other currencies carry their own rail-specific cut-offs, not yet documented (see PG-FR-011).

## Why it matters
The cut-off determines whether a release reaches the beneficiary with same-day value or moves to the next value date; PS-FR-005 and PS-FR-007 both enforce against it.
