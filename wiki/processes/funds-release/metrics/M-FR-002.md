## Definition
The time to complete a manual release that runs without an exception, receipt to execution. Turnaround only — released the same business day — not same-day value, set by the 14:00 CET cut-off (M-FR-004).

## Current reading
No current performance is measured. The target is same business day; there is no intra-day turnaround target (see PG-FR-010), unlike the 2-hour STP target.

## Why it matters
Same-day manual release keeps non-straight-through items within client expectations and prevents a backlog of held funds.
