## What happens
A Trade Finance Manager reviews the assembled application package and approves issuance. Guarantees above EUR 5 million additionally require sign-off by the Head of Trade Finance before proceeding. The approval is recorded in the Trade Finance System.

## Inputs
- Complete application package (facility check, wording approval, screening result)
- Guarantee amount (to determine whether Head of Trade Finance sign-off is required)

## Outputs
- Recorded issuance approval in the Trade Finance System
- For guarantees above EUR 5 million: co-approval by Head of Trade Finance
- Application cleared for guarantee generation and delivery

## Why it matters
The four-eyes approval control ensures no guarantee is issued without a Trade Finance Manager's approval, reducing operational risk. The escalation threshold for the Head of Trade Finance provides additional oversight on material exposures.
