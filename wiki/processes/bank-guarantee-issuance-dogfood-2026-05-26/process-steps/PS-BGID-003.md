## What happens
Applications with standard wording proceed directly to the next step without further review. Applications requesting bespoke wording are sent to the Legal team for review and sign-off before issuance can proceed. Legal review has no committed turnaround, making bespoke-wording cases a source of unpredictable delay.

## Inputs
- Facility-checked application
- Wording type flag (standard or bespoke)

## Outputs
- Standard wording: application cleared directly for sanctions screening
- Bespoke wording: Legal sign-off on guarantee text

## Why it matters
Bespoke wording adds unpredictable delay because Legal review has no committed turnaround.
