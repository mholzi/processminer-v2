## What happens
The Trade Finance Officer generates the guarantee instrument in the Trade Finance System using the approved wording and terms. The executed guarantee is transmitted to the beneficiary's bank via SWIFT. The client's facility utilisation is updated to reflect the new commitment.

## Inputs
- Recorded issuance approval
- Approved guarantee wording and terms
- Beneficiary bank SWIFT details

## Outputs
- Executed guarantee instrument generated in the Trade Finance System
- Guarantee transmitted to beneficiary's bank via SWIFT
- Client facility utilisation updated

## Why it matters
This step converts the internal approval into the legally binding instrument the client's counterparty requires and completes the process.
