## The gap
The Legal team has no committed SLA for bespoke guarantee wording review, and no pre-approved clause library exists. Review duration is unpredictable and the AI wording classifier has no authoritative standard library to train or validate against.

## Resolution
Agree a 2-business-day SLA with Legal and build a structured clause library from historical standard-template guarantees. The library doubles as the AI classifier's ground truth and reduces full-review volume. Both are prerequisites for TD-2 and TD-5 and underpin the wording-review target state TS-2.

## Status
Open. SLA negotiation and clause-library build must be completed before the AI classifier's shadow-mode deployment begins.
