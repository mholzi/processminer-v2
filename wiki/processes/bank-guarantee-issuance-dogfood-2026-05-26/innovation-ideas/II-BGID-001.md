## The idea
Route applications that are within facility limits, use a standard wording template, and pass sanctions cleanly into a same-day fast lane with system-only checks. The four-eyes control stays but is invoked immediately, not after batched legal review.

## Expected benefit
Cut turnaround from 3 business days to under 4 hours for the ~60% of applications that qualify. Lifts the bank to parity with BNP Connexis Trade in the standard-wording segment.

## Feasibility
Requires a structured wording-template flag on PS-BGID-003, a facility-headroom auto-check on PS-BGID-002, and a routing rule in TFS. No new product; reuses existing controls. Estimated 2 quarters.
