## The idea
Expose process milestones — intake accepted, credit check passed, wording approved, SWIFT delivered — to the corporate via the existing Corporate Portal with timestamps and the responsible function.

## Expected benefit
Removes opaque waiting, cuts inbound 'where is my guarantee?' calls to the relationship manager, and lets clients self-serve status questions during the legal-review wait.

## Feasibility
Reuses existing TFS status events and the Corporate Portal pipeline. Mostly a UI + entitlement change. 1 quarter; low risk.
