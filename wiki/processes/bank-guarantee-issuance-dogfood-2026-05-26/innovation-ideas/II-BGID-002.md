## The idea
Add an AI pre-classifier at PS-BGID-001 intake that scores each guarantee wording against the bank's standard-template library and flags bespoke clauses, risky obligations and missing carve-outs before the application reaches Legal.

## Expected benefit
Routes 70%+ of intake straight to the standard path, concentrates the legal team on truly bespoke wording, and cuts PS-BGID-003 median time from 2.4 to under 0.8 business days.

## Feasibility
Needs a wording-clause dataset and a vendor or in-house LLM with legal-domain fine-tuning. Model risk + explainability sign-off required. 3-4 quarters with a phased shadow-mode rollout.
