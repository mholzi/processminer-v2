## Responsibility
The Legal team reviews and signs off non-standard guarantee wording before issuance proceeds.

## In this process
Guarantees with bespoke wording are routed to Legal for review and sign-off at ps-3. Standard-wording guarantees bypass Legal entirely. Legal has no committed turnaround for this review, which is a known source of delay.
