## Responsibility
The Trade Finance Manager is accountable for issuance approval and the four-eyes control gate that prevents unauthorised guarantee issuance.

## In this process
The manager reviews the assembled application package and approves issuance at ps-5. The approval is recorded in the Trade Finance System, satisfying the four-eyes control (C1).
