## Responsibility
The Compliance Analyst is responsible for sanctions and compliance screening of every guarantee beneficiary before issuance is approved.

## In this process
The analyst screens the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country against the sanctions list using the Sanctions Screening Tool (ps-4). The screening result is attached to the application. A screening hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.
