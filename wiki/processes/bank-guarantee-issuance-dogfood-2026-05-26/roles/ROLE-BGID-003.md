## Responsibility
The Head of Trade Finance provides escalated approval authority for large-value bank guarantee issuances.

## In this process
For guarantees above EUR 5 million, the Head of Trade Finance must provide an additional sign-off at the issuance approval step (ps-5), alongside the Trade Finance Manager's approval.
