## Responsibility
The Trade Finance Officer is responsible for the operational steps of the guarantee issuance process — application intake, credit and facility checks, and guarantee generation and delivery.

## In this process
The officer receives and validates incoming guarantee applications (ps-1), confirms the client's facility limit is sufficient (ps-2), generates the guarantee instrument in the Trade Finance System, and arranges SWIFT transmission to the beneficiary's bank (ps-6). The client's facility utilisation is updated on completion.
