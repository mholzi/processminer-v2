## The decision
Agree a 2-business-day formal SLA with the Legal team for bespoke guarantee wording review and build a structured pre-approved clause library covering at least 80% of standard-template cases, maintained by Legal and used as the AI classifier's training ground truth.

## Options considered
- Formal 2-business-day SLA plus a maintained pre-approved clause library (chosen).
- Outsource bespoke wording review to external legal counsel with a contractual turnaround.
- Accept the status quo of ad-hoc Legal review with no committed SLA.

## Rationale
Legal review with no SLA is the root cause of unpredictable delay for bespoke cases. A formal SLA establishes accountability; the clause library reduces full-review volume and is a prerequisite for the AI classifier's shadow-mode accuracy validation.
