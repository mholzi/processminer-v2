## Definition
The elapsed time from a complete guarantee application to delivery of the executed guarantee to the beneficiary, measured in business days. Scoped to standard-wording guarantees with no sanctions screening hit.

## Current reading
No measured actual is reported in the source document. The process carries a design target of 3 business days; no actual performance data against that target is published.

## Why it matters
Turnaround time is the service-level commitment given to corporate clients. Consistent achievement is required to underpin any SLA-backed trade finance offering.
