## The trend
SWIFT is retiring MT category-7 messages including MT760 by November 2027 in favour of ISO 20022 guarantee messages, with a structured data payload replacing the free-text format.

## Relevance
PS-BGID-006 SWIFT delivery and downstream reconciliation will need re-wiring to the ISO 20022 schema. Mapping legal wording into structured fields is a non-trivial design choice.

## Evidence
SWIFT-published roadmap mandates ISO 20022 cut-over for guarantee traffic by November 2027; no extension announced.
