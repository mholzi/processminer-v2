## The trend
Tier-1 trade-finance desks are deploying LLM-based wording assistants to pre-classify guarantees as standard or bespoke and to flag risky clauses before a human legal review.

## Relevance
PS-BGID-003 Wording Review is the largest single contributor to turnaround. An AI pre-classifier could route 70%+ of applications onto a no-touch fast lane and concentrate legal effort on the genuinely bespoke 30%.

## Evidence
Celent 2026 report: 11 of the top-20 European trade-finance banks have piloted AI wording-review in the last 18 months; 4 have moved to production.
