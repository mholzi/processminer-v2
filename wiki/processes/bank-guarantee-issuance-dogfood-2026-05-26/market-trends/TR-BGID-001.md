## The trend
Corporate clients increasingly expect same-day or intraday issuance of standard bank guarantees, in line with the speed of payments and FX. Multi-day SLAs are seen as a competitive disadvantage.

## Relevance
The current 3-business-day target sits well above the emerging benchmark. The process must either compress the standard path or carve out a fast lane for low-risk applications to remain competitive.

## Evidence
BCG 2026 survey: 62% of corporate treasurers cite turnaround time as the top factor in choosing a guarantee bank, up from 41% in 2023.
