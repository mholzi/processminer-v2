## The gap
Application Intake (PS-BGID-001) has no detective or preventive control. The Trade Finance Officer who receives the application is the same person who checks its completeness — there is no second pair of eyes and no system-enforced validation gate at this step.

## Risk
An incomplete or incorrectly scoped application could advance if the officer's manual check is missed, creating rework or errors at later steps. The risk is bounded: the facility check (CP-BGID-003) and the four-eyes issuance approval (CP-BGID-001) provide substantive gates downstream.

## Remediation
Accepted risk. The SME confirmed no control at intake is deliberate: the material gates are the facility limit check (CP-BGID-003) at step 2 and the four-eyes approval (CP-BGID-001) at step 5. No additional control is planned.
