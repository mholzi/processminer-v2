## Description
Applications frequently stall at the Credit and Facility Check step when the client has not pre-arranged sufficient guarantee facility limit to cover the requested guarantee amount. The application is parked and routed to the Credit team, making this the most common reason for processing delay.

## Impact
Stalled applications break the 3-business-day turnaround target for standard guarantees. The Credit team engagement adds an unquantified cycle-time overhead; clients experience unexpected delays with no clear resolution timeline.

## Root cause
Clients do not monitor their facility utilisation in real time and may be unaware of their available limit before submitting an application. There is no pre-submission limit check or proactive notification to prompt a client to arrange a limit increase in advance.
