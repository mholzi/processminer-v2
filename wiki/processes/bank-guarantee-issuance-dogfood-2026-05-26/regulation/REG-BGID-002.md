## What it requires
Guarantees issued on demand terms must conform to the ICC Uniform Rules for Demand Guarantees (URDG 758). The document does not describe the specific obligations URDG 758 imposes on form, content or demand presentation; those details are not stated in the source.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantees follow URDG 758 where the guarantee is issued on demand terms.

## How it is met
The Wording Review step refers bespoke wording to the Legal team for sign-off before issuance. The document does not state how URDG conformance is verified for each issuance or whether standard templates are considered URDG-conformant.
