## The journey
Corporate clients submit guarantee applications electronically via the Citi Trade Portal module inside CitiDirect. Where a revolving framework agreement is in place and wording is pre-agreed, Citi commits to delivering an electronically-signed eGuarantee within two business days of submission.

## Relevance
A two-day SLA backed by a contractual framework agreement is a concrete speed benchmark. Processes without pre-agreed wording libraries or revolving credit facilities will struggle to match this, exposing a turnaround and digitisation gap for repeat-issuer clients.
