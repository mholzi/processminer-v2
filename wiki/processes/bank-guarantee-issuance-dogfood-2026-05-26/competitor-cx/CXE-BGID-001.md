## The journey
A corporate client (Vanderlande) initiates a guarantee request inside Surecomp's RIVO platform; an XML-formatted Swift/ICC open-API message transmits the instruction to BNP Paribas in real time, without manual intervention, email, or PDF exchange, and the issued guarantee is returned digitally through the same channel.

## Relevance
The pilot sets a benchmark for straight-through corporate-to-bank guarantee issuance. Banks that still rely on email, PDF forms, or branch visits to collect guarantee instructions face a visible experience gap relative to this emerging API-native channel.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas, Vanderlande, and Surecomp announced successful completion of the Swift-ICC API guarantee pilot on 14 November 2024. BNP Paribas head of digital trade channels called it "a significant industry milestone."
