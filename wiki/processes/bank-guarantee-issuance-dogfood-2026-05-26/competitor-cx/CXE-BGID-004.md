## The journey
Corporate clients access cash management, trade finance, supply chain finance, and guarantee services through a single SG Markets platform. Trade finance and guarantees sit alongside payments in one digital hub, with enhanced ergonomics and security delivered through an open-banking integration strategy.

## Relevance
Euromoney singled out SocGen's unified cross-product platform as the key differentiator — corporates no longer navigate separate portals per product. Banks that separate guarantee issuance from cash management portals create unnecessary client effort at application and approval stages.

## Evidence
Euromoney named Société Générale Europe's Best Transaction Bank 2025 on 13 November 2024, citing its integrated platform spanning trade finance, cash management, and correspondent banking as the decisive factor.
