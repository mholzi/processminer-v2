## The journey
Corporate clients submit guarantee and trade finance instructions through the Komgo platform, which connects to ING via Konsole API, eliminating media breaks and manual re-keying. Requests flow straight into ING's back-end systems; clients track status in real time through the InsideBusiness portal across 35 countries.

## Relevance
ING's API-native intake removes the handoff friction — fax, email, PDF — that remains common in mid-tier bank guarantee workflows. The combination of a third-party TMS connector and unified real-time tracking sets a high bar for turnaround predictability.

## Evidence
Komgo case study documents ING's Konsole API connectivity, noting reduced media breaks, automated backend data integration, and operational efficiency gains for corporate guarantee applicants.
