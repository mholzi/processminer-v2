## The journey
SME exporters register and apply for trade finance entirely online in minutes, receive a decision within 48 hours, and access payment as soon as goods ship — no paper, no branch, no relationship manager required. MODIFI positions itself as a direct-to-SME alternative to bank-intermediated trade finance.

## Relevance
MODIFI's sub-48-hour fully digital journey sets a speed and friction benchmark that corporate treasurers compare against incumbent bank guarantee processes. Its UX raises the bar for digital onboarding and status visibility even where bank guarantees are not offered directly.

## Evidence
MODIFI product page: 100% digital — apply online, register in minutes, access payment in days. Finextra: SMEs apply in under ten minutes with responses generally within 48 hours.
