## The journey
J.P. Morgan's Trade Express tool gives corporate clients and counterparties unauthenticated 24/7 visibility into bank guarantee and LC status. No portal login is required — clients enter a branch code, reference number, date, currency and amount to see statuses including received, in-process, processed, and settled.

## Relevance
Login-free counterparty status tracking removes a major source of client service calls during the guarantee lifecycle. A process that requires clients to phone or email for status updates is measurably behind this self-service benchmark, driving unnecessary operational cost.
