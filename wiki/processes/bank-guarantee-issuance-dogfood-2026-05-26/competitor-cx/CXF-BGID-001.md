## The journey
Corporate clients submit, track and amend guarantee and LC applications through a single digital channel connecting multiple banks in real time. AI text-conversion processes emailed guarantee wording in under ten seconds, compressing issuance from days to hours with no branch visit required.

## Relevance
The multi-bank, single-portal model directly challenges a bank's lock-in advantage. If Surecomp-powered rivals offer real-time status transparency and sub-hour issuance, corporates with high guarantee volumes will benchmark their incumbent bank against that standard.

## Evidence
DZ BANK adopted RIVO in June 2024 for "real-time corporate communication, transaction status transparency and accelerated trade finance issuance." Surecomp committed in November 2024 to "reducing bank guarantee processing time by thirty percent" via AI.
