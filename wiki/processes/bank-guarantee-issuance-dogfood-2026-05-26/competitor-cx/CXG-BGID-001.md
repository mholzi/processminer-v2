## The journey
Corporate clients apply for, amend, and track guarantees and SBLCs through HSBCnet — a single portal covering all trade products. A configurable Trade dashboard shows outstanding and pending transactions; smartform applications feed directly into processing with 24/7 access.

## Relevance
HSBC bundles guarantees with the full trade product suite inside one authenticated portal, raising client expectations for real-time visibility and self-service amendment. A process that relies on email or phone intake creates a noticeable channel gap against this benchmark.
