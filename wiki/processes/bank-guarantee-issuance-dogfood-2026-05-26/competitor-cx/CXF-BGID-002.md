## The journey
Corporates order bank guarantees and open letters of credit entirely online, with requests reaching bank staff instantly and status visible around the clock. No branch visit is needed; minimum paperwork is required; issuance time has been cut from two working days to several hours.

## Relevance
Comarch-powered banks already set an hours-not-days benchmark for guarantee issuance. A corporate client experiencing that speed will expect the same self-service transparency and absence of branch dependency from any incumbent bank.

## Evidence
Raiffeisen Bank Polska case study: Comarch Guarantees Online "decreased the time of issuing an average banking guarantee from two working days to several hours." Corporates manage guarantees "without visiting the branch office."
