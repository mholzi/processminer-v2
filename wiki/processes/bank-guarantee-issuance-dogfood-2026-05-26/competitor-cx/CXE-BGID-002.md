## The journey
Corporate clients apply for, issue, and manage guarantees, sureties, and standby LCs through a web-based Guarantee Vault platform co-developed with Digital Vault Services. Domestic guarantees can be issued fully digitally; the platform acts as a central register spanning multiple guarantor banks.

## Relevance
Commerzbank's multi-bank vault lets a corporate treasurer consolidate guarantee management across banking partners in one portal. Banks with siloed, single-bank portals create operational friction for large corporates managing guarantee exposure across several institutions.

## Evidence
Commerzbank product page confirms digital guarantee, surety, and standby LC processing via the Guarantee Vault Multigarantenplattform, built with Digital Vault Services GmbH, accessible through a web interface.
