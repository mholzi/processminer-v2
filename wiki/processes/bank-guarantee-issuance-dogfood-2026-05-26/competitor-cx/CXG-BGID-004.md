## The journey
Standard Chartered completed the first live bank guarantee transaction under ICC-Swift API standards via Komgo's Konsole platform in January 2026. Corporates initiate guarantees through Komgo; the API automates instruction receipt and issuance, delivering "greater speed, transparency and reliability" with no paper handling end-to-end.

## Relevance
API-native issuance triggered directly from a corporate ERP or trade platform is the emerging frontier for high-volume guarantee clients. Standard Chartered's milestone sets client expectations around fully automated, paperless issuance that most bank processes have not yet reached.
