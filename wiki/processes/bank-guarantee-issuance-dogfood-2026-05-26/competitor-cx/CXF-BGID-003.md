## The journey
Corporates access a unified portal to apply for guarantees, submit wording, track status and retrieve documents 24/7 without calling their bank. Finastra's Assist.AI layer adds conversational guidance through the guarantee application workflow via a cloud-ready REST API layer.

## Relevance
Finastra's platform, deployed by banks worldwide and named Euromoney's best trade finance software in 2025, raises corporate expectations for always-on guided guarantee origination. Banks without a comparable portal face attrition as treasury teams standardise on self-service.
