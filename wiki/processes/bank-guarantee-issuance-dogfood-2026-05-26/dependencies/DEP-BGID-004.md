## The dependency
Applications with wording the AI classifier flags as bespoke, or that carry a low confidence score, are routed to the Legal team for review and sign-off before issuance proceeds.

## What crosses the boundary
The guarantee application with the AI classifier's clause-risk annotations is handed to Legal. Legal returns approved or amended wording for inclusion in the guarantee instrument before the process advances.

## Why it matters
Legal sign-off is a compliance requirement for non-standard wording. The formal 2-business-day SLA is designed to make this dependency predictable rather than an open-ended source of delay.
