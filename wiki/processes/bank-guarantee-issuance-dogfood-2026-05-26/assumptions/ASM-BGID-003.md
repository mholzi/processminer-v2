## The assumption
The Legal team has sufficient capacity and process maturity to commit to a 2-business-day SLA for bespoke guarantee wording review, once the pre-approved clause library and AI pre-annotation reduce full-review volume.

## Why it is unconfirmed
Legal team workload, resource constraints and existing commitments have not been assessed. The 2-business-day SLA target has not been discussed or agreed with Legal and may require additional headcount or process investment.

## Impact if wrong
If Legal cannot commit to a 2-business-day SLA, bespoke cases remain on the ad-hoc path and the AI classifier cannot be validated against a baseline, blocking the wording-review target state.
