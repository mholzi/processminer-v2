## The assumption
AML, sanctions and URDG 758 obligations do not require a human officer to manually review each standard-wording, in-facility application; automated gate checks satisfy the regulatory requirements provided the four-eyes issuance approval control is retained.

## Why it is unconfirmed
The regulatory interpretation has not been validated with Compliance or Legal. EU AML directives and URDG 758 impose process obligations but their precise application to automated intake routing has not been formally documented or approved internally.

## Impact if wrong
If Compliance determines each gate requires a human touch-point, the fast lane and AI classifier auto-routing must include mandatory human review stages, significantly reducing the throughput benefit.
