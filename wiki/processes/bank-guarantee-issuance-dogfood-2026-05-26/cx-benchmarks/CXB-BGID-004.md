## The benchmark
83% of corporate treasury and finance executives rate real-time forecasting and transaction visibility as high value, yet only 31% of banks share that assessment — a 52-point perception gap that translates directly into unmet client expectations.

## Relevance
For guarantee issuance, clients expect live status updates at each stage: credit approved, document prepared, guarantee issued, advice sent. The visibility gap identified by CGI suggests most banks are not meeting this expectation today.

## Evidence
CGI Transaction Banking Survey 2025 (n=356 corporates, 357 banks): 83% of corporates vs 31% of banks rate real-time forecasting as high value — a 52-point gap — cgi.com, fetched 2026-05-26.
