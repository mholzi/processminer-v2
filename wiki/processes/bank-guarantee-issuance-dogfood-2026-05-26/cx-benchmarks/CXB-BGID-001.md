## The benchmark
Paper-based bank guarantee issuance typically takes 3–4 business days end-to-end. Leading banks piloting digital fast-track workflows can compress this to minutes for pre-approved wording and verified counterparties, setting a clear digitisation benchmark.

## Relevance
At 80–150 issuances per month, shaving even one day off the median cycle reduces total client wait by 80–150 person-days monthly. Clients in contract-critical situations cite guarantee timing as a working-capital bottleneck and evaluate banks accordingly.

## Evidence
HSBC 2023 announcement on its digital demand-guarantee pilot reported reduction from a "typical 3–4 days" to near-real-time issuance for eligible transactions — Business Money, fetched 2026-05-26.
