## The benchmark
67% of corporate trade finance clients plan to change their roster of bank partners within 12 months, and 76% will alter the number of partners they work with — signalling high churn risk when service expectations go unmet.

## Relevance
Only 54% of surveyed corporates said their bank is meeting expectations; 18% expressed active dissatisfaction. For a guarantee-issuing bank, slow turnaround and lack of digital access are the primary friction drivers cited as switching triggers.

## Evidence
Accenture survey of 675 trade finance executives across 15 countries (Aug–Sep 2022): 67% planning partner roster change; 76% changing number of partners; 18% dissatisfied with bank dealings overall.
