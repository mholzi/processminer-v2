## The benchmark
Only 16% of corporate trade finance clients — mostly large multinationals — describe their own trade finance operations as fully digital. The majority still rely on paper-heavy, manual bank interactions for instruments such as guarantees.

## Relevance
Low corporate digital maturity means digital portal adoption will be constrained unless onboarding is actively supported. Clients upgrading their own operations will migrate to banks with strong digital channels first.

## Evidence
Accenture 2022 survey (n=675, 15 countries): "only 16% of companies — most of them larger corporates — report that they are fully digital." Companies cited efficiency and faster turnaround as top priorities.
