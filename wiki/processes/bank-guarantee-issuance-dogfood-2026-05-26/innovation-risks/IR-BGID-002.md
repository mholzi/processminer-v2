## The risk
Once same-day issuance (II-BGID-001) becomes visible to clients on the standard segment, the bank loses the rhetorical cover for the slower 3-day path on bespoke wording — even where the slow path is appropriate.

## Likelihood & impact
Likely. Sales teams will be pushed to commit same-day across all segments. Impact: relationship-manager friction, attempted overrides on legal review, reputational hit when bespoke applications slip the implied SLA.

## Mitigation
Publish clear segment-specific SLAs to clients up front; train RMs on the bespoke triage criteria; instrument over-promised cases and review monthly with the trade-finance head.
