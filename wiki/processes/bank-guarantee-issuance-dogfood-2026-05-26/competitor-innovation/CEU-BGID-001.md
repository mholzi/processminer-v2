## The move
BNP Paribas's Connexis Trade portal offers same-day issuance for standard-wording guarantees up to EUR 5 million, with automated facility check, sanctions screen and MT760 dispatch — sub-30-minute end-to-end in the published cases.

## Relevance
Direct head-to-head proof that 3-business-day SLAs are no longer competitive in the standard-wording segment. We are visibly slower for the same product, with the same risk profile, to the same beneficiary banks.

## Evidence
BNP product page (Feb 2026) and Trade Finance Global case study citing 12-minute median issuance for in-policy applications.
