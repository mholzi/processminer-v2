## The move
Mitigram has positioned itself as the marketplace where corporates request guarantees from multiple banks in parallel, with a curated, structured wording library that removes ambiguity from intake.

## Relevance
The wording library shifts the bespoke-vs-standard decision upstream — the corporate makes it at submission. If clients route through Mitigram, the bank becomes a price-takers on an already-standardised intake.

## Evidence
Mitigram reported 40+ trade-finance banks on platform and 2× year-on-year guarantee volume in 2025 (GTR News, January 2026).
