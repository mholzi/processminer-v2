## Purpose
Beneficiary and country screening against sanctions lists.

## Role in this process
Used by the Compliance Analyst during the sanctions and compliance screening step to screen the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country. The screening result is attached to the application; a hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.
