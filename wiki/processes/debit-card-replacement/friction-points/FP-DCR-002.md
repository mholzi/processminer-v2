## Description
Once the replacement is ordered the client has no usable debit card at all until the new one arrives by post, four to six business days later.

## Root cause
The process only ever issues a physical card by post; there is no instant digital card or expedited option to bridge the gap.

## Client impact
For several days the client cannot pay by card and must fall back on cash or another card — a real disruption the bank does nothing to soften.
