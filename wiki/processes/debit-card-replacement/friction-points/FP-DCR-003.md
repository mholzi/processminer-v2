## Description
After the call the client has no visibility of the replacement card — not whether it has been produced, posted, or is about to arrive.

## Root cause
The card bureau interface is a one-way instruction with no tracking or delivery status fed back to the client or the bank.

## Client impact
The client is left guessing, and if the card never arrives they only find out by calling again — restarting the whole wait from zero.
