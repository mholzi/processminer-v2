## Description
A client who cannot answer the knowledge-based security questions on the phone is turned away from the call and told to visit a branch with photo identification.

## Root cause
Knowledge-based verification relies on answers the client set years ago and rarely revisits, and the phone channel has no stronger verification fallback.

## Client impact
The client must make an in-person trip just to be believed, adding days to a process they expected to finish on the call — and the card stays unreplaced meanwhile.
