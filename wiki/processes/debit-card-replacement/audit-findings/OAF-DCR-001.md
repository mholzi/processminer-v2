## Finding
A 2025 thematic review of Retail Card Operations found that the fraud-exposure check on lost and stolen card replacements is not consistently evidenced: the analyst's review leaves no standard record, so an auditor cannot confirm it was performed or what it concluded.

## Recommendation
Require the fraud-exposure review to record a dated, attributable outcome for every lost or stolen card case, so the control is auditable and its timeliness can be measured.
