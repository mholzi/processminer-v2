## The moment
The client realises their card is lost or stolen and reaches for the phone — not knowing yet whether money has already gone.

## Why it matters
This is where the relationship is tested. The client is frightened and wants the bank to be fast, calm and in control. How the first minute feels colours the whole experience.

## Design implication
The first contact must reach a reassuring outcome — the card blocked — with as little friction as possible, and never make a frightened client feel doubted.
