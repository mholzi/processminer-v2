## The moment
The agent confirms the card is blocked and can no longer be used — the single thing the client called to hear.

## Why it matters
This is the relief point that resolves the fear the client arrived with. Delivered clearly and early, it turns an alarming event into a handled one.

## Design implication
Block the card and confirm it as early in the contact as possible — never leave the client waiting through other steps before they hear it.
