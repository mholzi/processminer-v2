## The moment
The call ends and the client faces four to six business days with no working debit card and no way to see where the replacement is.

## Why it matters
The fear is resolved but the inconvenience is not. A multi-day gap with no card and no visibility is the lasting impression most clients take from the process.

## Design implication
Close the card gap — an instant digital card, or a tracked and faster delivery — so the client is not left stranded and guessing.
