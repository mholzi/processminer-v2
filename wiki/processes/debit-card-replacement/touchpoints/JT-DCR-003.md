## What the client does
The client listens as the agent confirms the existing card has been blocked and can no longer be used.

## What the bank does
The agent places the block in the Card Management System and tells the client, plainly, that the card is now stopped.

## Experience
This is the moment of relief — the worry of someone spending on the card lifts. A clear, immediate confirmation here sets the tone for the rest of the experience.
