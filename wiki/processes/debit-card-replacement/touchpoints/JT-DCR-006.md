## What the client does
An already-logged-in client opens the mobile app, finds their card, and raises a replacement request, choosing the reason from a short list.

## What the bank does
The app records the request and the reported reason and routes it for processing, with no agent involved.

## Experience
Quick and low-effort for a confident client, but it is a silent channel — no live confirmation that the card is blocked, which an anxious client wants.
