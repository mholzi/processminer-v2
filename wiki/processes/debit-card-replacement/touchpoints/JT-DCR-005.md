## What the client does
At the end of the call the client hears when the new card should arrive and that it must be activated on first use.

## What the bank does
The agent states the expected delivery window — four to six business days — and the activation requirement, and closes the request.

## Experience
The client now knows what to expect, but also that they face several days without a working card. The delivery window is the part they remember.
