## What the client does
The client calls the Contact Centre, often within minutes of noticing the card is gone, and tells the agent it is lost, stolen, or damaged and that they need a replacement.

## What the bank does
The agent takes the call, captures the reported reason, and opens the replacement request in the customer record.

## Experience
The client is usually anxious — a missing card feels urgent — and wants to hear, fast, that nothing can be spent on it. The call is their reassurance.
