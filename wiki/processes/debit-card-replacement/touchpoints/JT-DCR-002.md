## What the client does
Before anything can be done, the client must answer a set of knowledge-based security questions to prove they are the genuine cardholder.

## What the bank does
The agent asks three security questions and checks the answers against the customer record, needing at least two correct to proceed.

## Experience
This is the most effortful point of the call. A client who is stressed, travelling, or simply set the answers up years ago can stumble — and the clock is ticking on a card they cannot yet block.
