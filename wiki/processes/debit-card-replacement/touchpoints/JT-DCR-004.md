## What the client does
A client who could not pass phone verification must travel to a branch and present photo identification in person before the replacement can be processed.

## What the bank does
Branch staff verify the photo identification and resume the replacement request the Contact Centre could not complete.

## Experience
A frustrating detour: the client has already explained the problem once, and is now asked to make a trip in person just to be believed. It can add days.
