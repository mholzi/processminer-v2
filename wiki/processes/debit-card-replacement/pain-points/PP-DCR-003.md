## Description
For lost or stolen cards the request is handed to a separate Fraud Operations team for the exposure check. The agent who took the call has no visibility of when that review will be completed.

## Impact
The agent cannot tell the customer when their replacement will actually proceed, so customers call back repeatedly to chase progress, adding avoidable contact volume to the Contact Centre.

## Root cause
The fraud review sits in a manual queue in a separate team, with no shared SLA or case status surfaced back to the Contact Centre agent.
