## The gap
The fraud-exposure review on lost or stolen cards (CP-DCR-003) has no defined completion time and no independent or second-line check — it rests entirely on one analyst's manual judgement.

## Risk
PSD2's protection against unauthorised use depends on timely fraud detection; an unbounded, unreviewed manual check leaves that obligation only loosely assured.

## Remediation
Set a service-level standard for the fraud-exposure review and introduce a sample-based quality check or second-line review of its outcomes.
