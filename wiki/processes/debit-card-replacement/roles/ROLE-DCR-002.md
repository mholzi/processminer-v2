## Responsibility
Owns the production side of the replacement: orders the like-for-like replacement card and releases it to the card bureau for dispatch to the customer.

## In this process
The clerk orders the replacement card in the Card Management System once the existing card is blocked and any fraud check is clear, and holds the order when the registered address is unconfirmed.
