## Responsibility
Owns the fraud assessment for cards reported lost or stolen, reviewing recent transactions for unauthorised activity before the replacement proceeds.

## In this process
When a card is reported lost or stolen, the Fraud Analyst reviews recent transactions and, where suspicious activity is found, hands the case to the Card Fraud process while the replacement is held.
