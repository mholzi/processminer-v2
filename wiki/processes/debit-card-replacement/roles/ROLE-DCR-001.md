## Responsibility
Owns the customer-facing handling of a replacement request: verifies the caller's identity, blocks the existing card, and confirms the replacement and its dispatch to the customer.

## In this process
The agent receives the request, performs knowledge-based identity verification, applies the immediate card block, flags lost or stolen cards to the Fraud Analyst, and confirms the expected delivery time to the customer.
