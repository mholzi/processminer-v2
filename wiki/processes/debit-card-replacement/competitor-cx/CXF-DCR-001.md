## The journey
A Revolut customer freezes the card themselves in the app the instant it is lost, then orders a replacement immediately in the same screen with an estimated delivery date shown. While the physical card is in transit, the new card is added to Apple Pay or Google Pay and used straight away.

## Relevance
Revolut puts the whole journey — block, reorder, keep paying — in the customer's own hands, with no agent, no identity call and no waiting for a usable card. This process routes every request through an agent or app hand-off and a manual fraud queue.
