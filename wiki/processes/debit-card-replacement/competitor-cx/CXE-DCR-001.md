## The journey
A Barclays customer reports a debit card lost, stolen or damaged in the mobile app under Cards, and orders the replacement in the same flow. The new card is posted to the address on file within five working days. Customers who need a card sooner can buy a temporary debit card at selected branches.

## Relevance
Barclays pairs the standard postal replacement with a same-day fallback — a branch-issued temporary card — and a fully self-service app journey. This process offers neither: no expedited option, and a phone channel that still leans on knowledge-based verification.

## Evidence
Barclays help pages on reporting a lost, stolen or damaged debit card, and on the temporary debit card facility (GBP 12, valid 28 days), fetched May 2026.
