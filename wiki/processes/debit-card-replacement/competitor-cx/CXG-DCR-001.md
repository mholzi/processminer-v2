## The journey
When a Chase customer reports a card lost or stolen, Chase replaces it and the replacement is pushed straight into the customer's digital wallet — Apple Pay or Google Pay — so it can be used immediately, while the physical card arrives by post within three to five days.

## Relevance
The Chase customer is never without a usable card: digital access is restored at the moment of replacement. This process leaves the customer with no card at all for five to seven days — the single longest gap in the journey.

## Evidence
Chase digital-payments help pages on lost or stolen cards and Apple Pay, and WalletHub's summary of Chase replacement timelines (3-5 days), fetched May 2026.
