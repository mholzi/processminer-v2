## The benchmark
Industry research finds 74% of consumers say receiving a debit or credit card instantly matters — rising to 86% of Millennials and 85% of Gen Z. Around 40% would prefer a replacement card issued instantly rather than mailed.

## Relevance
This process has no instant option at all: every replacement is posted and takes five to seven business days. Against the benchmark the journey sits well below what most customers — and a clear majority of younger ones — now expect.

## Evidence
Vericast, 'Creating Your Card Strategy for 2026': 74% / 86% / 85% instant-issuance importance, and 40% preferring an instantly issued replacement card.
