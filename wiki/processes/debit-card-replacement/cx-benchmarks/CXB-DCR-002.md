## The benchmark
Industry data shows 47% of instantly issued cards are used within eight hours of issuance, that instantly issued cards outperform mailed cards by 53% over a 45-day window, and that they drive up to 32% more spend in the first 30 days.

## Relevance
Because this process only ever mails the replacement, the card sits unused through the postal window and the bank forgoes the early-activation and spend uplift an instant or digital card would capture.

## Evidence
ABCorp and Vericast instant-card-issuance data: 47% used within eight hours, 53% outperformance over 45 days, up to 32% more spend in the first 30 days.
