## What it requires
GDPR requires the bank to process customer personal data lawfully and only as needed, to keep it accurate and up to date, to correct it promptly when wrong, and to protect it with appropriate security.

## Why it applies
The process handles customer personal data throughout — identity details, the card number, the registered address, and recent transaction history during the fraud check — all of which fall under GDPR.

## How it is met
Identity verification (CP-DCR-001) limits who can act on the data; dispatching only to the registered address on file (CP-DCR-004) depends on that address being accurate, which the address-confirmation exception (EX-DCR-003) enforces.
