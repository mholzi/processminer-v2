## What it requires
PSD2 requires the payment service provider to let a cardholder report a lost or stolen card and have it blocked at any time, caps the cardholder's liability for unauthorised use at EUR 50, and obliges the bank to refund unauthorised payments within one business day.

## Why it applies
The debit card is a payment instrument and this process is the bank's response when it is lost, stolen or damaged — the exact event PSD2's blocking and liability rules govern. In Germany PSD2 is transposed through the ZAG and the BGB.

## How it is met
The immediate-block control (CP-DCR-002) satisfies the blocking duty; identity verification (CP-DCR-001) and the fraud-exposure review (CP-DCR-003) limit unauthorised use. Liability and refund handling sit with the separate Card Fraud process.
