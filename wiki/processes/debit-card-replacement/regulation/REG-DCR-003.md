## What it requires
The EBA outsourcing and third-party risk-management guidelines require the bank to govern functions it has outsourced — due diligence, contractual control, ongoing monitoring and exit planning — with DORA covering the ICT dimension.

## Why it applies
Card production and dispatch are performed by an external card bureau, an outsourced function this process depends on; the bureau interface and its service levels fall under third-party risk governance.

## How it is met
Oversight of the outsourced card bureau is provided by the card-bureau service-level and oversight monitoring control (CP-DCR-005); the DORA-scoped ICT aspects of the bureau interface remain to be confirmed with the SME.
