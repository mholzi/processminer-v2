## What happens
The Hiring Manager, jointly with the HRBP, conducts the formal 90-day probation review and reaches a confirmation decision. The decision is documented in Workday. Control CTL-NHO-06 requires this to be completed within 95 days of start date.

## Inputs
- Day 60 goals documented in Workday performance system
- Onboarding history and check-in records

## Outputs
- Probation decision record in Workday
- Workday probation record satisfying CTL-NHO-06

## Why it matters
The probation review is the formal gateway from 'new hire' to 'confirmed employee'. A documented, evidence-based decision satisfies the CTL-NHO-06 control requiring documentation within 95 days.
