## What happens
The Hiring Manager nominates an onboarding buddy or peer and makes the introduction to the new hire by Day 2 of employment.

## Inputs
- Team roster and availability for buddy candidates
- New hire's role and team context to identify a suitable peer

## Outputs
- Named buddy assigned and introduced to new hire
- New hire has a peer contact to support navigation of team norms during Week 1

## Why it matters
Peer support during the first week helps the new hire navigate team norms and reduces early uncertainty.
