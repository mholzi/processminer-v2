## What happens
Facilities issues the new hire's building access pass and walks them through the physical site health-and-safety orientation. Desk, locker and parking arrangements (where applicable) are confirmed, consistent with the pre-boarding allocation made at step 1.10.

## Inputs
- Confirmed desk, building pass and parking from pre-boarding (ps-10)
- Facilities sub-ticket in ServiceNow (ps-6)

## Outputs
- Activated building access pass handed to new hire
- Desk and locker assignment confirmed
- H&S walk-through completed

## Why it matters
Physical access and health-and-safety orientation are needed before the new hire can work on site independently.
