## What happens
The new hire completes all Tier 1 mandatory training modules in Cornerstone (LMS) by end of Day 5. The document lists Code of Conduct, Information Security and Data Privacy as the Tier 1 modules at the completion step (step 3.1); AML awareness is included in the Day-1 assignment (step 2.5).

## Inputs
- Mandatory training curriculum assigned in Cornerstone (ps-17)
- LMS access confirmed via first login (ps-15)
- Day 5 completion deadline set in LMS

## Outputs
- LMS completion records for all Tier 1 modules
- Compliance training completed by Day 5

## Why it matters
Control CTL-NHO-02 requires Tier 1 completion before the new hire can be granted access to production systems. Completion by Day 5 is tracked with a 100% target (§9 Metrics).
