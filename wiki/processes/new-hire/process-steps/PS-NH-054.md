## What happens
The Hiring Manager holds a check-in with the new hire at the 30-day mark. The outcome is documented in Workday against the employee record.

## Inputs
- New hire's progress in role over first 30 days
- LMS training completion status

## Outputs
- Check-in documented in Workday
- Audit trail record created to support the Day 90 probation confirmation decision

## Why it matters
The 30-day check-in is the first formal waypoint of the probation period. Documenting it in Workday creates an audit trail supporting the Day 90 confirmation decision.
