## What happens
The Hiring Manager holds a dedicated one-to-one meeting with the new hire on Day 1. The session covers role expectations, team introductions, and a first-week plan.

## Inputs
- Role profile and reporting line documentation
- First-week induction plan prepared by the Hiring Manager

## Outputs
- New hire has a first-week plan
- Role expectations communicated
- Team introduction schedule agreed

## Why it matters
Early clarity on role expectations supports new-hire engagement and time-to-productivity.
