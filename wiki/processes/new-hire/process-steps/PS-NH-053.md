## What happens
The new hire completes all role-specific training modules and any required professional certifications assigned in the LMS (Cornerstone) within the first 30 days.

## Inputs
- Role-specific training curriculum assigned in Cornerstone (LMS)
- Tier 1 mandatory training completion record (prerequisite)
- Role profile detailing required certifications

## Outputs
- LMS completion records for all role-specific modules
- New hire is qualified to take on role responsibilities without requiring supervised support

## Why it matters
Role-specific training ensures the new hire has the knowledge and any required certifications to perform their duties before taking on unsupervised responsibilities.
