## What happens
The Hiring Manager reviews the role equipment template and confirms or amends the equipment list, including any exceptions to the standard template. The document describes this as confirming the equipment list against a role template with exceptions (step 1.5). Specific hardware examples (laptop specification, peripherals, mobile device) are not described in the source and have been removed.

## Inputs
- Role equipment template
- ServiceNow onboarding ticket

## Outputs
- Confirmed equipment list with any approved exceptions
- IT sub-ticket can be raised with accurate specification

## Why it matters
Confirming the equipment list at T-7 gives IT sufficient lead time to source and ship the device before the T-2 deadline, supporting Day-1 readiness.
