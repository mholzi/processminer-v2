## What happens
The new hire reviews and provides their electronic signature on the policy acknowledgement pack sent via DocuSign. The pack covers key policies including the Acceptable Use Policy (POL-IS-002) and Data Privacy Policy (POL-DP-003).

## Inputs
- Policy acknowledgement DocuSign envelope prepared by HR Operations
- Active DocuSign account accessible via new hire's corporate email

## Outputs
- Signed policy acknowledgement envelope completed and stored in DocuSign
- Evidential record created confirming the new hire has acknowledged their policy obligations

## Why it matters
Signed policy acknowledgements create an evidential record that the new hire has been made aware of and accepted their obligations under key policies.
