## What happens
HR Operations sends the new hire a Day-1 logistics email covering arrival time, office location, dress code, and a contact name. These four content items are stated explicitly in step 1.11. The claims that the email confirms device delivery status or flags outstanding pre-boarding tasks are not in the source and have been removed; likewise 'first-day schedule' is not mentioned.

## Inputs
- Confirmed desk location
- Arrival time and contact information

## Outputs
- Day-1 logistics email sent to new hire covering arrival time, location, dress code and contact
- New hire is informed of Day-1 arrangements and can prepare for arrival

## Why it matters
Clear logistics communication ensures the new hire knows where to go and who to contact on Day 1.
