## What happens
The Hiring Manager conducts a 60-day check-in with the new hire. Goals are set and recorded in the performance system (Workday).

## Inputs
- New hire's progress and performance to date
- Role expectations as defined in role profile

## Outputs
- Formal goals entered in performance management system (Workday)
- New hire has documented performance expectations ahead of the Day 90 probation review

## Why it matters
Setting formal, documented goals at Day 60 gives the new hire clear expectations for the Day 90 probation review.
