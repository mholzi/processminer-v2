## Purpose
Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that manages candidates through the recruitment pipeline up to hire.

## Role in this process
Greenhouse is the source of the candidate-to-hire handover. The onboarding process requires the approved requisition to be closed in the ATS as a prerequisite (section 4).
