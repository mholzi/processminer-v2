## Purpose
Electronic signature platform used to collect legally binding signatures on contracts and policy acknowledgement documents.

## Role in this process
HR Ops collects the signed contract and documents via DocuSign at T-7 (step 1.4). The new hire acknowledges the policy pack on Day 1 (step 2.6). A late signature triggers an expedited approval flow.
