## Purpose
Identity provider and Single Sign-On (SSO) platform managing user accounts, authentication and access entitlements across the organisation.

## Role in this process
IT provisions the Okta account, email and role-based access at T-3 (step 1.8). The new hire completes first login and MFA enrolment on Day 1 (step 2.3). Pre-issued access is revoked on no-show.
