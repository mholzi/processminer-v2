## Purpose
Mobile Device Management (MDM) platform used to enrol, configure and manage corporate end-user devices.

## Role in this process
IT ships the new hire's device pre-imaged and enrolled in MDM at T-2 (step 1.9). If the device is delayed beyond Day 1, a loaner kit is issued and the shortfall is tracked with IT (exception, section 8).
