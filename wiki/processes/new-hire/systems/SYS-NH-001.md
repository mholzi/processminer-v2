## Purpose
Human Resources Information System (HRIS) managing the employee master record, organisational structure and compensation.

## Role in this process
HR Ops triggers the onboarding workflow at T-10 (step 1.1) and creates the employee record at T-5 (step 1.7). Workday records check-in outcomes (step 4.2) and probation decisions (step 5.2), and enforces CTL-NHO-05 segregation of duties.
