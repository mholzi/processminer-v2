## Purpose
Third-party background screening provider performing background and right-to-work checks.

## Role in this process
Compliance initiates background and right-to-work checks at T-10 (step 1.3). The HireRight report evidences control CTL-NHO-01, blocking access provisioning until screening is clear. An adverse finding triggers escalation and suspends provisioning.
