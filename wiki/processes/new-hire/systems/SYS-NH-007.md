## Purpose
Learning Management System (LMS) used to assign, deliver and track mandatory training curricula for employees.

## Role in this process
L&D assigns mandatory Tier 1 training on Day 1 (step 2.5); completion by Day 5 is required by CTL-NHO-02. LMS records are the control evidence. Role-specific training and certifications are tracked through Day 30 (step 4.1).
