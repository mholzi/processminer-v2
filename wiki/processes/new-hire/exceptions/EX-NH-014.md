## Description
The new hire does not appear on their confirmed first day without advance notice.

## Handling
HR Operations attempts contact within 2 hours of the expected arrival time. If no response is received within 24 hours, the withdrawal procedure is initiated. Any pre-issued access is revoked.

## Impact
Medium. The role remains unfilled and provisioning work must be undone. Revocation of pre-issued access is a security-critical sub-step that must not be skipped.
