## Description
The new hire's pre-imaged, MDM-enrolled device does not arrive before or on Day 1 due to supply or logistics delays.

## Handling
IT Service Desk issues a loaner device from the local hub. The shortage is tracked with IT until the permanent device is available.

## Impact
Medium. The new hire can still onboard using the loaner but may experience delays in accessing role-specific tooling until the permanent device is assigned.
