## Description
HireRight returns an adverse finding during the pre-boarding background screening check, before the new hire's start date.

## Handling
Provisioning is paused immediately. HR Operations escalates to the HRBP and Compliance / Risk. The adverse-action procedure is followed.

## Impact
High. Failing to act on an adverse finding exposes the organisation to regulatory sanction and reputational harm. The adverse-action procedure governs next steps including any outcome for the hire.
