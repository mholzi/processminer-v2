## Description
The new hire has not returned the signed contract by the T-3 deadline, leaving fewer than three working days before the start date.

## Handling
The hiring manager and HRBP jointly approve an expedited flow. The exception and approval are recorded in the exception log.

## Impact
Medium. Without a signed contract on file the organisation's employment terms may be harder to enforce. The exception log entry and manager / HRBP approval create an audit trail but do not substitute for the signed document.
