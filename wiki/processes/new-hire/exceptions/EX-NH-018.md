## Description
The new hire requires a work visa or permit before they are legally entitled to begin work in the hiring country.

## Handling
A Phase 0 — visa and work permit verification — is inserted before Phase 1 of the standard onboarding flow. Phase 1 does not commence until the right to work is legally established.

## Impact
High. Starting the standard onboarding process before valid work authorisation is in place would constitute illegal working. Delays in visa processing can materially push the start date, affecting business planning and candidate experience.
