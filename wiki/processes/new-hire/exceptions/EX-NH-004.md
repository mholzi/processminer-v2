## Description
The new hire is unable to present the required right-to-work or I-9 documents on or before Day 1.

## Handling
Employment does not commence. HR Operations works with the candidate to resolve documentation and agrees a revised start date.

## Impact
High. Commencing employment without right-to-work verification is a strict-liability offence in most jurisdictions. The hire cannot begin work until valid documentation is provided and verified.
