## What it checks
Whether the hiring manager's probation confirmation decision has been recorded in Workday within 95 days of the new hire's start date.

## Control activity
The 90-day probation review and confirmation decision is conducted by the Hiring Manager and HRBP (step 5.2, Day 90). HR Operations closes the onboarding ticket and archives evidence at step 5.3 (Day 95). The Workday probation record constitutes the evidence.

## Risk addressed
Without a documented decision, the organisation may inadvertently pass an employee through probation without formal review, losing the ability to act on performance concerns and creating employment law exposure.

## Timing
Per hire. The review is due at the 90-day probation meeting; documentation must be recorded in Workday by Day 95 at the latest.
