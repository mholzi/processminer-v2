## What it checks
Whether the new hire holds valid right-to-work or I-9 documentation, physically verified by HR Operations before or on the first day of employment.

## Control activity
HR Operations greets the new hire on Day 1 and completes the I-9 or right-to-work verification in person (step 2.1). The signed I-9 or RTW form is filed in the employee record as evidence. If documentation is incomplete, employment does not commence.

## Risk addressed
Commencing employment without valid right-to-work verification exposes the organisation to illegal working penalties, regulatory sanctions and reputational damage.

## Timing
Per hire, on or before Day 1. Must be completed at the Day 1 greeting step before the employee begins substantive work.
