## Responsibility
Compliance / Risk manages background screening, regulatory reference checks and sanctions / PEP screening for all new hires.

## In this process
Initiates background and right-to-work checks via HireRight at T-10 (ps-3) and is consulted at the T-1 go/no-go check (ps-12). Executes control CTL-NHO-01. On adverse findings, escalates alongside the HRBP and pauses provisioning.
