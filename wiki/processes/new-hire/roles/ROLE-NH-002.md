## Responsibility
The Hiring Manager is responsible for defining the role profile, approving the equipment list, running first-week induction, and conducting the 30-, 60- and 90-day check-ins with the new hire.

## In this process
Confirms the equipment list pre-boarding (ps-5). On Day 1, runs the role expectations 1:1 (ps-16) and assigns a buddy (ps-21). Conducts the 30-day (ps-25) and 60-day (ps-27) check-ins and is jointly accountable for the 90-day probation review (ps-28).
