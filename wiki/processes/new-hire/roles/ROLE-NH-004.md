## Responsibility
IT Service Desk provisions employee accounts, devices and access entitlements according to the role template defined by the Hiring Manager.

## In this process
Provisions the Okta account and role-based access at T-3 (ps-8) and ships the MDM-enrolled device at T-2 (ps-9). On Day 1, supports first login and MFA enrolment (ps-15). During Week 1, validates system access and handles exceptions (ps-23).
