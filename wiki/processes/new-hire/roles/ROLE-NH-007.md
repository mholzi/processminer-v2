## Responsibility
Payroll sets up the payroll record, processes tax forms and benefits deductions, and confirms first pay cycle alignment for each new hire.

## In this process
During Week 1, Payroll confirms the payroll record is active and verifies alignment with the first pay cycle by Day 5 (ps-22).
