## Responsibility
HR Operations owns the end-to-end orchestration of the onboarding process — from triggering the workflow on offer acceptance through to closing the onboarding ticket at the end of probation.

## In this process
HR Ops triggers the Workday workflow (ps-1), collects documents and creates the employee record (ps-7), and sends the Day-1 logistics email (ps-11). On Day 1, HR Ops greets the new hire and completes right-to-work verification (ps-13). HR Ops closes the onboarding ticket at Day 95 (ps-29).
