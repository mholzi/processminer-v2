## Responsibility
The HR Business Partner signs off compensation and benefits enrolment, approves exceptions to the standard onboarding flow, and is jointly accountable for the probation review decision.

## In this process
Consulted on contract and compensation matters (ps-4) and jointly accountable for the 90-day probation review (ps-28). In exception cases, the HRBP handles adverse background findings and approves expedited flows for late contract signatures.
