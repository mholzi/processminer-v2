## Responsibility
Facilities allocates the desk and locker, issues the building pass, and arranges parking where applicable.

## In this process
During pre-boarding, Facilities confirms the desk assignment, building pass and parking by T-2 (ps-10), working from the Facilities sub-ticket raised by HR Ops in ServiceNow. On Day 1, Facilities issues the physical building pass and completes the health-and-safety walk-through with the new hire (ps-14).
