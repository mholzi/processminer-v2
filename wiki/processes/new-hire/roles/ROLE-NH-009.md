## Responsibility
The New Hire is responsible for providing required documentation, completing all pre-boarding tasks, attending induction sessions and completing mandatory training within the required timelines.

## In this process
Provides ID and banking details pre-boarding (ps-4). On Day 1, completes first login and acknowledges the policy pack (ps-15, ps-18). During Week 1, completes Tier 1 training by Day 5 (ps-19) and validates system access (ps-23).
