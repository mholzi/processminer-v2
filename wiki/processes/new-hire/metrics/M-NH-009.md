## Definition
The percentage of new hires who complete all Tier 1 mandatory training modules (Code of Conduct, Information Security, Data Privacy) in the LMS by the end of Day 5 of employment.

## Current reading
No measured actual is available. The document sets a 100% target; LMS completion data against this target is not reported in the source document.

## Why it matters
Tier 1 completion is a documented control gate: per CTL-NHO-02, production system access is blocked until all modules are marked complete in the LMS. Any shortfall means hires cannot work or the control is bypassed.
