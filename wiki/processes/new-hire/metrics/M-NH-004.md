## Definition
The elapsed working days from opening the onboarding ticket in ServiceNow to closing and archiving it at Day 95 (step 5.3), measured end-to-end per hire.

## Current reading
No measured actual is available. The ≤ 12 working days figure is the target stated in the process design document; actual cycle times are not reported.

## Why it matters
Cycle time is an operational efficiency signal for the onboarding process. Breaches may indicate bottlenecks in IT provisioning, background screening, contract collection or manager check-ins.
