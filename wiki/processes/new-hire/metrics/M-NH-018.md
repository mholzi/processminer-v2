## Definition
The Net Promoter Score collected from new hires via the HR onboarding pulse survey at Day 30 (step 4.3). The document does not describe the NPS calculation methodology or the survey questions used.

## Current reading
No measured actual is available. The target of ≥ +40 is set in the process design document; no historical NPS scores are reported.

## Why it matters
The 30-day NPS provides an early signal on new-hire experience. A score below +40 gives HR Operations and Hiring Managers time to intervene before the 90-day probation window closes.
