## What happens
Applications with standard guarantee wording proceed directly to the next step without additional review. Applications with bespoke wording are sent to the Legal team, who review the guarantee text and provide sign-off before issuance.

## Inputs
- Application with confirmed facility availability
- Guarantee wording (standard template or client-proposed bespoke text)

## Outputs
- Approved guarantee wording (standard: implicit; bespoke: Legal sign-off record)
- Application cleared to proceed to sanctions screening

## Why it matters
Legal review of bespoke wording ensures the instrument is acceptable before issuance is committed.
