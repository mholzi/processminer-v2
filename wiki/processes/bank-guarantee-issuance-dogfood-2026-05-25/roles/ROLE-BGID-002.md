## Responsibility
Accountable for issuance approval — no guarantee is issued without the Trade Finance Manager's recorded sign-off.

## In this process
Reviews the assembled guarantee package and approves issuance at the issuance approval step (ps-5), recording the decision in the Trade Finance System. Acts as the four-eyes control owner for guarantee issuance.
