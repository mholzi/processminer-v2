## Responsibility
Reviews and provides sign-off on bespoke guarantee wording before issuance.

## In this process
Engaged exclusively at the wording review step (ps-3) for bespoke-wording guarantees. Receives the draft wording, reviews it and provides sign-off. Standard-wording guarantees bypass this step.
