## Responsibility
Responsible for the operational execution of the guarantee issuance process — intake, checks, generation and delivery of guarantee instruments.

## In this process
Receives and validates the guarantee application at intake (ps-1), confirms the client's facility limit is sufficient at the credit and facility check (ps-2), then generates the guarantee instrument in the Trade Finance System and the guarantee is transmitted to the beneficiary's bank via SWIFT at generation and delivery (ps-6).
