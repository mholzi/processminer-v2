## Responsibility
Owns sanctions and compliance screening for all guarantee applications.

## In this process
Screens the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country against the sanctions list using the Sanctions Screening Tool at the sanctions and compliance screening step (ps-4). Attaches the screening result to the application; a screening hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.
