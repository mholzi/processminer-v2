## Responsibility
Handles limit increases when a client's guarantee facility is insufficient for a new application.

## In this process
Consulted at the credit and facility check step (ps-2) when the client's available facility limit is insufficient to cover the requested guarantee amount. The application is parked and routed to the Credit Team; this is the most common reason for delay.
