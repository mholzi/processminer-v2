## Description
A web portal through which corporate clients submit Bank Guarantee applications to the bank.

## Usage
An intake channel for Bank Guarantee applications; clients submit application details — beneficiary, amount, currency, wording type and validity period — through this portal, after which the Trade Finance Officer reviews the application.
