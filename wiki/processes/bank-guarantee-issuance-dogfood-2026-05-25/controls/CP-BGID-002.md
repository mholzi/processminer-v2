## What it checks
That the guarantee beneficiary and the beneficiary's country are not subject to active sanctions, and that the screening result is on record before issuance proceeds.

## Control activity
The Compliance Analyst screens the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country against the sanctions list using the Sanctions Screening Tool, and attaches the screening outcome to the application. A screening hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.

## Risk addressed
Without screening, the bank risks issuing a guarantee to a sanctioned party, constituting a regulatory breach that could attract substantial fines and reputational damage.

## Timing
Runs once per guarantee application at the sanctions and compliance screening step, before issuance approval is sought.
