## Purpose
Network used for transmission of the executed guarantee to the beneficiary's bank.

## Role in this process
Used in the Guarantee Generation and Delivery step (ps-6). Once the guarantee instrument is generated in the Trade Finance System, it is transmitted to the beneficiary's bank via SWIFT.
