## Purpose
Tool used by Compliance for beneficiary screening against the sanctions list.

## Role in this process
Used by the Compliance Analyst in the Sanctions and Compliance Screening step (ps-4). The beneficiary and beneficiary's country are screened; the screening result is attached to the application before issuance approval.
