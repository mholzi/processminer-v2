## What it requires
Where a guarantee is issued on demand terms, it must conform to the ICC Uniform Rules for Demand Guarantees (URDG 758).

## Why it applies
The bank issues bank guarantees on behalf of corporate clients in favour of a third-party beneficiary; where those instruments are issued on demand terms they fall within the scope of URDG 758.

## How it is met
The document does not specify how URDG 758 conformance is ensured; this remains to be confirmed with the process owner.
