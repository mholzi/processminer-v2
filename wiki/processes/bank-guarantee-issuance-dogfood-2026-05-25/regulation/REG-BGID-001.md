## What it requires
The process must comply with anti-money-laundering obligations under EU AML directives and with EU sanctions regulations, including screening the beneficiary and the beneficiary's country against the sanctions list before issuing a guarantee.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantee issuance involves corporate clients and third-party beneficiaries, making it subject to EU AML directives and sanctions regulations.

## How it is met
The Compliance Analyst screens every beneficiary and the beneficiary's country against the sanctions list before issuance, and the screening result is attached to the application as required by control C2.
