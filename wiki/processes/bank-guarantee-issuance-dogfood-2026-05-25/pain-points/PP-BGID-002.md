## Description
Bespoke guarantee wording adds unpredictable delay to the issuance process because the Legal team's review has no committed turnaround time, leaving the application in an indeterminate waiting state.

## Impact
Bespoke-wording applications are excluded from the 3-business-day SLA (which is measured only for standard-wording guarantees with no screening hit), leaving no reliable cycle-time commitment for clients requesting bespoke terms.

## Root cause
Legal review has no committed turnaround for bespoke guarantee wording, creating an open-ended hand-off with no defined SLA to drive resolution.
