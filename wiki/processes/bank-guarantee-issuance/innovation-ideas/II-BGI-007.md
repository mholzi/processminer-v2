## The idea
Define a formal deputy approval policy for TFM absences and configure the Trade Finance System to route applications to a designated deputy when the primary TFM is unavailable, with a named escalation path for Head of Trade Finance sign-off above EUR 5 million.

## Expected benefit
Eliminates the approval bottleneck when the TFM or Head of Trade Finance is unavailable, preventing SLA breaches caused by a single point of authority with no documented cover.

## Feasibility
Primarily a governance and system-routing change within the Trade Finance System's existing approval workflow. Low complexity; the key dependency is agreeing the delegation policy with senior management.
