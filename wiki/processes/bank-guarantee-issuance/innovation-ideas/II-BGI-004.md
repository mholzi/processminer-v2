## The idea
Implement automated monitoring of guarantee facility utilisation that flags clients and relationship managers when available headroom falls below a configurable threshold, shifting credit-limit management from reactive to proactive.

## Expected benefit
Reduces applications stalling at the credit check by ensuring clients pre-arrange sufficient limit before submitting — directly addressing the most common cause of SLA breach.

## Feasibility
Low technical complexity — the Trade Finance System already holds utilisation data; the change is threshold monitoring and notification routing, with no core system rebuild required.
