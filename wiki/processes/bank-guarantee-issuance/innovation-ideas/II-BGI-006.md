## The idea
Establish a formal SLA between Trade Finance and the Legal desk for bespoke wording reviews, with a digital workflow tracking submission, acknowledgement and sign-off dates — with automatic escalation when the SLA is at risk.

## Expected benefit
Gives Trade Finance and clients a reliable delivery commitment for bespoke guarantees, reducing commercial exposure from missed tender deadlines and unknown turnaround times.

## Feasibility
Primarily a governance and workflow change — requires SLA agreement between Trade Finance and Legal, and a lightweight ticket-tracking integration. Low technical complexity.
