## The idea
Deploy an agentic AI validator at the Corporate Portal intake step that checks every submitted application for completeness — required fields, valid BIC format, wording-type consistency — before the TFO sees it, and returns an actionable exception list to the client for immediate correction.

## Expected benefit
Eliminates the TFO's time spent chasing incomplete submissions and closes the system-enforced completeness control gap, accelerating hand-off to the credit and wording review steps.

## Feasibility
Requires integration between the Corporate Portal and an AI validation service; field-level rules are well-defined. Oracle's Application Validator Agent for bank guarantees demonstrates the pattern is productionisable.
