## The idea
Replace or augment the current string-match Sanctions Screening Tool with an NLP-based engine that incorporates beneficiary country, industry context and historical analyst decisions to disambiguate common beneficiary names from genuine sanctions hits.

## Expected benefit
Reduces the Compliance Analyst's false-positive investigation burden, cutting avoidable delay at sanctions screening for applications that are clearly not sanctions-related.

## Feasibility
AI sanctions screening vendors offer plug-in NLP engines compatible with existing systems; the critical dependency is compliance function validation and a calibration period before go-live.
