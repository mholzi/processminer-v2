## What it requires
The notional of each demand guarantee counts as an exposure to the applicant client under CRR Article 390 and must not cause the client group's total exposure to exceed 25% of the bank's Tier 1 capital (CRR Article 395).

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank is subject to CRR large exposure rules enforced by the ECB. Every guarantee issued increases total exposure to the applying corporate client, which must remain below the 25% cap.

## How it is met
The facility limit check (CP-BGI-003) ensures the guarantee amount falls within the client's approved facility, which Credit sets with large exposure limits in view. Full mapping to be confirmed with the SME.
