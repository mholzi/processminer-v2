## What it requires
KYC and beneficiary personal data must not be kept longer than necessary (storage limitation, Article 5(1)(e)); the lawful basis for processing is legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)); the right to erasure is overridden during statutory AML and commercial retention periods (Article 17(3)(b)).

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank collects beneficiary names, addresses and other personal data during guarantee issuance, making it a data controller subject to GDPR. KYC data collected under GwG is processed under Article 6(1)(c).

## How it is met
KYC and guarantee records are retained under GwG (5 years), HGB §257 and AO §147 (6–10 years); beneficiary personal data is held for the longer applicable period. Controls to be confirmed with the SME.
