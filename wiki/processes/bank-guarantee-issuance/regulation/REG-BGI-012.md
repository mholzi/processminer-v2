## What it requires
Institutions offering trade finance products — including bank guarantees — must apply enhanced due diligence on complex transactions, monitor for TBML red flags at intake, and document the commercial rationale for the underlying trade.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantees are explicitly named in FATF TBML guidance as instruments susceptible to trade-based money laundering. The issuance process — from intake to beneficiary screening — is directly in scope for TBML red-flag monitoring.

## How it is met
Beneficiary screening at step 4 (CP-BGI-002) covers the primary due diligence check. TBML red-flag monitoring at intake and commercial rationale documentation to be confirmed with the SME.
