## What it requires
Credit institutions must obtain and review a borrower's financial statements before extending credit — including guarantees — above the materiality threshold. Under §21(1) KWG, bank guarantees (Garantien) are credits for this purpose.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantees create contingent credit exposure for the issuing bank; §21(1) KWG expressly classifies guarantees as credits, bringing material guarantee applications above the §18 threshold within the credit documentation regime.

## How it is met
Step 2 credit check references the client's approved guarantee facility, established against reviewed financial information by the Credit team. Specific §18 threshold application to be confirmed with the SME.
