## What it requires
Demand guarantees having the character of credit substitutes must be assigned a 100% credit conversion factor (CCF) under CRR Annex I; the resulting risk-weighted exposure value must be included in capital adequacy calculations.

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank is a European credit institution subject to CRR 575/2013 as supervised by the ECB and BaFin. Each demand guarantee issued creates an off-balance-sheet contingent credit exposure requiring full risk-weighted asset treatment.

## How it is met
The guarantee notional is recorded in the Trade Finance System and feeds into capital reporting; issuance approval (CP-BGI-001) confirms terms are within delegated authority. Capital treatment classification to be confirmed with the SME.
