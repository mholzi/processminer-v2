## What it requires
Demand guarantees issued under URDG 758 must conform to ICC rules.

## Why it applies
URDG 758 is the default framework for all demand guarantees issued by this process; it is not a per-client election. ISP98 standby instruments are out of scope, covered by a separate Standby LC process.

## How it is met
Approved standard-wording templates were pre-vetted by Legal for URDG 758 conformance at template approval and are used directly. Bespoke wording is validated per-case by the Legal team at step 3 before issuance.
