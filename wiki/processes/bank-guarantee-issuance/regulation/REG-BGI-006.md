## What it requires
Guarantee instruments and booking documents (Buchungsbelege) must be retained for 10 years from year-end; commercial correspondence (applications, confirmations) must be retained for 6 years. Retention commences at the end of the calendar year of the last entry or document.

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank is a German credit institution and Kaufmann subject to HGB §257 and AO §147. Guarantee instruments are Buchungsbelege and commercial records; both frameworks apply in parallel.

## How it is met
Guarantee instruments and approval records are stored in the Trade Finance System; archiving schedules and retention controls to be confirmed with the SME.
