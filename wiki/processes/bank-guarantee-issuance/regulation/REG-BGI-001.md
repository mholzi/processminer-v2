## What it requires
Beneficiaries must be screened against sanctions lists and AML-checked before issuance, under AMLD6 (EU Directive 2018/1673 and the 2024 AMLR package), the German Geldwäschegesetz (GwG), EU Council Regulation 833/2014 (Russia/Belarus sanctions), and the EU consolidated sanctions list framework.

## Why it applies
The bank is a German credit institution subject to AMLD6 as transposed by the GwG, and to EU sanctions regulations including Council Regulation 833/2014 and the EU consolidated list framework. These obligations apply to guarantee issuance as a trade finance product.

## How it is met
Step 4 sanctions screening: the Sanctions Screening Tool automatically writes the result to the application record; the Compliance Analyst investigates hits. Serious matches trigger a suspicious-transaction report from the MLRO to BaFin and the German FIU under GwG.
