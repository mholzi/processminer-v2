## What it requires
Significant institutions must maintain a documented framework for delegating approval authority, allocate management body oversight of material transactions including contingent liabilities, and uphold three lines of defence (Section 6, Guidelines 38–40).

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank is a significant institution under CRD, directly subject to EBA/GL/2021/05. The guarantee issuance approval hierarchy — TFM below EUR 5 million, Head of Trade Finance above — must sit within a documented delegation framework.

## How it is met
CP-BGI-001 records the delegated authority caps and dual-tier approval chain; the EUR 5 million co-sign threshold implements a documented escalation. Formal delegation framework documentation to be confirmed with the SME.
