## The decision
Document a formal deputy approval policy for TFM and Head of Trade Finance absences and configure the Trade Finance System to automatically route applications to the designated deputy when the primary approver is unavailable.

## Options considered
- Formal deputy policy with system-enforced routing to designated deputy
- Ad hoc escalation to Head of Trade Finance on TFM absence (current state)
- Expand TFM headcount to reduce single-point approval dependency

## Rationale
A policy plus system routing requires no headcount change and directly eliminates the approval queue stall. Ad hoc escalation is the current state and the documented cause of PP-BGI-005; headcount expansion addresses the symptom and carries higher cost and longer lead time.
