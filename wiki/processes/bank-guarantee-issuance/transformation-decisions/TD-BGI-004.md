## The decision
Establish a formal SLA between Trade Finance and the Legal desk for bespoke wording reviews, with a lightweight digital workflow in the Trade Finance System tracking submission, acknowledgement and sign-off dates and auto-escalating on SLA breach.

## Options considered
- Formal SLA with digital workflow tracking and automatic escalation on breach
- Informal SLA agreement with manual TFM escalation as today
- Dedicated Legal resource embedded in Trade Finance for bespoke cases

## Rationale
The current informal arrangement directly causes PP-BGI-002. A formal SLA with tracking creates accountability. An embedded Legal resource would reduce latency but is a higher-cost structural change better treated as a future option after the SLA baseline is established.
