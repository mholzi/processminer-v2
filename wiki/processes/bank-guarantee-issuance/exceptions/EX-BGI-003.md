## Description
The client requests bespoke guarantee wording rather than the bank's standard template, triggering a mandatory Legal review before issuance can proceed.

## Handling
Routed to the Legal team, which may approve the wording, redline it, or propose amendments. Technical negotiation runs TFO ↔ Legal directly; client-facing changes loop back via the relationship manager. Once sign-off is reached, the TFO manually advances to step 4. Informal target: 2 business days; overdue reviews escalate TFM → Trade Finance Legal desk lead.

## Impact
Adds unpredictable delay; Legal review has no committed turnaround time, so the standard 3-business-day SLA does not apply to bespoke-wording applications.
