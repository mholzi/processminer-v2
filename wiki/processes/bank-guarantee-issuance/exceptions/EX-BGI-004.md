## Description
The application arrives at intake with missing or insufficient information — commonly missing beneficiary bank SWIFT details, wrong currency, or vague wording-type instructions.

## Handling
The TFO holds the application and contacts the client through their relationship manager to obtain the missing detail. The application does not proceed until the required information is supplied; applications are never rejected outright.

## Impact
Delays the start of processing; the TFO loses real time chasing missing details from clients.
