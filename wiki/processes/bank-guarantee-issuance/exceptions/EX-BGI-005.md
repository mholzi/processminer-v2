## Description
At the issuance approval step, the Trade Finance Manager decides the application cannot proceed as submitted — either declining it outright or returning it to the TFO for rework due to unacceptable wording risk, incorrect pricing, or a terms issue.

## Handling
For a return, the TFM records the reason in the Trade Finance System and routes the application back to the TFO, who corrects the identified issue and resubmits for approval. For an outright decline, the application is closed and the client notified via the relationship manager.

## Impact
Delays issuance; a return adds at least one full approval cycle. An outright decline terminates the application and requires the client to submit a revised request if they wish to proceed.
