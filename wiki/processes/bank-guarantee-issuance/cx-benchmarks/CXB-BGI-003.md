## The benchmark
Industry analysis from EY and Coherent Market Insights indicates that digitising trade finance document workflows — including guarantee applications and processing — can reduce transaction processing times by up to 75% and could boost bank trade revenues by up to 20% within five years.

## Relevance
Applying a 75% reduction to the current 3-business-day standard guarantee SLA implies a viable benchmark of under one business day for digital-native processing. HSBC's STP capability already demonstrates this is achievable today, making the 3-day SLA look like a structural ceiling rather than a competitive target.

## Evidence
Coherent Market Insights' 2025 trade finance transformation report cited a 75% processing time reduction potential from digital adoption; EY's global trade finance analysis corroborated the range and the 20% revenue uplift estimate.
