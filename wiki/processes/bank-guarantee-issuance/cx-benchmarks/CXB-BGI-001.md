## The benchmark
ICC and Swift published the first industry-wide API specifications for bank guarantees and standby letters of credit in August 2024, establishing real-time, ERP-integrated guarantee initiation as the new structural standard. Corporates connected to a compliant bank can now instruct guarantees from their own systems without manual re-keying, portal submission, or email.

## Relevance
As API-compliant banks (BNP Paribas, Standard Chartered) go live, corporate clients gain a comparison point: same-session, zero-rekeying guarantee initiation. Deutsche Bank's current portal-and-RM channel will look like an extra manual layer to any corporate already connected to a peer offering ICC-Swift API initiation.

## Evidence
ICC and Swift published the standards in August 2024. Standard Chartered completed the first live commercial transaction in January 2026; BNP Paribas piloted the same standard with Vanderlande in November 2024.
