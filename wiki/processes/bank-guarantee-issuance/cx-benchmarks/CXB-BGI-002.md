## The benchmark
The 2024 CGI-BAFT Trade Technology Survey found that 90% of banks are actively investing in digital client-experience improvements for trade finance, with corporate clients expecting end-to-end digital services without delay or manual intervention. Intelligent process automation — AI and machine learning — led investment priorities, signalling that automation is now baseline, not premium.

## Relevance
For bank guarantee issuance, the 90% investment figure defines the competitive floor: any corporate interacting with a digitally mature peer will benchmark channel transparency, submission completeness guidance, and status visibility against a sector that overwhelmingly treats these as hygiene rather than differentiators.

## Evidence
CGI-BAFT 2024 survey of over 100 global banking respondents; 80% stated trade digitisation depends on full ecosystem collaboration — corporates, banks, shipping, and regulators — signalling shared responsibility for data quality at intake.
