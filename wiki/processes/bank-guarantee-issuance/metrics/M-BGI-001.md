## Definition
Time from receipt of a complete application to guarantee delivery, measured for the clean-path subset only: standard wording, no screening hit and no facility shortfall.

## Current reading
Target: 5 business days (revised from 3; the previous target was unrealistic once the mandatory collateral confirmation step was included). Actual run-rate: ~4–5 business days. Bespoke and credit-shortfall applications excluded; no end-to-end metric exists.

## Why it matters
A client-commitment metric: corporate clients rely on it for tender deadlines and contractual obligations; missing it has direct client-relationship consequences. Also the headline competitive metric for trade finance speed vs other corporate banks.
