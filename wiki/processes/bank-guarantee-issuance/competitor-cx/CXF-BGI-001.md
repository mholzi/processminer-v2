## The journey
Corporate clients submit bank guarantee requests through Komgo's GTK web portal, where embedded AI pre-validates guarantee wording against internal policies and bank preferences before submission. Routine low-risk guarantees are auto-processed via Boost Approval; non-standard wordings are escalated to treasury. Real-time status notifications and a multi-bank dashboard give clients continuous visibility.

## Relevance
A corporate using Komgo experiences issuance turnaround 50% faster than a traditional bank channel, with 70% of routine guarantees requiring no manual back-and-forth. Deutsche Bank's bespoke-wording bottleneck — where Legal review adds to a multi-day queue — is exactly the gap Komgo's AI text-validation closes for clients who can choose platforms.

## Evidence
TK Elevator (12,000+ guarantees/year across 80 countries) cut average issuance time by 50% with 70% of new guarantees processed automatically through Komgo GTK's Boost Approval feature.
