## The journey
Corporate clients initiate standby letters of credit and guarantees through J.P. Morgan's Trade Channel platform — a self-service web portal handling application submission through to transaction status inquiry. For global corporates, JPMorgan facilitates local issuance and re-issuance across its international network, eliminating bilateral bank relationships in each jurisdiction.

## Relevance
JPMorgan's recognised #1 digital trade ranking in 2024 and its integrated local-issuance network means global corporates comparing guarantee channels will see a self-service, multi-jurisdiction offering against which fragmented or RM-dependent initiation flows are a clear step down in experience and control.

## Evidence
J.P. Morgan ranked #1 in Trade in the 2024 Coalition Greenwich Digital Transformation Benchmarking Study and was awarded Best Digital Asset Capabilities for Trade Finance by World's Best Trade Finance Providers 2025.
