## The journey
Large corporates connect their ERP or treasury system to Standard Chartered's guarantee workflow via Komgo's Konsole platform, using ICC-Swift API standards. Guarantee instructions flow API-first from the corporate into the bank, automating instruction receipt and triggering digitally initiated issuance — eliminating manual re-keying and the paper application step entirely.

## Relevance
API-native initiation raises the bar beyond portal self-service: corporates that integrate once gain straight-through instruction flow for every subsequent guarantee, making manual application submission and data re-entry that still drives rework in our process look architecturally outdated to sophisticated global clients.

## Evidence
Standard Chartered executed its first live ICC-Swift API-standard guarantee transaction for a global energy company through Komgo on 15 January 2026, confirming it is among the first banks to commercialise the ICC-Swift API standards.
