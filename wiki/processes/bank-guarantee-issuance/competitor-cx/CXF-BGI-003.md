## The journey
Corporate clients access a self-service Trade Portal to apply for guarantees, track status, and manage documents across LCs, guarantees, and collections in a single front-to-back workflow. An in-built GenAI assistant (Assist.AI) supports documentation, compliance checks, and guarantee wording queries in real time, while OCR and NLP automate document handling on the bank's back end.

## Relevance
Finastra's unified portal gives corporate clients the self-service visibility and real-time status tracking that Deutsche Bank's channel currently lacks — directly addressing the unpredictable Legal-review wait and incomplete-application rework. Banks running Finastra Trade Innovation can offer a single portal for all trade instruments, raising the bar for transparency and speed.

## Evidence
ODDO BHF cited Finastra Trade Innovation's flexibility to increase automation in processing workflows for letters of credit and guarantees. Finastra won Best-in-Class Trade Finance Platform at IBSi Global FinTech Innovation Awards 2025 for its Maybank deployment.
