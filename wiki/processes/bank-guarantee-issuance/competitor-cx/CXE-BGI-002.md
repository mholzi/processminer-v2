## The journey
Corporate clients access UniCredit's Trade Finance Gate (TFG) through the UniCredit Corporate Portal to submit, amend, and monitor guarantees and letters of credit across 18 countries from a single interface. By September 2025 TFG adoption reached over 81% of eligible transactions, with OCR-assisted LC availments cutting processing time by 50%.

## Relevance
UniCredit's cross-border single-portal model means a corporate with European subsidiaries can manage its entire guarantee portfolio centrally. Deutsche Bank's multi-country experience may feel fragmented by comparison, creating a retention risk for multi-market corporates.

## Evidence
Global Finance awarded UniCredit Best Trade Finance Bank in Western Europe, citing 80% core-process harmonisation across 18 countries in six months and TFG adoption exceeding 81% by September 2025.
