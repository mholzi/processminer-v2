## The journey
Corporate clients use BNP Paribas's Centric platform to initiate, amend, monitor, and release bank guarantees from a single web interface alongside cash management, FX, and supply chain finance. The platform includes AI-powered client assistance and deep reporting, replacing email and PDF form submissions for guarantee applications.

## Relevance
Centric bundles guarantee management into the same session as payments and FX, reducing context-switching for corporate treasury teams. Clients who experience this integrated workflow will view siloed guarantee portals or email-to-operations channels as a significant usability gap.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas's Centric product page states the platform enables online management of guarantee lifecycles — issue, monitor, amend, and release bank guarantees with an intuitive interface — across all trade finance instruments in one place.
