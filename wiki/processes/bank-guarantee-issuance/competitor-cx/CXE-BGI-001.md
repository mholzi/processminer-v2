## The journey
A corporate client (Dutch logistics company Vanderlande) submitted a guarantee application through Surecomp's Rivo platform, which transmitted it to BNP Paribas via the SWIFT–ICC open API standard in real time, with no manual rekeying or MT798 messaging required. The pilot validated a low-cost ISO 20022-compatible channel requiring no SWIFT connectivity on the corporate side.

## Relevance
BNP Paribas is demonstrating an API-native guarantee channel that removes the manual submission step entirely. If scaled, corporates submitting to Deutsche Bank via email or PDF-form would experience that workflow as outdated friction by comparison, opening a channel and speed gap.

## Evidence
GTR reported on 20 Nov 2024 that BNP Paribas called the pilot 'the beginning of a new and very promising journey in the digitalisation of bank guarantees' and invited all trade finance stakeholders to join.
