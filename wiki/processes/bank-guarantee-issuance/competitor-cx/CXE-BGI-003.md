## The journey
Commerzbank is replacing its legacy trade finance back office with Surecomp's DOKA-NG, hosted in its private cloud, to unify processing of guarantees, standby letters of credit, and documentary credits in a single system. The upgrade is designed to expedite client approvals, reduce operating costs, and improve real-time visibility into transaction status.

## Relevance
Faster back-office processing directly shortens the guarantee issuance cycle for corporate clients. If Commerzbank reduces internal cycle time, its turnaround promise to corporates improves — increasing pressure on Deutsche Bank to match or explain any remaining delays in credit review or legal wording steps.

## Evidence
FinTech Futures reported in February 2024 that Commerzbank selected DOKA-NG to future-proof trade finance. Commerzbank ranked No. 1 in Corporate Banking Digitalisation in the FINANCE Banken Survey 2024 and Market Leader in Technology in the Euromoney Trade Finance Survey 2025.
