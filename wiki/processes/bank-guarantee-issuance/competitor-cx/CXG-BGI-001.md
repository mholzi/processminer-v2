## The journey
Corporate clients submit guarantee applications via HSBC Trade Solutions, a fully digital trade finance platform. The portal pre-populates client data, automates validation checks, and routes the guarantee straight through to beneficiary notification — reducing issuance from the typical 3–4 days to minutes with zero human intervention on eligible standard-wording transactions.

## Relevance
HSBC's STP capability directly narrows the window Deutsche Bank must close: clients who routinely wait 2–5 days for credit-limit-dependent or bespoke-wording guarantees will increasingly benchmark against a peer that delivers standard guarantees in the same session, making manual touchpoints a visible experience gap.

## Evidence
HSBC processed the world's first STP bank guarantee for KPMG Qatar in December 2023 — zero human intervention and in a matter of minutes — via HSBC Trade Solutions, with global rollout across 19+ additional markets planned for 2024–25.
