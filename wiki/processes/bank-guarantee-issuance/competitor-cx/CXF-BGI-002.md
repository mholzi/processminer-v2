## The journey
Corporate clients connected to Komgo's Konsole API submit guarantee instructions directly from their ERP or treasury system. The API, fully compliant with ICC-Swift industry standards for bank guarantees, automates receipt of instructions at the bank and accelerates issuance — eliminating manual re-keying and email relay that define traditional bank channels.

## Relevance
When a corporate's system sends a guarantee instruction via Komgo's API, the bank receives a structured, pre-validated message rather than a PDF or email — removing the primary source of incomplete-application rework. A Deutsche Bank client using this model would bypass the data-entry delay cycle entirely for routine issuances.

## Evidence
Standard Chartered completed the first live ICC-Swift API-standard bank guarantee transaction through Komgo's Konsole for a global energy company in January 2026, offering clients greater speed, transparency and reliability.
