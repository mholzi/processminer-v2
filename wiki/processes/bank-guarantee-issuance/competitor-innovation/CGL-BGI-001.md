## The move
Standard Chartered completed the first production ICC-SWIFT API-standard bank guarantee transaction via Komgo's Konsole in January 2026 — automating instruction receipt and accelerating issuance for a global energy company, with rollout planned across all group markets.

## Relevance
Standard Chartered is among the first global banks with a fully interoperable digital guarantee workflow; the competitive gap widens for banks still reliant on manual SWIFT MT798 processes.

## Evidence
The January 2026 transaction was described as 'a significant step towards a fully interoperable and integrated digital bank guarantee ecosystem'; Standard Chartered plans progressive rollout to all group markets.
