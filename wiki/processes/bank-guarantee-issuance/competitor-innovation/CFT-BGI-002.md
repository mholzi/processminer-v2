## The move
Komgo's Konsole platform enables corporate clients to submit bank guarantee requests digitally to multiple banks in real time via ICC-SWIFT API standards, removing manual form completion and separate bank portal dependencies.

## Relevance
Banks not connected to Komgo risk losing corporate clients who can compare speed and pricing across Komgo-connected counterparts — creating disintermediation pressure for traditional manual-workflow competitors.

## Evidence
Komgo's Konsole was used for Standard Chartered's first live ICC-SWIFT API bank guarantee transaction in January 2026, cited as evidence of a maturing multi-bank digital ecosystem.
