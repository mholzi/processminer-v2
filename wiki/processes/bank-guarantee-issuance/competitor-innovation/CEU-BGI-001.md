## The move
BNP Paribas, with Surecomp and corporate client Vanderlande, completed the first trade finance guarantee processed via the ICC-SWIFT API standard in November 2024, enabling real-time digital guarantee requests without SWIFT messaging platform connectivity.

## Relevance
If BNP Paribas productionises API-based guarantee issuance, it can offer faster and lower-cost delivery — a service-level capability that banks relying on manual SWIFT MT798 workflows cannot match.

## Evidence
Surecomp's Rivo platform transmitted a guarantee request to BNP Paribas in real time for Vanderlande without manual intervention; BNP Paribas called it a significant industry milestone.
