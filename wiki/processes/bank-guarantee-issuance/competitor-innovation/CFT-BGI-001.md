## The move
Surecomp's RIVO and DOKA-NG suite offers banks a Trade Finance-as-a-Service stack with ICC-SWIFT API-driven guarantee origination, issuance and settlement — connecting corporates to banks without SWIFT messaging connectivity costs.

## Relevance
Banks adopting Surecomp gain automated intake-to-issuance guarantee workflows; the gap for banks with manual processes is widening as API-native platforms drive down cost and turnaround.

## Evidence
Surecomp processed the first ICC-SWIFT API guarantee with BNP Paribas and Vanderlande in November 2024; Crown Agents Bank selected Surecomp's TF-as-a-Service solution in 2025.
