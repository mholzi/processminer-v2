## The gap
No formal deputy approval policy exists for TFM or Head of Trade Finance absences. TD-6 requires a senior management governance decision to document the delegation framework and configure system routing in the TFS.

## Resolution
Requires a senior management decision to document the delegation policy, designate named deputies at both TFM and Head of Trade Finance levels, and configure approval routing in the TFS. Until the policy is agreed, PP-BGI-005 remains an active SLA risk at every TFM absence.

## Status
Open. Governance decision not yet taken; senior management alignment required before system routing can be configured.
