## What it checks
That cash collateral has been received and blocked before issuance proceeds for any guarantee not fully covered by the client's approved facility.

## Control activity
The Trade Finance Officer confirms collateral receipt and blocking before the application can advance to issuance approval. Issuance cannot proceed without this confirmation for partially-secured guarantees.

## Risk addressed
Issuance of a guarantee without confirmed collateral cover when the guarantee is not fully covered by the client's approved facility.

## Timing
Performed at the collateral confirmation step for every partially-secured guarantee, prior to issuance approval.
