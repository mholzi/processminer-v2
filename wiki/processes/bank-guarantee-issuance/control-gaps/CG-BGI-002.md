## The gap
There is no system-enforced control ensuring the TFO selects the correct approved template for standard-wording guarantees; template selection is a manual judgement call with no conformance check.

## Risk
A wording type mis-designated at intake can cause a non-standard case to slip through as standard, bypassing Legal review and resulting in a guarantee issued on unapproved wording.

## Remediation
Consider a template-selection validation in the Trade Finance System that cross-checks the wording type field against the selected template and flags any mismatch before the application advances.
