## The gap
There is no system-enforced completeness check at application intake; completeness is assessed by the Trade Finance Officer as a manual judgement call.

## Risk
Incomplete applications may not be caught at intake, slipping into later steps and causing rework when the deficiency surfaces at credit check or wording review.

## Remediation
Consider adding field-level validation in the Corporate Portal to enforce mandatory fields at submission.
