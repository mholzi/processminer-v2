frequency: On demand — typically several applications per business day across the corporate client book.
scopeIn: Issuance of a Bank Guarantee (BG) on behalf of a corporate client in favour of a third-party beneficiary.
scopeOut: Guarantee amendments, claims and cancellations; counter-guarantee issuance (where the bank backs another bank's guarantee) — all covered by separate processes.
processInput: Bank Guarantee application submitted via Corporate Portal, including beneficiary details, guarantee amount, currency, wording type, validity period and the underlying commercial contract reference.
processOutput: Executed guarantee instrument transmitted to the beneficiary's bank via SWIFT; client's guarantee facility utilisation updated.
