## Responsibility
Provides mandatory co-approval sign-off for bank guarantees above EUR 5 million; the Deputy Head of Trade Finance holds delegation to sign in their absence.

## In this process
Mandatory co-approver at step 5 for any guarantee exceeding EUR 5 million — the Trade Finance System enforces the sign-off and the TFM cannot finalise issuance alone above this threshold. In the Head of Trade Finance's absence, the Deputy Head of Trade Finance holds delegation to sign in their place.
