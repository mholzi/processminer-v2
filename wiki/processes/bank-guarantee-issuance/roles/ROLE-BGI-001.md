## Responsibility
Performs application intake, credit and facility checks, wording review initiation, and guarantee generation and delivery.

## In this process
Handles steps 1, 2, 3, and 6 — receives the client application, checks completeness and facility limit, routes bespoke wording to Legal, generates and transmits the executed guarantee instrument, and performs the manual facility-utilisation update after SWIFT transmission.
