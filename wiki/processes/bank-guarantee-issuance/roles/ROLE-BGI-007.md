## Responsibility
Holds accountability for the sanctions screening control and makes escalation decisions on borderline screening hits referred by the Compliance Analyst.

## In this process
Accountable for step 4 (sanctions and compliance screening). Decides the outcome of borderline hits escalated by the Compliance Analyst; serious matches (sanctioned entity or country watch-list) are escalated further to Group Compliance / MLRO.
