## Responsibility
Screens beneficiaries against the sanctions list and investigates screening hits. Routine clears are the Analyst's decision; borderline hits escalate to the Compliance Officer; serious matches (sanctioned entity or country watch-list) escalate to Group Compliance / MLRO.

## In this process
Performs sanctions and compliance screening at step 4; the Sanctions Screening Tool automatically writes the result to the application record. Investigates suspended applications: routine clears decided by the Analyst, borderline hits escalated to the Compliance Officer, serious matches to Group Compliance / MLRO.
