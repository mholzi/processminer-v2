## Responsibility
Assesses and resolves guarantee facility limit shortfalls referred from the issuance process: may approve a temporary limit increase, a permanent facility amendment, or decline the request. No formal SLA; typical resolution is 2–5 business days, with larger cases requiring Credit Committee approval taking longer.

## In this process
Handles EX-BGI-001 — insufficient facility limit referrals at step 2. Can approve a temporary limit increase, a permanent facility amendment, or decline. Once resolved or declined, the TFO manually re-queues the application; resolution is not automatic. No formal SLA: typical 2–5 business days, larger cases run longer.
