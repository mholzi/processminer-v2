## Responsibility
Reviews and approves bespoke guarantee wording — and may redline or propose amendments — to ensure it is legally sound before issuance proceeds. No formal SLA; informal target is 2 business days. Overdue reviews escalate via TFM to the Trade Finance Legal desk lead.

## In this process
Provides wording review and sign-off at step 3 for bespoke guarantee text; may also redline or propose amendments. Technical legal points are negotiated directly with the TFO; client-facing changes loop back via the relationship manager. Informal target 2 business days; overdue reviews escalate TFM → Trade Finance Legal desk lead.
