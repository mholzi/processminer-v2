## Responsibility
Approves issuance of bank guarantees and is accountable for the four-eyes issuance control. Delegated authority is capped at EUR 2 million (lowered from EUR 5m, v2 revision); above that threshold the Head of Trade Finance must co-sign. In the TFM's absence, a Senior TFO deputises.

## In this process
Approves issuance at step 5; delegated authority is capped at EUR 2 million (lowered from EUR 5m, v2 revision) — above that the Head of Trade Finance must co-sign. Accountable for steps 1, 2, 3 and 6 as supervisory escalation point. A Senior TFO deputises in the TFM's absence.
