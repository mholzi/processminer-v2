## Description
Approvals queue when the Trade Finance Manager is unavailable, and the EUR 5 million escalation path adds unpredictable delay when the Head of Trade Finance is travelling or otherwise unreachable.

## Impact
Applications stall at the approval step with no progress, directly breaching the 3-business-day turnaround target when the bottleneck is not resolved promptly.

## Root cause
The approval authority is concentrated in a single named TFM per desk and a single Head of Trade Finance, with no documented deputy or delegation mechanism to cover absences.
