## Description
Incomplete submissions are the biggest intake friction — missing beneficiary bank SWIFT details, wrong currency, and vague wording-type instructions are the most common deficiencies.

## Impact
The TFO loses real time chasing missing details from clients, delaying the start of guarantee processing.

## Root cause
Clients and relationship managers are not always familiar with the full set of required application fields.
