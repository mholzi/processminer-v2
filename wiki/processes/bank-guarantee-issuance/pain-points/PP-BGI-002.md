## Description
Bespoke wording requests add unpredictable delay because the Legal team has no committed turnaround time for wording reviews.

## Impact
Bespoke wording applications cannot be committed to any service level, creating uncertainty for the Trade Finance team. The client is also left without a delivery date, which can cause downstream commercial consequences — missed tender deadlines or contractual exposure.

## Root cause
No formal SLA agreement between Trade Finance and Legal for bespoke wording reviews, compounded by variable wording complexity and Legal's workload prioritisation across other matters. Roughly 10–15% of applications follow the bespoke path — a meaningful share of volume.
