## The trend
AI-powered sanctions screening tools using NLP and contextual disambiguation are dramatically reducing false positive alert rates in trade finance, enabling compliance teams to focus investigation effort on genuine risks.

## Relevance
High false positive rates at sanctions screening are a documented pain point; an AI uplift to the Sanctions Screening Tool would reduce avoidable investigation load and shrink turnaround time for clean applications.

## Evidence
Everest Group 2025 benchmarks 85-90% of sanctions alerts as false positives, with 30-45 minutes per Level 1 review. HSBC achieved 60%-plus alert reduction and detected two to four times more genuine suspicious activity using AI.
