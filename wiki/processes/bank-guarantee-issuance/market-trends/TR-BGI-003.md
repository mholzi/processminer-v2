## The trend
The ICC and SWIFT published the first industry-wide API specifications for bank guarantees in 2024, enabling real-time straight-through corporate-to-bank guarantee issuance without reliance on the SWIFT MT798 messaging platform.

## Relevance
Adoption of these standards would automate instruction receipt at intake, remove manual SWIFT steps at guarantee generation and delivery, and enable system-enforced pre-transmission validation — directly closing the pre-transmission verification control gap.

## Evidence
Standard Chartered completed the first live ICC-SWIFT API bank guarantee via Komgo in January 2026; BNP Paribas and Surecomp ran the first corporate pilot in November 2024.
