## The trend
In 2025-2026 agentic AI is moving from pilot to production in corporate banking, with purpose-built agents performing trade finance intake validation, completeness checking and policy screening — functioning as autonomous digital co-workers under human oversight.

## Relevance
In advisory flag-and-continue mode, the agent reduces TFO information-chasing but is a tool, not a control; CG-BGI-001 remains open without hard-flag mode for mandatory fields and logged override decisions. At current volumes a 5–10% false-positive rate risks trading information-chasing for disputed-flag mediation — a 4-week shadow pilot before deployment is recommended.

## Evidence
By Q1 2026, 44% of finance teams had deployed agentic AI — up from 7% in January 2025, a 600% year-on-year increase. Oracle launched a Bank Guarantee Application Validator Agent in April 2026.
