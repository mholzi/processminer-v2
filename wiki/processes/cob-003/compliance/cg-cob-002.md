## The gap
Account configuration is verified in practice during PS-COB-004, but this verification is not captured as a formal, named control.

## Risk
An undocumented control is invisible to audit and can quietly erode — no owner, no testing schedule, no evidence trail.

## Remediation
Formalise account-configuration verification as a control with an owner, a testing cadence and recorded evidence.
