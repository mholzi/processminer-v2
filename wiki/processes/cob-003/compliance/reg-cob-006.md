## What it requires
That no single person both performs and approves a sensitive action. This is an internal segregation-of-duties policy rather than external law.

## Why it applies
Account creation is a sensitive action that must not be both carried out and signed off by the same person.

## How it is met
Through the four-eyes check performed during Account Setup (PS-COB-004).
