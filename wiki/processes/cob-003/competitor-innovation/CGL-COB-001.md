## The move
Citi rolled out a fully digitized onboarding process on CitiDirect Commercial Banking, with real-time status updates and a 360-degree client view across cash, loans, trade, FX, servicing and onboarding/KYC, plus a Digital Servicing Hub for queries and document submission.

## Relevance
A global competitor gives commercial clients real-time onboarding status and staff a single platform view. COB-003 clients get no status visibility and staff have no single case view - both a client-experience and an efficiency gap.

## Evidence
Citi's 2025 release says CitiDirect Commercial Banking's fully digitized onboarding with real-time status updates significantly reduced onboarding turnaround times, on a platform giving a 360-degree consolidated client view.
