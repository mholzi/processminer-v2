## The move
BNP Paribas built Welcome, an in-house digital platform that streamlines KYC, account opening and e-banking contract subscription into a single onboarding journey, available across Europe and Asia and progressively rolling out worldwide.

## Relevance
A close European peer has unified KYC and account opening on one platform, while COB-003 still spreads onboarding across six-plus disconnected systems with re-keying. The gap is a slower, more error-prone journey than the peer now offers business clients.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas Cash Management describes Welcome as an in-house digital platform easing KYC, account opening and e-banking contract subscription, available in Europe and Asia and being rolled out worldwide.
