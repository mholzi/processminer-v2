## The move
ING runs a digital-first business-banking platform serving self-employed, SME and mid-corporate clients across eight markets, leaning on a strong digital identity and onboarding speed as the deliberate competitive edge in the SME segment.

## Relevance
ING competes for the same SME clients as BizBanking and treats onboarding speed and user experience as a differentiator that can outweigh product breadth - direct pressure on COB-003's multi-day, multi-system journey.

## Evidence
Analyst coverage notes ING's Business Banking serves around 1.7 million self-employed and SME customers across eight markets, with digital identity and onboarding speed cited as its segment edge.
