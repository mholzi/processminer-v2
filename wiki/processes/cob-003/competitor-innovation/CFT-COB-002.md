## The move
Revolut Business runs onboarding entirely in-app: the client completes a structured, guided digital flow, and once the steps are finished the account opens immediately, with no paper forms or wet-ink signatures.

## Relevance
A fintech delivers fully digital, signature-free, in-app onboarding while COB-003 still breaks its digital flow with a wet-ink signature and re-keying across systems - friction the challenger has designed out entirely.

## Evidence
Comparative reviews note Revolut and Tide handle business-account setup entirely in-app with a clear, structured flow, and the account opens immediately once the steps are completed.
