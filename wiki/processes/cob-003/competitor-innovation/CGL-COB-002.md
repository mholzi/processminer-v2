## The move
HSBC is investing 1.8 billion dollars in generative AI to retire legacy systems and redesign banking processes, and has simplified its business onboarding journey to open accounts in under three days.

## Relevance
A global competitor is rebuilding onboarding on a generative-AI foundation and advertising sub-three-day account opening. COB-003's KYC step alone runs two to five days, leaving a widening turnaround gap against AI-redesigned peers.

## Evidence
HSBC reports a 1.8 billion-dollar generative-AI investment redesigning banking processes, 85 percent of employees enabled on GenAI tools, and a simplified onboarding journey opening accounts in under three days.
