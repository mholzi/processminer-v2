## What it checks
That the client has given valid, recorded consent for the bank to process their personal data.

## Control activity
The Operations Officer presents the data-protection notice at intake and records the client's consent before any personal data is processed downstream.

## Risk addressed
Processing personal data without a lawful basis — a GDPR violation with regulatory and reputational consequences.

## Timing
Once, at Application Receipt (PS-COB-001). A manual control.
