## What it checks
That access to the new account is protected by strong, multi-factor authentication from the moment it goes live.

## Control activity
The system enforces SCA during account setup — no credentials are issued without it.

## Risk addressed
Unauthorised account access and payment fraud.

## Timing
Applied automatically during Account Setup (PS-COB-004). An automated control.
