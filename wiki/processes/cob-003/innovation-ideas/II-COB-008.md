## The idea
Add an open-banking connector to the credit step so a client can permission live bank-account data - cash flow, transaction history, balances - feeding the scorecard alongside or in place of the external bureau pull.

## Expected benefit
Removes the unpredictable, invisible bureau wait, gives the Credit Analyst a current cash-flow view rather than a stale report, and speeds the overdraft decision for the client.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity - needs an open-banking aggregator integration into the credit decisioning system, client-consent handling, and scorecard recalibration. Addresses pain point PP-COB-005.
