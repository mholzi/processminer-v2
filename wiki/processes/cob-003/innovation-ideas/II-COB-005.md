## The idea
Introduce AI-assisted screening that auto-clears low-risk routine alerts and learns from analyst decisions to suppress repeat false matches, replacing today's conservatively-tuned thresholds that have no learning loop.

## Expected benefit
Cuts the 40 percent false-positive load on KYC analysts, shortens the manual-review delay on onboarding, and lets analysts focus on genuine risk; industry platforms report 50 to 66 percent false-positive reductions.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity, high strategic fit. Needs model integration plus the explainability and audit layer regulators now require for automated decisions. Addresses pain point PP-COB-003.
