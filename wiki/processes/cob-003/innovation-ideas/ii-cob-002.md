## The idea
Replace manual chasing with an automated reminder sequence that escalates if documents are still missing.

## Expected benefit
Frees Operations Officer capacity and gives the client a predictable, less nagging experience. Estimated effort reduction -3.

## Feasibility
Low complexity — a contained automation. Addresses friction point FP-COB-002.
