## The idea
Add a guided, plain-language assistant that walks the client through ownership and control questions with worked examples, translating regulatory KYC language into terms a small-business owner can answer confidently.

## Expected benefit
Cuts wrong answers and the back-and-forth they trigger, improves data quality on the KYC step, and removes a frustrating moment in the client journey.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity. Needs content design plus a conversational layer over the existing KYC questionnaire. Addresses friction point FP-COB-004.
