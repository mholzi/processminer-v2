## The idea
Build a single onboarding workspace that stitches the six-plus systems, CRM, OWS, KYC, screening, core banking and card management, into one orchestrated case view, so staff see the full state of a case and data is entered once and propagated automatically.

## Expected benefit
Removes re-keying errors and slow handovers, gives staff and clients a clear single picture of case progress, and frees Operations Officer capacity for assessment work rather than navigation.

## Feasibility
High complexity, high strategic fit. Needs an integration layer across legacy systems and is the largest of the proposed changes. Addresses pain point PP-COB-002, the bank's core system-fragmentation problem.
