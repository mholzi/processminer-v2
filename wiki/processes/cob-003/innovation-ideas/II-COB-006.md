## The idea
Accept EU Digital Identity Wallet credentials so a client can share pre-verified identity and business attributes directly, instead of sourcing, uploading and re-submitting identity documents that the bank then verifies manually.

## Expected benefit
Reduces the documents a client must find and the incomplete-application chasing loop, speeds identity verification, and positions the bank for the January 2027 wallet-acceptance mandate.

## Feasibility
High complexity. Needs a wallet-connector integration and legal sign-off, but the eIDAS 2.0 timeline makes it a near-term requirement rather than an option. Addresses pain point PP-COB-001.
