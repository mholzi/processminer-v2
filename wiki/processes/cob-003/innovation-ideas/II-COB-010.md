## The idea
Let the client book their own welcome-call slot from the Relationship Manager's calendar, and send an automated digital welcome package with access credentials and first-step guidance the moment the account goes live.

## Expected benefit
Ends the three-attempt phone-and-email chase, de-fragments the Relationship Manager's time, and gives the client an immediate, self-paced activation rather than waiting for the bank to call.

## Feasibility
Low complexity - a self-scheduling widget linked to RM calendars plus a templated welcome message triggered on activation. Addresses pain point PP-COB-006.
