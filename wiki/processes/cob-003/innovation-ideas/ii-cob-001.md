An interactive checklist that adapts to the client's business type and validates uploads in real time, so the client sees immediately what is still missing or wrong.

## Expected benefit
Directly attacks the incomplete-application rate and manual document chasing. Estimated client-effort reduction of -6 on the worst touchpoint.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity, high strategic fit — it addresses friction point FP-COB-001 and the bank's single biggest cycle-time drag.
