## The idea
Stand up continuous controls monitoring over onboarding: formalise account-configuration verification as a named, automatically-tested control, and instrument product disclosure to capture a recorded client acknowledgement that monitoring checks on every case.

## Expected benefit
Closes two control gaps at once - gives account-configuration verification an owner and an evidence trail, and supplies the disclosure proof audit currently lacks, raising the medium-effective rating.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity - needs a controls-monitoring layer over core banking and the disclosure step, plus control-owner assignment. Addresses compliance gaps CG-COB-001 and CG-COB-002.
