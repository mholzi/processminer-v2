## Target description
Activation is client-paced. The moment the account goes live, an automated digital welcome package with access credentials and first-step guidance reaches the client. Rather than the Relationship Manager chasing a slot across phone and email, the client books their own welcome-call time from the RM's calendar. The relationship still gets a personal call, but the scheduling friction around it is removed.

## What changes
- A templated digital welcome package is triggered automatically on account activation
- The client books their own welcome-call slot from the RM's calendar
- The three-attempt phone-and-email chase to agree a time is eliminated
- The client gets immediate, self-paced activation instead of waiting for the bank to call

## Rationale
Welcome-call scheduling takes three attempts on average and fragments the Relationship Manager's time. Letting clients self-schedule and delivering the welcome digitally removes that manual chase while keeping the personal contact that sets the relationship tone.
