## What happens
The Operations Officer creates the account in the core banking system, configures the agreed products, and triggers debit-card provisioning. A four-eyes check confirms the configuration before the account goes live.

## Inputs
The approved KYC file and, where applicable, the credit decision.

## Outputs
A live, fully configured business account ready to transact, with debit cards ordered.

## Why it matters
This is where the relationship becomes real. Configuration errors here surface as transaction failures the client sees on their very first day.
