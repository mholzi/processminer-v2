## What happens
The Operations Officer samples completed onboarding cases, checks them against the quality and compliance standard, and logs any defects for correction and trend analysis.

## Inputs
- Completed onboarding cases from the preceding 10 business days
- The quality and compliance standard the cases are checked against

## Outputs
- A quality verdict for each sampled case
- A defect log feeding correction and trend analysis

## Why it matters
It is the feedback loop. Without it the same defects repeat unnoticed across hundreds of applications.
