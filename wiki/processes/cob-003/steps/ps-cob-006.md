## What happens
The Team Lead samples completed onboarding cases, checks them against the quality and compliance standard, and logs any defects for correction and trend analysis.

## Inputs
Completed onboarding cases from the preceding 10 business days.

## Outputs
A quality verdict per sampled case and a defect log that feeds continuous improvement.

## Why it matters
It is the feedback loop. Without it the same defects repeat unnoticed across hundreds of applications.
