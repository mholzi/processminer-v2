## The gap
The bank has no connector to the EU Digital Identity Wallet and no legal sign-off for accepting wallet-based credentials, yet eIDAS 2.0 makes acceptance a January 2027 obligation.

## Resolution
The transformation must build a wallet-connector integration and secure legal and compliance sign-off on relying on wallet-issued attributes for KYC. Target state TS-COB-001 depends on this capability; the regulatory deadline sets a fixed sequencing constraint the plan must work back from.

## Status
Open — neither the connector nor the legal assessment has started; the January 2027 mandate makes this time-critical.
