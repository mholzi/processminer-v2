## The moment
The first time the client hears back after submitting — an acknowledgement, a question, or silence.

## Why it matters
It sets the client's expectation for the entire relationship. Speed and clarity here are remembered; so is silence.

## Design implication
Guarantee a fast, substantive first response — ideally an automated acknowledgement plus a clear next step within hours.
