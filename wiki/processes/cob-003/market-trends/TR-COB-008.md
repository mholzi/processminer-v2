## The trend
Banks are adding self-service appointment scheduling with advisors and automated welcome journeys - guided tutorials and a clear digital welcome package delivered the moment an account is provisioned - letting clients book and resume on their own terms across devices.

## Relevance
COB-003's activation relies on the Relationship Manager initiating every contact attempt, averaging three tries to agree a welcome-call slot. Client self-scheduling and an automated welcome package would remove that manual chasing and de-fragment the RM's time.

## Evidence
Banking onboarding best-practice guides for 2025 describe self-service advisor scheduling and automated customer welcome journeys with guided tutorials as standard digital-onboarding features.
