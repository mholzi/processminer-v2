## The trend
Banks are moving from periodic, sample-based control testing to continuous controls monitoring - automated tests run on key controls in near real time across the full transaction population, generating audit-ready evidence and pushed by resilience rules such as DORA.

## Relevance
COB-003 verifies account configuration in practice but not as a named control, and its product-disclosure control is only medium-effective with weak evidence. Continuous monitoring would test these controls automatically and leave the evidence trail that audit currently lacks.

## Evidence
Continuous controls monitoring is reported to cut compliance and audit costs by around 75 percent and to reduce control-testing time from 50 hours to under 10 hours per control each year.
