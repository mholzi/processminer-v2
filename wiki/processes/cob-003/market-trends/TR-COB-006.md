## The trend
Lenders are using open-banking APIs to pull SMEs' live bank-account data - cash flow, transaction history, balances - with the client's permission, replacing outdated credit reports and manual document submission with a current, comprehensive view that supports real-time credit decisions.

## Relevance
COB-003's credit assessment waits on an external bureau whose response time varies and is invisible to the analyst. Permissioned open-banking data would give a real-time, cash-flow-based view, removing the unpredictable bureau wait on the overdraft step.

## Evidence
Open-banking lenders such as Qred report real-time financial-data access improving credit decisions; analyses describe instant data enabling real-time decisions and faster onboarding for thin-file and self-employed SME borrowers.
