## The trend
Banks are adopting process mining and process intelligence to monitor onboarding workflows from event data, surfacing where time leaks through long waits, handovers and reassignments, and tracking SLA adherence continuously rather than through periodic, look-back reports.

## Relevance
COB-003 defines an SLA on every step but measures adherence nowhere, and only total cycle time is known, not the per-step split. Process mining would expose the real bottleneck step and flag a slipping week before a client ever complains.

## Evidence
SLA dashboards show what was missed while process mining reveals why; banking process-intelligence guides report it isolating SLA breaches and pinpointing the waits, handoffs and reassignments where time leaks.
