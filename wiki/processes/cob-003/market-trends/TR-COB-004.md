## The trend
Banks are deploying agentic AI and generative-AI intelligent document processing in onboarding: systems interpret variable documents, extract and validate data, escalate exceptions and keep audit trails. Front- and back-office systems are being unified into a single connected workflow.

## Relevance
COB-003 staff move across six or more disconnected systems, re-keying data with no single case view, and chase documents manually. Agentic document processing on a unified onboarding platform addresses both the system-switching burden and the incomplete-application loop.

## Evidence
Deloitte and BCG agentic-AI banking analyses, and TCS on generative AI in onboarding, describe unified-platform orchestration and GenAI document processing cutting evaluation time from 30 minutes to seconds.
