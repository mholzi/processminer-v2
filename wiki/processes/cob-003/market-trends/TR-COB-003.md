## The trend
Under eIDAS 2.0, every EU member state must offer an EU Digital Identity Wallet by the end of 2026, and regulated businesses including banks must accept wallet-based digital identities for KYC from January 2027. Citizens and companies carry reusable, pre-verified identity attributes.

## Relevance
COB-003 collects and verifies identity and business documents manually, a major source of chasing and delay. Reusable verified credentials from the EU Digital Identity Wallet let the process pre-fill and trust identity data, cutting documents the client must find and the bank must check.

## Evidence
The EU eIDAS 2.0 regulation sets the end-2026 wallet availability and January 2027 acceptance deadlines; Zyphe and Moody's KYC analyses describe the resulting onboarding impact for banks.
