## The trend
Client onboarding abandonment is rising sharply across financial services. A 2025 survey of 600 senior decision-makers found 70 percent of firms lost clients to inefficient onboarding, up from 48 percent in 2023, with commercial and corporate banks facing the steepest challenges.

## Relevance
COB-003 loses time to incomplete applications, repeated document chasing and multi-system handovers, exactly the poor data management and process delays that 86 and 77 percent of banks blame for abandonment. Each lost business client is forgone revenue and wasted onboarding effort.

## Evidence
Fenergo's 2025 survey of 600 decision-makers and FinTech Global reporting found 70 percent of firms lost clients and that KYC and AML issues drive one in five abandonments, costing an estimated 3.3 billion dollars a year.
