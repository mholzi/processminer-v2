## The trend
Banks are replacing conservatively-tuned, rule-based AML and sanctions screening with AI- and machine-learning-driven systems, and moving from periodic checks to perpetual KYC. AI agents now clear routine low-risk alerts automatically, and AI-enhanced platforms report cutting false positives by 50 to 66 percent.

## Relevance
COB-003's screening tool flags around 40 percent of applications as false positives, and KYC analysts spend significant time clearing harmless name matches. AI-driven screening with a learning loop directly targets that waste and the manual-review delay it adds to onboarding cycle time.

## Evidence
Anaptyss' 2026 AML and KYC Trends and Regula Forensics' 2025 KYC Trends report AI cutting false positives by 50 to 66 percent and the industry shift toward perpetual KYC.
