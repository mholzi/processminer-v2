## Description
Staff move between six or more systems to complete one onboarding — CRM, OWS, KYC platform, screening, core banking, card management — re-keying the same data along the way.

## Impact
Data-entry errors, slow handovers and visible staff frustration. No single screen shows the full state of a case.

## Root cause
The systems were added over time without an integration layer or a single case view stitched across them.
