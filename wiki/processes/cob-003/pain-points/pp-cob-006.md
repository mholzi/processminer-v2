## Description
Reaching the client for the welcome call takes several attempts across phone and email before a slot is agreed.

## Impact
On average three attempts per client; the activation step drags and the Relationship Manager's time is fragmented.

## Root cause
There is no client self-scheduling option — the bank initiates every contact attempt manually.
