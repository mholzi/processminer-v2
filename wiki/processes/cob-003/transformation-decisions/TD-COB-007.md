## The decision
Let clients book their own welcome-call slot from the Relationship Manager's calendar, and send an automated digital welcome package with credentials and first-step guidance the moment the account goes live.

## Options considered
- Add both client self-scheduling and an automated welcome package
- Add only the automated welcome package and keep RM-initiated call scheduling
- Add only self-scheduling and keep manual welcome communication
- Keep the current manual phone-and-email activation

## Rationale
Welcome-call scheduling takes three contact attempts on average and fragments the Relationship Manager's time. This is the lowest-complexity change in the set — a scheduling widget and a templated message — and it removes the chase without losing the personal welcome call.
