## The decision
Define and document which role owns first-touch handling of partner-channel applications, so partner submissions are triaged with the same clear accountability as the other three intake channels.

## Options considered
- Assign partner-channel first-touch to the existing Operations Officer triage role
- Create a dedicated partner-channel intake role
- Route partner submissions through the partner with a defined hand-back point
- Leave first-touch ownership undefined

## Rationale
The process runs through four intake channels but only the partner channel has no documented first-handling owner, so applications can sit unassigned. This is a governance decision, not a technology one — the target design needs a single accountable role named before partner volume scales.
