## Responsibility
Owns the client relationship and the onboarding process end to end — handles client communication and activation, supervises the onboarding team, gives the four-eyes approval on sensitive actions, and is accountable for process quality as process owner.

## In this process
Performs Client Communication & Activation (PS-COB-005) and delivers product disclosure to the client. As process owner, gives the four-eyes approval on sensitive actions and is accountable for the remaining onboarding steps, including the post-onboarding quality check.
