## The gap
Each step has a defined SLA, but whether those SLAs are actually being met is not measured or reported anywhere.

## Impact
The process cannot tell a healthy week from a slipping one. Problems are noticed only when a client complains.

## Next step
Instrument OWS to capture step start/end times and report SLA adherence on a dashboard.
