## The gap
The process is triggered through four channels, but for partner-channel submissions the initial-handling responsibility is not documented.

## Impact
Partner-channel applications can sit unassigned; accountability for the first triage is ambiguous.

## Next step
Define and document which role owns first-touch handling for partner-channel applications.
