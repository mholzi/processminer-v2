## Definition
The average elapsed processing time from application receipt to a live, activated account.

## Current reading
4–6 hours of processing time on average — though calendar time is longer when documents must be chased.

## Why it matters
Cycle time is what the client feels. It is also the headline number any improvement effort will be judged against.
