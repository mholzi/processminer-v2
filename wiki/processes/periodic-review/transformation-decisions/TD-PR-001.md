## The decision
Periodic KYC Review is owned by Financial Crime Operations. The RM owns the client relationship but is not accountable for review execution.

## Options considered
- FCO-owned process with RM in a consultation role
- Keep RM-owned with better tooling

## Rationale
The root cause of overdue reviews is incentive misalignment, not tooling. RMs are measured on AUM and revenue; periodic reviews are a cost. Moving accountability to FCO and reducing RM involvement to a consultation role removes the incentive conflict.
