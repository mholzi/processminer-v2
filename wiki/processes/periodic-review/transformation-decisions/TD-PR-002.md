## The decision
Buy a vendor case-management platform (Fenergo or ComplyAdvantage Mesh). Build the Trigger Engine, the STP Decision Engine, and the Audit Ledger in-house.

## Options considered
- Build everything
- Buy everything

## Rationale
Case management is a commodity; integration depth and configurability matter more than uniqueness. The trigger / decision / audit layer is our competitive and regulatory differentiator and must not be a black box to FCO and Internal Audit.
