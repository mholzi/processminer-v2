## The decision
One SLA framework with risk-rating-driven tiers, applied identically across all booking centres.

## Options considered
- Single risk-rating-driven SLA framework across all booking centres
- Per-country SLAs reflecting local norms

## Rationale
Group internal audit has flagged inconsistency between booking centres twice. A single framework is defensible to the lead supervisor (FINMA as group regulator).
