## The decision
STP share is hard-capped at 70% of the eligible book, even if the model would route more cases through STP.

## Options considered
- Let the model decide — no cap
- Cap at 50%

## Rationale
70% balances cost reduction against the supervisory expectation that human review remains a material control. The cap is removable when challenger model validation has run for ≥ 12 months.
