## The decision
Hard cap STP share at 70% of the eligible book, even if the model would route more.

## Options considered
- Let the model decide
- Cap at 50%

## Rationale
70% balances cost-out against the supervisory expectation that human review remains a material control. The cap is removable when challenger model validation has run for at least 12 months.
