## The decision
All non-PB client outreach defaults to the mobile app with RM-mediated outreach as a 30-day fall-back. Private Banking stays RM-mediated.

## Options considered
- Email outreach
- Branch visit
- Phone

## Rationale
Mobile app outreach achieves 7-day median completion in the pilot vs. 21 days for email. RM-mediated remains essential for PB tone-of-voice and for clients without the app (~9% of the book).
