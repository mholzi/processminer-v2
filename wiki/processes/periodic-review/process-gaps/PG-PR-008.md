## The gap
Exit-letter templates are not centralised. The gap is recorded in the Gap Log as: 'Exit-letter templates not centralised.' In the target state, on exit or restriction, Customer Communications is notified to draft the regulatory-compliant exit letter.

## Impact
Group internal audit has flagged inconsistency between booking centres twice. A single, centrally controlled framework is required to be defensible to the lead supervisor.

## Next step
Centralise exit-letter templates in the Customer Communications function with Legal oversight. Owner: Legal. Target close: Q1 2027.
