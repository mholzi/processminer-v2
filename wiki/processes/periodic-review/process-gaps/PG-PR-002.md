## The gap
Client outreach for the periodic KYC review is conducted via email, phone, and in-person visits. There is no digital-first outreach channel; the Outreach Service targeting the mobile app does not yet exist in the As-Is state.

## Impact
Median outreach completion takes 21 days by email versus 7 days in the mobile-app pilot, prolonging the overall cycle. The As-Is outreach rate is 91%; the bank seeks to reduce this through STP and targeted outreach.

## Next step
Deploy the Outreach Service with mobile app as the primary channel and RM-mediated outreach as a 30-day fall-back. Owner: Digital. Target close: Q2 2027.
