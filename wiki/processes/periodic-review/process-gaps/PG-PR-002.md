## The gap
There is no KPI tracking median cycle time separately by risk tier. Cycle time is not measured at all by risk tier.

## Impact
Process improvement efforts cannot identify which risk tier drives delays; SLA compliance cannot be verified per tier. Severity: Medium. Owner: PMO. Target close: Q3 2026.

## Next step
Implement cycle-time KPI reporting broken down by risk tier. Owner: PMO. Target close: Q3 2026.
