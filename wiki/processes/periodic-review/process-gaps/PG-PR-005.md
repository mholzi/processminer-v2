## The gap
Quality-assurance sampling of completed reviews is ad-hoc and not statistical. The 5% random QA sampling control (KYC-C-04) covering both STP and analyst approvals is a target-state control that does not yet exist in the As-Is process.

## Impact
Ad-hoc QA does not provide the structured assurance that the target control framework requires. The gap is classified Medium severity in the Gap Log.

## Next step
Redesign QA sampling as a statistically valid 5% random selection covering both STP and analyst approvals (target control KYC-C-04). Owner: QA. Target close: Q4 2026.
