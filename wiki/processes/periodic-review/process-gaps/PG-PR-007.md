## The gap
Clients have no visibility of their KYC review status. In the As-Is state they learn review status only at the next outreach. No in-app or digital channel allows a client to see whether a review is in progress or when it was completed.

## Impact
The absence of status visibility is an identified friction point in the client journey. The target process adds an explicit CX principle: 'The client sees their KYC status and next review date in the app at all times.'

## Next step
Expose KYC review status in the bank's mobile app as part of the Outreach Service build. Owner: Digital. Target close: Q3 2027.
