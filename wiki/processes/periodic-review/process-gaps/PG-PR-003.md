## The gap
Legal entities onboarded before 2018 have incomplete beneficial-owner graphs in the Entity Resolution system; the data was not captured consistently under the prior onboarding process.

## Impact
Pre-fill cannot fully populate beneficial-owner data for legacy entities; these cases are less likely to achieve STP eligibility and require analyst triage. Severity: Medium. Owner: Data. Target close: Q4 2027 (remediation programme).

## Next step
Execute remediation programme PRJ-ENT-BO to backfill beneficial-owner data for pre-2018 entities. Owner: Data. Target close: Q4 2027 (remediation programme).
