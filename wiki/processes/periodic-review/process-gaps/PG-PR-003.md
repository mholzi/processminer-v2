## The gap
The As-Is process has no defined or measured KPI for review cycle time broken down by risk tier (Low / Medium / High). The gap is recorded in the Gap Log as: 'No KPI on cycle time per risk tier.'

## Impact
The 18.4% overdue rate on High-risk clients as of Q1 2026 is a documented headline problem. Without per-tier cycle-time KPIs the process owner lacks a structured basis for identifying which risk segment is driving overdue reviews.

## Next step
Define and instrument per-tier cycle-time KPIs in the target dashboard. Owner: PMO. Target close: Q3 2026.
