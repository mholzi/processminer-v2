## What it checks
A fresh sanctions, PEP, and adverse-media screen is run at sign-off to catch any new hits that arose after the initial pre-fill screening.

## Control activity
The Screening Service (Dow Jones RC, subject to re-contracting) re-runs the screen at sign-off. The result is written to the Audit Ledger as part of the case's evidence pack. Evidence: Screening evidence record in the Audit Ledger.

## Risk addressed
Approving a client whose sanctions status, PEP designation, or adverse-media profile changed after the pre-fill screen was taken — a gap that could result in a sanctions violation or breach of AMLD6 ongoing-monitoring obligations.

## Timing
Per case, at the sign-off step (Step 6).
