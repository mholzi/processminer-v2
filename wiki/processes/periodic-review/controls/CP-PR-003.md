## What it checks
High-risk clients, PEPs, recommend-exit decisions, and approve-with-conditions decisions where the condition restricts a regulated product must receive mandatory Financial Crime Officer (FCO) sign-off.

## Control activity
The Financial Crime Officer records sign-off against their SSO identity. The entry is immutable. Cases requiring FCO sign-off are routed by the process following the Reviewer Triage decision at Step 5 or directly for qualifying case types. Evidence: Sign-off ledger.

## Risk addressed
Insufficiently scrutinised approvals for high-risk clients, PEPs, and exit decisions — the primary regulatory escalation risk. Absence of systematic 4-eyes enforcement is gap G-03 (High severity, target close Q3 2026 interim policy / Q1 2027 system).

## Timing
Per case — mandatory for every High-risk client, PEP, recommend-exit decision, and approve-with-conditions decision that restricts a regulated product.
