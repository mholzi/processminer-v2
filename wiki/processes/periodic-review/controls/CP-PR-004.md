## What it checks
A random 5% sample of STP auto-approvals and FCO Analyst approvals is reviewed for decision quality and compliance with policy.

## Control activity
Cases are selected via random sampling by the QA function. The reviewer assesses each case against the evidence pack and decision rationale, producing a QA scorecard per case. Findings are reported to the Head of Financial Crime Operations and reviewed monthly. Evidence: QA scorecards.

## Risk addressed
Model drift in the STP Decision Engine, or systematic analyst errors, that would otherwise accumulate undetected across large volumes — gap G-07 (Medium severity, ad-hoc sampling not statistically designed, target close Q4 2026).

## Timing
Per case, with monthly aggregate reporting.
