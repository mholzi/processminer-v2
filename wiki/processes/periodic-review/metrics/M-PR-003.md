## Definition
Median elapsed calendar days from case open to final FCO sign-off for High-risk reviews, covering outreach, FCO Analyst triage (Step 5 SLA: 5 days) and 4-eyes sign-off. Tracked monthly under the Efficiency KPI dimension.

## Current reading
47 days as at Q1 2026 (measured actual). Near-term target (Y+1): 18 days via structured case management and digital outreach. Y+2 target: 12 days, requiring the full SLA framework (Decision D6) across all booking centres.

## Why it matters
High-risk reviews carry the heaviest regulatory weight; a 47-day median means many cases breach the annual review cadence. Reaching 18 days requires system-enforced FCO triage SLA and deterministic digital outreach replacing the current paper and email flow.
