## Definition
Median calendar days from the ReviewDue event to case close for High-risk clients. Covers the full cycle including targeted outreach, FCO analyst triage, and FCO sign-off. Part of the Efficiency KPI dimension.

## Current reading
47 days as at Q1 2026. Target Y+1: 18 days; Target Y+2: 12 days. The SLA framework applies a single risk-tier-driven target with no segment carve-outs (Decision D6).

## Why it matters
High-risk reviews require structured FCO analyst triage (Step 5 SLA: 5 working days) and deterministic outreach with a hard 30-day timeout.
