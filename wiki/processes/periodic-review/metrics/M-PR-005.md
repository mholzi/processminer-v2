## Definition
Percentage of periodic KYC reviews requiring client contact to request data or documents. Step 4 (Targeted Outreach) fires only when the pre-fill data delta is non-zero. Tracked under the Client Experience KPI dimension.

## Current reading
91% of reviews required client contact as at Q1 2026 (measured actual). Near-term target (Y+1): 38%, achievable once pre-fill and STP cover ~62% of the eligible book. Y+2 target: 28%.

## Why it matters
A 91% outreach rate signals systematic over-collection: the bank asks for data it already holds in onboarding records, transactions and the customer master. This creates GDPR data-minimisation exposure. Reducing to 38% validates pre-fill completeness exceeds the STP eligibility threshold.
