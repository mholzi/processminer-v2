## Definition
Percentage of periodic KYC reviews in which the bank contacts the client to request data or documents. Targeted outreach (Step 4) fires only when the minimal data delta is non-zero; this metric measures how often

## Current reading
91% of reviews require client contact as at Q1 2026. Target Y+1: 38%; Target Y+2: 28%. Reviews completed without client contact are tracked separately as a quality signal.

## Why it matters
A 91% outreach rate reflects the As-Is over-collection problem: clients are asked for documents the bank already holds in onboarding records, transactions, and the customer master.
