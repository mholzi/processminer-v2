## Definition
Percentage of the High-risk client book whose periodic KYC review has passed its due date and remains uncompleted. Measured monthly by risk tier; falls under the Coverage KPI dimension (Section 10).

## Current reading
18.4% of the High-risk book overdue as at Q1 2026 (measured actual). Near-term target (Y+1): 4.0%; Y+2: below 1.0%. Subject of live regulatory findings: BaFin §44 KWG inspection (Sep 2025) and audit report IA-2025-117.

## Why it matters
The primary supervisory exposure metric. The 18.4% overdue rate was cited in the BaFin §44 KWG inspection (Sep 2025) and internal audit report IA-2025-117. Reducing it below 4% by Y+1 is the programme's most urgent commitment.
