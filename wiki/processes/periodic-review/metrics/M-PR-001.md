## Definition
Percentage of the High-risk client book with a periodic KYC review that has passed its due date and has not been completed. Measured monthly by risk tier as part of the Coverage KPI dimension.

## Current reading
18.4% of the High-risk book overdue as at Q1 2026 — cited in the Executive Summary alongside the BaFin §44 KWG inspection (Sep 2025) and internal audit report IA-2025-117. Target Y+1: ≤4.0%; Target Y+2: <1.0%.

## Why it matters
The primary regulatory exposure metric for the process. The 18.4% reading is cited alongside the BaFin §44 KWG inspection (Sep 2025) and internal audit report IA-2025-117 as the reason this DTP exists.
