## Definition
Fully loaded cost per completed periodic KYC review, covering RM, FCO Analyst and sign-off time plus system costs. Tracked monthly under the Efficiency KPI dimension and reviewed quarterly with the Operational Risk Committee.

## Current reading
CHF 312 per review as at Q1 2026 (measured actual). Near-term target (Y+1): CHF 118, a 62% reduction via STP growth and reduced RM hours. Y+2 target: CHF 84, a 73% reduction.

## Why it matters
The headline efficiency KPI for the transformation programme. Reviewed quarterly by the Operational Risk Committee alongside cycle time and RM hours. The Y+2 reduction to CHF 84 is contingent on Private Banking cutover to the Case Manager (Roadmap: 2027 Q3).
