## Definition
Fully-loaded cost per completed periodic KYC review. Part of the Efficiency KPI dimension reviewed monthly by PMO and quarterly by the Operational Risk Committee.

## Why it matters
The headline efficiency KPI for the transformation programme. The CHF 312 current cost and CHF 84 Y+2 target represent a 73% reduction, driven by STP volume growth and RM hour reduction as the target process embeds.
