## Definition
Average Relationship Manager hours consumed per completed periodic KYC review, covering outreach initiation, document chasing, cover-sheet preparation and Compliance Ops hand-off. Tracked monthly under the Efficiency KPI dimension (Section 10).

## Current reading
3.2 hours per review as at Q1 2026 (measured actual). Near-term target (Y+1): 0.6 hours, once the RM role is reduced to a consultation and PB fall-back function (Decisions D1, D4). Y+2 target: 0.4 hours.

## Why it matters
RM time is the primary cost driver and root of the incentive-misalignment: RMs measured on AUM treat reviews as a cost. Decision D1 removes RM accountability. The drop from 3.2 h to 0.6 h at Y+1 confirms D1 is working.
