## Definition
Average Relationship Manager hours consumed per completed periodic KYC review, covering outreach initiation, document chasing, cover-sheet completion, and follow-up with Compliance Ops. Part of the Efficiency KPI dimension.

## Current reading
3.2 hours per review as at Q1 2026. Target Y+1: 0.6 h; Target Y+2: 0.4 h.

## Why it matters
Transformation Decision D1 separates process ownership from client ownership: the RM owns the client relationship but is no longer accountable for review execution. The RM hours metric tracks whether that accountability shift has landed.
