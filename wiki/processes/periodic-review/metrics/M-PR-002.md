## Definition
Median calendar days from the ReviewDue event to case close for Low-risk clients. Part of the Efficiency KPI dimension tracked monthly on the PMO dashboard.

## Current reading
14 days as at Q1 2026. Target state: 0 days via STP auto-approval in both Y+1 and Y+2, once the STP Decision Engine is live.

## Why it matters
Low-risk reviews that complete without client contact should close the same day once data pre-fill and STP auto-approval are operational, matching the 0-day target.
