## Definition
Median elapsed calendar days from case open to close-out for Low- and Medium-risk client reviews. Tracked monthly under the Efficiency KPI dimension (Section 10).

## Current reading
14 days as at Q1 2026 (measured actual). Near-term target (Y+1): 0 days via straight-through processing (STP); Y+2: 0 days (STP). Target assumes ~62% of Low- and Medium-risk reviews auto-complete without human intervention.

## Why it matters
Low-risk reviews eligible for STP should close on the day the case opens once pre-fill and auto-approval are live. A 14-day median confirms manual handling dominates a case type the target process aims to run at zero elapsed time.
