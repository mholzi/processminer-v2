## What it requires
GwG Art. 6 imposes special clarification obligations on increased risk. The document maps this article to that obligation.

## Why it applies
The bank operates a Swiss booking centre. High-risk clients and PEPs require mandatory 4-eyes FCO sign-off, engaging the special clarification obligations of Art. 6.

## How it is met
High-risk cases are ineligible for STP and routed through FCO Analyst triage. Evidence pack includes source-of-funds signals and beneficial-owner graph. Mandatory 4-eyes FCO sign-off applied before close-out, recorded in the Audit Ledger with case ID, actor, and policy clause.
