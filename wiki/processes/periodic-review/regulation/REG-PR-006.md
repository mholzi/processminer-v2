## What it requires
Art. 5(1)(c) requires data minimisation — only data that is adequate, relevant and limited to what is necessary may be processed. Art. 25 requires privacy by design, embedding data-protection principles into the process architecture from the outset.

## Why it applies
Client outreach during the review requests personal data from clients. The process must minimise collection to what the bank cannot already verify from its own records, and the outreach architecture must embed privacy by design as required by Art. 25.

## How it is met
The data-minimisation pre-fill approach computes the minimal data delta — only data the bank cannot already verify from client master, Document Vault, or Transaction Datamart — and asks the client for that alone.
