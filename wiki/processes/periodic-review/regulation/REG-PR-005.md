## What it requires
BaIT AT 4.3 requires IT-based control execution and evidence completeness. The document maps this module to those obligations.

## Why it applies
A BaFin §44 KWG inspection in September 2025 raised findings on control execution, ageing and evidence completeness in the Periodic KYC Review process. This DTP is directly designed to close that finding.

## How it is met
Every control action is written to the immutable, hash-chained Audit Ledger with case ID, actor, policy clause, and timestamp — directly closing the BaFin §44 finding on evidence completeness.
