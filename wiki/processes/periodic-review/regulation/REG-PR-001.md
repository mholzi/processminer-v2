## What it requires
Art. 13(1)(d) requires financial institutions to apply ongoing monitoring and periodic review of customer relationships, including re-verification of client identity and beneficial ownership on a risk-rated cadence.

## Why it applies
The bank operates EU booking centres covered by AMLD6. Periodic KYC Review is the bank's primary ongoing-due-diligence control under this directive, satisfying the ongoing monitoring and periodic review obligation stated in the document's regulatory mapping (§5.1).
