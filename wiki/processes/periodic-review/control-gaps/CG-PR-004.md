## The gap
There is no system-enforced 4-eyes sign-off requirement for High-risk client, PEP or exit-recommendation cases. Whether a case is escalated to the Financial Crime Officer depends on the analyst on duty, not a written rule. Escalation criteria are opaque and not codified in the workflow.

## Risk
High AML regulatory exposure. High-risk and PEP reviews may be approved by a single analyst without FCO review, breaching enhanced due diligence obligations under AMLD6 Art. 13(1)(d) and AMLO-FINMA §22/§23. Inconsistent sign-off records prevent reliable reconstruction for supervisory purposes.

## Remediation
Two-track remediation: interim mandatory FCO countersignature policy by Q3 2026; full system enforcement by Q1 2027 via Case Manager routing with sign-off recorded against the FCO's SSO identity in the Audit Ledger. Owner: FCO.
