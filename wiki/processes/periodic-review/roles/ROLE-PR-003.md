## Responsibility
The Financial Crime Officer (FCO) is the senior accountable role for Periodic KYC Review, providing mandatory 4-eyes sign-off on high-risk, PEP, recommend-exit, and approve-with-conditions cases that restrict a regulated product.

## In this process
R/A for 4-eyes sign-off (Step 6): mandatory for High-risk clients, PEPs, recommend-exit, approve-with-conditions restricting a regulated product, and random 5% QA sample. Sign-off recorded against FCO SSO identity and immutable. Accountable for client exit drafting. Consulted on triage (Step 5).
