## Responsibility
The FCO Analyst (Financial Crime Operations Analyst) is Responsible for executing targeted client outreach (Step 4) and is Responsible/Accountable for triaging all non-STP review cases (Step 5).

## In this process
Responsible for targeted outreach (Step 4), working the single-thread digital outreach and escalating to RM-mediated channel as fall-back.
