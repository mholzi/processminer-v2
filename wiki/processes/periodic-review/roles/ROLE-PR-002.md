## Responsibility
The Financial Crime Operations Analyst (FCO Analyst) is responsible for executing targeted client outreach (Step 4) and is responsible and accountable for triaging all non-STP review cases (Step 5).

## In this process
R/A for targeted outreach (Step 4): works the Outreach Service request, escalates to RM-mediated fall-back after 30 days non-response. R/A for reviewer triage (Step 5) with 5-day SLA: reviews evidence pack and decides to approve, approve with conditions, refer to EDD, or recommend exit.
