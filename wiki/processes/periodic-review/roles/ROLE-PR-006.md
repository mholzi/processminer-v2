## Responsibility
The STP Decision Engine evaluates each case for straight-through processing eligibility and auto-approves qualifying cases without human intervention, subject to a hard cap of 70% of the eligible book.

## In this process
Consulted during case pre-fill (Step 2) to signal STP eligibility flags. R/A for the STP decision (Step 3): auto-approves cases that are Low or Medium risk, have completeness ≥ 92, carry no open screening hit, were not triggered by an event, and have unchanged product mix.
