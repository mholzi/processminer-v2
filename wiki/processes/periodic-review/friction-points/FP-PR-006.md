## Description
When outreach is needed, the client receives an email with a paper form to complete, scan, and return — and may be required to visit a branch in person. The document names this friction as 'Email + paper form + scan + return-trip to branch.'

## Root cause
The As-Is outreach channel is paper- and email-based with no digital self-service option. The RM emails Compliance Ops a Word cover sheet with no prior check of existing records. Gap G-04 records this as a High-severity Digital gap.

## Client impact
The multi-step physical process — receive email, print form, complete, scan, return or visit branch — creates significant effort. The burden is disproportionate when the bank already holds much of the requested information, and branch attendance is a barrier for non-local clients.
