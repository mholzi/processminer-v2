## Description
At the start of each review cycle, the Relationship Manager calls the client to ask whether anything has changed — identity, address, beneficial ownership, source of wealth — before checking what the bank already holds. The client receives an unsolicited compliance contact with no explanation.

## Root cause
The As-Is process has no central trigger engine and no pre-fill step. The RM works from a monthly Excel extract; when an RM leaves, their clients drop off the list. There is no mechanism to query existing holdings before outreach.

## Client impact
The call is perceived as intrusive and unnecessary, particularly by clients who have had no changes. It erodes trust, consumes client time, and signals that the bank does not use its own data — creating a poor first impression of an unavoidable compliance obligation.
