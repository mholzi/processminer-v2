## Description
Whether a case escalates to the FCO depends on which analyst is on duty, not on a written rule. There is no deterministic escalation logic.

## Impact
Inconsistent application of the 4-eyes principle, as documented in Gap G-03 (No 4-eyes enforcement on High / PEP, severity: High).

## Root cause
Escalation rules are informal and analyst-discretionary; no written criteria and no system enforcement of escalation thresholds.
