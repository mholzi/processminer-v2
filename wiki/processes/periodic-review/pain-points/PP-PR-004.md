## Description
Whether a review case is escalated to the Financial Crime Officer depends on which Compliance Ops analyst is on duty at the time of triage, not on a documented written rule. There is no policy specifying the conditions under which a case must go to 4-eyes FCO sign-off.

## Impact
Inconsistent escalation is recorded as gap G-03 (No 4-eyes enforcement on High/PEP — High severity), with a target close of Q3 2026 for an interim policy and Q1 2027 for system enforcement.

## Root cause
The escalation decision was never formalised in a written policy or encoded in a system rule, making it analyst-dependent and inconsistently applied.
