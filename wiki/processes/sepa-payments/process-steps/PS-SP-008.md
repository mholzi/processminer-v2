## What happens
The Payment Hub generates a pacs.008 ISO 20022 credit-transfer message and submits it to the clearing and settlement mechanism via the CSM Gateway. SCT Inst payments are routed to the RT1 instant gateway; standard SCT payments are submitted to the STEP2 batch gateway for the next available settlement cycle.

## Inputs
- Confirmed debit booking
- Rail selection flag (SCT Inst or standard SCT)
- Payment fields: debtor account, creditor IBAN, creditor name, BIC, amount, remittance reference
- pacs.008 message generated by Payment Hub

## Outputs
- pacs.008 submission to the CSM Gateway
- Submission timestamp for SLA monitoring

## Why it matters
Timely submission to the correct gateway determines whether the beneficiary receives funds within 10 seconds (SCT Inst via RT1) or by the next STEP2 settlement cycle (standard SCT).
