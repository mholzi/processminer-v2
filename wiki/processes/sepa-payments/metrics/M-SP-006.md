## Definition
The daily deadline by which a standard SCT instruction must have cleared validation and screening to qualify for same-day execution via STEP2. Instructions arriving after this point roll to the next business day.

## Current reading
The document states the cut-off as 16:00 CET. No data on adherence rates or volume patterns close to the cut-off is provided.

## Why it matters
The cut-off time is the operational boundary between same-day and D+1 client commitments. Instructions that miss it trigger Exception E-7, rolling to the next cycle with the customer informed of the revised execution date.
