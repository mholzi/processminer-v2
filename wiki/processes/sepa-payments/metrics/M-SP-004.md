## Definition
Elapsed time from identification of a payment exception to its resolution — rejection, release, fallback, or customer notification — across all exception types (E-1 through E-7).

## Current reading
No measured actual is recorded in the source document; only the SLA target is stated.

## Why it matters
Exceptions unresolved beyond one business day risk scheme-deadline breaches for R-transactions, which the document notes must be actioned within scheme deadlines (Exception E-6).
