## Definition
End-to-end elapsed time from the moment the payment instruction is accepted and submitted to the RT1 instant gateway until funds are confirmed available to the beneficiary.

## Current reading
No measured actual is recorded in the source document; only the SLA target is stated.

## Why it matters
The 10-second rule is the defining constraint of the SCT Inst scheme. Breach fires the instant-to-standard fallback exception (E-5), affecting client experience.
