## Responsibility
Drives a single payment through validation, risk, routing, booking and confirmation. Owns the per-payment state machine and the latency budget for each step. The hub's brain.

## Technical detail
Saga pattern over Kafka with PostgreSQL holding the per-payment state row. Step timeouts enforced by a tick scheduler; failed payments fail-closed with a customer-visible reason.
