## Responsibility
Holds the customer balance, processes holds and bookings for SEPA payments, emits the booking-completed events the hub and the notification service consume.

## Technical detail
Temenos Transact native postings with a CDC pipeline that re-emits booking outcomes onto Kafka. The CDC layer is the only new component the core team owns; postings logic itself is unchanged.
