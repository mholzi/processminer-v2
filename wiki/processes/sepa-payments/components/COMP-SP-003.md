## Responsibility
Chooses the SEPA rail (SCT / SCT Inst / SDD Core / SDD B2B) and the CSM endpoint for every validated instruction, based on amount, urgency, beneficiary BIC and customer entitlements.

## Technical detail
Drools rule engine with rules sourced from PostgreSQL and reloadable at runtime. Decision packs are versioned per release; audit trail records which rule pack fired for each routing decision.
