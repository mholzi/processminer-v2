## Responsibility
Receives booking-outcome events and pushes a notification to the customer over their preferred channel (push, in-app, SMS, email) with a 30-second SLA from event to device.

## Technical detail
Channel-specific adapters for FCM, APNS, Twilio and SendGrid. Delivery state captured in Redis with a 30-day TTL for customer-service search. Fallback chain configured per customer preference.
