## Responsibility
Translates between the bank's canonical ISO 20022 form and each CSM endpoint's wire format. Handles MQ for STEP2 batch rails and REST for TIPS-style instant endpoints.

## Technical detail
Hosted in the CSM security zone with no outbound internet access. PostgreSQL replay buffer holds 30 minutes of in-flight messages so a CSM blip does not lose payments. SWIFT Alliance Gateway sits in front.
