## Purpose
Every risk decision — including the feature values that drove it — is written to the audit store so regulators, model-monitoring and customer disputes can trace why a payment was held or blocked.

## Contract details
Topic `risk.decisions.audit`, retained 7 years in Cassandra. Encrypted at rest, accessed only through an audit query API with regulator and compliance role-based access control.

## Failure mode
If the audit topic is unavailable, the risk engine fails-closed and refuses to score — a decision without an audit trail is not allowed by policy or by the regulator.
