## Purpose
The hub hands off outbound clearing messages to the CSM Gateway, which delivers them to STEP2 (SCT, SDD) or RT1 / TIPS (SCT Inst). Inbound clearing messages return through the same gateway.

## Contract details
IBM MQ queues for STEP2; REST over mTLS for TIPS-style endpoints. ISO 20022 pacs.008 outbound and pacs.002 inbound. Routing decision is made by the hub before the hand-off.

## Failure mode
CSM unavailability is the rare case — the gateway buffers up to 30 minutes. Beyond that the hub fails-closed and dispatches a regulator-visible incident notification.
