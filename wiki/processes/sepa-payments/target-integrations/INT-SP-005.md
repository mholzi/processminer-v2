## Purpose
Notification service consumes booking-outcome events from core and pushes a customer notification within seconds. Avoids the channel having to poll for outcome — core's event is the source of truth.

## Contract details
Topic `payments.booking.completed`, schema versioned in the AsyncAPI catalogue. Notification service consumes with a 30-second SLA from event publish to customer-visible push.

## Failure mode
Push failure falls back to SMS, then to email, with delivery state captured for the customer service search. A persistent delivery outage triggers a customer-facing incident notice.
