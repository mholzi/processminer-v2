## Responsibility
Provides the second-approver (4-eyes) sign-off on bulk payment files before they are released for clearing.

## In this process
The Ops Approver holds Accountable and Responsible status for the bulk-file release approval step (ps-6), satisfying control cp-6. For all other steps the role is Informed.
