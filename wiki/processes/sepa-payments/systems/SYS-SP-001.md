## Purpose
Capture and submit outbound payment instructions from customers across online banking, mobile banking, and host-to-host file upload channels.

## Role in this process
Channel Platforms are the entry point for SEPA payment processing. Online and mobile banking receive payment instructions from customers; the host-to-host channel receives bulk payment files (pain.001) from corporates. Instructions are forwarded to the Payment Hub.
