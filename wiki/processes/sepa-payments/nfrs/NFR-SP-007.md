## Definition
Every in-scope SEPA transaction requires Strong Customer Authentication as defined by PSD2 RTS, including the dynamic linking requirement that ties the authentication to the specific amount and beneficiary.

## Measurement
Channel attaches the SCA evidence to every payment instruction; the hub validates the evidence and records the SCA method used. Reported monthly to compliance.

## Verification
Annual external assurance by the PSP audit firm. Continuous internal sample-based review of SCA evidence against the PSD2 RTS criteria.
