## Definition
Every risk decision (verdict and feature values), every booking event and every channel submission stored for at least 7 years in an immutable form accessible to regulators and compliance investigations.

## Measurement
Cassandra cluster with WORM-mode storage and a regulator-facing audit query API. Storage usage and oldest-record age tracked on the platform dashboard.

## Verification
Quarterly audit-trail integrity check by Internal Audit. Annual regulator-led inspection of the immutability controls and the access query trail.
