## Description
Accept, validate, route and confirm a single payment instruction in seconds, end-to-end, so the bank can offer SCT Inst and instant retail flows without falling back to batch.

## Inputs and outputs
Inputs are payment instructions from channels and inbound clearing messages from the CSM. Outputs are debited customer accounts, an outbound ISO 20022 clearing message and a confirmation event to the originating channel.

## Boundaries
Excludes card payments (handled by the card scheme) and intra-day liquidity management (treasury). FX is out of scope — only EUR SEPA rails.
