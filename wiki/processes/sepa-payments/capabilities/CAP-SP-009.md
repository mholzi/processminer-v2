## Description
Match outbound and inbound clearing messages against the bank's own postings and against the CSM's settlement reports, surfacing every break for operations to investigate before the cut-off.

## Inputs and outputs
Inputs are the day's hub events, the core-banking postings and the CSM settlement files. Outputs are matched pairs, a break list with classification and a daily reconciliation summary for finance.

## Boundaries
Excludes nostro reconciliation (treasury) and customer fee reconciliation (billing). Limited to SEPA scheme settlements.
