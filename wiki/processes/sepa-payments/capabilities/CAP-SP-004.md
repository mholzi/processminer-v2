## Description
Score every outbound and inbound payment for fraud risk in real time using behavioural, device and network signals, and present a verdict to the orchestrator within the SCT Inst latency budget.

## Inputs and outputs
Inputs are the instruction, the originating channel context, the customer's recent activity and the live model snapshot. Outputs are a numeric fraud score, a decision band and the top contributing features for audit.

## Boundaries
Does not perform sanctions screening or AML pattern detection — those are sister capabilities. Out of scope: account takeover at login (handled by the channel).
