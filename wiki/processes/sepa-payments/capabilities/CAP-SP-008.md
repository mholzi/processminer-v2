## Description
Push every payment outcome — success, failure, hold-for-review — to the customer over their preferred channel within seconds, so the channel UI never has to poll and a held SCT Inst is never silently dropped.

## Inputs and outputs
Inputs are payment outcome events from the hub. Outputs are push notifications, in-app messages and SMS or email fallbacks, with delivery receipts captured for audit and customer-service search.

## Boundaries
Excludes marketing and product communications. Limited to transactional payment events. Channel-specific rendering (push payload, SMS template) is owned by the notification service.
