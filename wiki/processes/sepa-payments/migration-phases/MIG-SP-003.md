## Scope
Launch SCT Inst on the modernised stack — channel, hub, risk engine, core CDC, notification — for retail customers first, with corporate SCT Inst eligibility expanding through the quarter. 24/7 SCT Inst operations from day one.

## Acceptance criteria
- SCT Inst P99 ≤ 10s end-to-end in production for 30 consecutive days.
- Customer notification reaches devices within 30 seconds of booking.
- 24/7 on-call rota in place with at least one regional failover drill executed.
- Regulator notification of go-live completed.

## Risks
- Real-traffic peaks may exceed the capacity test envelope — capacity must be commissioned ahead of go-live.
- 24/7 on-call needs Financial Crime cover that does not exist today.
- TIPS / RT1 scheme certification windows are unforgiving.
