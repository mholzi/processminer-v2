## Scope
Decommission the legacy Sanctions, Fraud and AML engines, their data feeds and their integration plumbing. Archive their data into the unified audit store. Repurpose or release the on-premises capacity.

## Acceptance criteria
- No live consumer of any of the three legacy engines for 90 consecutive days.
- All historical data archived into the audit store with regulator-facing access verified.
- Vendor contracts wound down with no auto-renewal exposure.

## Risks
- A latent consumer surfaces during the freeze and delays cut-off.
- Vendor contract termination clauses may need legal-led negotiation.
