## Scope
Stand up the Real-Time Risk Engine and migrate sanctions, fraud and AML monitoring onto it. Run in shadow alongside the three legacy engines for one quarter, then cut over by rail (SCT first, SCT Inst readiness in Phase 3).

## Acceptance criteria
- Real-Time Risk Engine processes 100% of SCT volume in shadow with verdict parity > 99.5% against the legacy engines.
- Champion-challenger framework in place for the fraud model.
- AML alerts continuity demonstrated to the Financial Crime team and signed off.

## Risks
- Verdict-parity gap with the legacy fraud engine — likely the schedule risk for the phase.
- AML model lift-and-shift requires the original vendor's cooperation; commercial negotiation needed.
- Operational readiness training for the consolidated team is non-trivial.
