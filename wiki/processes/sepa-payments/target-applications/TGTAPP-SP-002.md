## Rationale
Three legacy engines — sanctions, fraud and AML monitoring — overlap on data and ops, and only sanctions runs synchronously. Consolidate into one streaming risk platform so SCT Inst hits the latency budget.

## Tech stack
Python 3.12 + Go on Kubernetes, Kafka Streams + Flink for the streaming layer, Cassandra for time-series state and PostgreSQL for case management.

## Risks
- Model lift-and-shift from the vendor fraud engine is the schedule risk.
- Model owners are reluctant to lose their toolchain — change-management cost.
- Operational readiness training for the consolidated team is non-trivial.
