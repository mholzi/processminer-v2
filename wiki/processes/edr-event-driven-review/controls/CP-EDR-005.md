That all case decisions and their rationale are logged for audit purposes.

## Control activity
The automated system records every decision made during the EDR along with the documented rationale.

## Risk addressed
Missing or altered decision records that would impair regulatory audit and supervisory review.

## Timing
Continuous; every decision is logged throughout the case lifecycle.
