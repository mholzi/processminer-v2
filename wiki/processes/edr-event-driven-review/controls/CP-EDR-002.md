## What it checks
That every risk-rating change is reviewed and approved by a second person, applying the four-eyes principle.

## Control activity
The Financial Crime Officer reviews and approves every risk rating recalculation produced by the Financial Crime Analyst before the rating is recorded.

## Risk addressed
Inaccurate or biased risk-rating decisions that would mis-categorise a customer's risk profile.

## Timing
Performed for every EDR case that results in a risk rating change.
