## What it checks
That high-risk ratings and exit recommendations receive MLRO sign-off before a case is closed.

## Control activity
The MLRO reviews and approves all cases where the re-assessed risk rating is high or the recommended action is exit, before case closure.

## Risk addressed
High-risk relationships or exit decisions made without adequate senior oversight.

## Timing
Applied to every case where the new risk rating is high or the further action is exit.
