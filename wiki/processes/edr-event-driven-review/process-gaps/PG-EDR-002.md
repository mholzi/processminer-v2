## The gap
The boundary between an open EDR and a Suspicious Activity Report is not defined — it is unclear whether an open EDR pauses SAR considerations.

## Impact
Ambiguity may lead to inconsistent handling of SAR considerations during an active EDR.

## Next step
Define the interaction between EDR and SAR with the SME and document the decision rule.
