## The gap
It is unclear whether low-confidence adverse-media hits should automatically open an EDR or remain under enhanced monitoring; no decision rule is documented.

## Impact
Without a defined threshold, adverse-media hits may be handled inconsistently across analysts.

## Next step
Clarify the adverse-media confidence threshold with the SME and document the decision rule in the process.
