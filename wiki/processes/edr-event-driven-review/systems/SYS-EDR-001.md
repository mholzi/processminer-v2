## Purpose
System of record for EDR cases.

## Role in this process
Used throughout the process to open and close EDR cases. The 2-business-day case-opening SLA is associated with a system control (C-1). Records decisions and rationale (C-5) and triggers automated re-screening before closure (C-4).
