## Responsibility
Second line oversight officer accountable for the re-assessment of customer risk ratings and responsible for deciding on customer retention or exit.

## In this process
Consulted on the CDD refresh activity (step 3); accountable for re-screening (step 4); approves the risk rating re-assessment (step 5); decides further action — retain at standard or enhanced monitoring, or recommend exit (step 6).
