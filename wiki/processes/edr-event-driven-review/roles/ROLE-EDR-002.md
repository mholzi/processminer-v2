## Responsibility
First line of defence analyst accountable for opening EDR cases, conducting initial assessments, and overseeing CDD refresh and risk rating activities.

## In this process
Opens the EDR case (step 1); conducts the initial assessment (step 2); instructs the RM to gather documentation and is accountable for the CDD refresh (step 3); executes re-screening (step 4); recalculates the risk rating (step 5); consulted on the further action decision (step 6); closes the case (step 7).
