## The move
HSBC launched an open API for bank guarantee issuance, enabling partner banks (ING, Standard Bank) to embed guarantee-status visibility and issuance capability directly into their own corporate banking platforms, with HSBC as the executing institution behind the scenes.

## Relevance
Positions HSBC as a backend guarantee factory accessible via API, bypassing manual portal intake and SWIFT-only delivery. Creates direct competitive pressure on the application intake step and SWIFT delivery model, and raises the bar on openness of the Trade Finance System.

## Evidence
GTR report confirmed first live API guarantee transactions between HSBC, ING Bank and Standard Bank; FinTech Futures reported HSBC processes ~500,000 guarantees per year through the API channel.
