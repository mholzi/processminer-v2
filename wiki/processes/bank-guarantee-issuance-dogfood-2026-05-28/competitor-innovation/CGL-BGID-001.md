## The move
HSBC launched a fully straight-through processed demand guarantee via its HSBC Trade Solutions platform, reducing issuance from three to four days to minutes with zero human intervention for standard-wording guarantees. The platform is rolling out across 28 markets through 2024–2025.

## Relevance
Directly targets the same 3-business-day SLA and bespoke-wording bottleneck that define this process's two documented pain points. HSBC's template-driven STP approach sidesteps the Legal wording review for standard instruments, setting a new client expectation benchmark for the entire industry.

## Evidence
First live STP transactions December 2023 (KPMG Qatar tender bond; Russell Finex advance payment guarantee); global rollout to 28 markets in progress.
