## The move
BNP Paribas partnered with Surecomp to process the first bank guarantee under the SWIFT/ICC API guarantee standard in November 2024. The transaction was transmitted in real time from corporate client Vanderlande, received and processed without any manual intervention, and is compatible with ISO 20022 MX.

## Relevance
STP guarantee issuance with zero manual steps is now production-proven at a Tier-1 European bank. This directly challenges the six-step manual/hybrid flow and SWIFT MT760 delivery model, and signals ISO 20022 readiness well ahead of competitors still on MT760.

## Evidence
GTR confirmed first live transaction under the SWIFT/ICC API standard via Surecomp Rivo with BNP Paribas as the receiving bank, November 2024.
