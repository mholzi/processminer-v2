## The move
XDC Ventures acquired and relaunched the Contour trade finance blockchain network in October 2025, pairing it with a new Stablecoin Lab piloting regulated USDC settlement of trade instruments — including guarantees — directly on-chain, targeting banks and corporates in the US, EU and Asia.

## Relevance
Represents the longest-term structural threat to SWIFT MT760-based guarantee delivery: if stablecoin-settled digital guarantees gain regulatory acceptance, the traditional issuance-and-delivery flow collapses into near-real-time on-chain execution, removing manual facility-utilisation updates at delivery.

## Evidence
Acquisition and Stablecoin Lab announced 22 October 2025 (CoinDesk). Confidence low: platform is in pilot/rebuild phase with no confirmed bank go-live on guarantees yet.
