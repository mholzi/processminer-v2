## The move
Commerzbank deployed Surecomp's DOKA-NG as its new core back-office guarantee engine (February 2024) while simultaneously launching Guarantee Vault — a multi-bank digital guarantee platform with Digital Vault Services — offering corporates legally valid domestic digital guarantees with centralised real-time management.

## Relevance
Combines a modernised back-office replacing legacy processing with a digital delivery layer that eliminates paper. The dual move closes the gap on both PP-BGID-001 headroom visibility and PP-BGID-002 wording delays via standardised digital templates and improved client self-service.

## Evidence
TFG confirmed DOKA-NG go-live 8 February 2024; Commerzbank product page confirms Guarantee Vault domestic digital guarantees live with cross-border digital to follow.
