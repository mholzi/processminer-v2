## The move
Surecomp's RIVO SaaS platform, live at LBBW (May 2026), lets corporate clients submit guarantee applications through a single digital portal and uses AWS-native generative AI to auto-parse bespoke wording requests — including legal clauses, expiry and transferability — into structured text, targeting a 30% reduction in processing time.

## Relevance
Directly targets both documented pain points: the manual unSLA'd bespoke-wording Legal review (PP-BGID-002 / PS-BGID-003) and the manual application intake (SYS-BGID-001). RIVO's AI wording check compresses specialist Legal review from days to under 10 seconds for standard clause sets.
