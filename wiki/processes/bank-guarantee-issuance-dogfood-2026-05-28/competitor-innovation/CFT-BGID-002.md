## The move
Enigio's MLETR-compliant trace:original technology, embedded into Oracle Financial Services' core banking platform via a September 2025 partnership, enables banks to issue fully electronic legally equivalent guarantees — delivered via SWIFT or email — without requiring beneficiaries to onboard any new platform.

## Relevance
Attacks the absence of a digital guarantee format in the delivery step (PS-BGID-006 / SYS-BGID-004). Because trace:original is interoperable with SWIFT and requires no counterparty onboarding, it offers a lower-friction adoption path than earlier blockchain consortia.

## Evidence
Oracle-Enigio partnership announced 22 September 2025; Enigio won Best Fintech at GTR Leaders in Trade Awards 2025, with customers reporting up to 90% cost savings and 80% less manual work.
