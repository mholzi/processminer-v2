## The move
Citi's Token Services for Trade replaces bank guarantees and letters of credit with smart contracts backed by tokenized deposits on a private permissioned blockchain, enabling 24/7 automated settlement in minutes once contractual conditions are met. First live transaction completed October 2024.

## Relevance
Citi is eliminating the manual issuance approval and SWIFT MT760 transmission steps that form the final two steps of this process, and bypassing facility limit friction by settling against tokenized cash. This represents an architectural threat to the conventional guarantee workflow over the medium term.

## Evidence
Citi press release 10 October 2024; pilot with CB Fenton and GAC Panama Shipping; platform on private Citi-owned blockchain requiring no token management by clients.
