## The idea
Deploy a GenAI wording classification engine at the Wording Review step to automatically assess bespoke guarantee text against the bank's approved clause library. Standard clause combinations are auto-approved without Legal review; only genuinely novel wording is routed to Legal — with an automated escalation trigger when the 2-business-day informal target is breached.

## Expected benefit
Reduces Legal review volume to only novel wording, cutting the average delay for bespoke guarantees and introducing a formal escalation mechanism that today does not exist (PP-BGID-002). Faster turnaround improves client satisfaction and SLA attainment.

## Feasibility
Proven by Surecomp RIVO at LBBW (May 2026), achieving sub-10-second accuracy matching a seasoned expert. Requires integration with the TFS wording library and Legal team ticketing workflow. Data-privacy review needed for SaaS option; alternatively deployable on-prem alongside Finastra TFS.
