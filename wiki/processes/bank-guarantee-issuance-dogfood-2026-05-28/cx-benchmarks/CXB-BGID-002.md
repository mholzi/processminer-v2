## The benchmark
83% of corporate clients rate real-time financial forecasting and status visibility as high value, yet only 31% of banks share that assessment — a 52-percentage-point perception gap. Clients consistently experience friction in bank integration, credit access, and real-time data availability.

## Relevance
Bank guarantee applicants currently receive no real-time status updates between submission and SWIFT transmission. The 52-point gap signals that the absence of live tracking is a structural dissatisfier, not a minor convenience issue, and a primary driver of multi-banking despite 97% headline satisfaction.

## Evidence
CGI Transaction Banking Survey 2026 (713 respondents, February 2026): 83% of corporates view real-time forecasting as high value vs. 31% of banks; 97% satisfied with their primary bank yet 79% added more banking partners in the past year.
