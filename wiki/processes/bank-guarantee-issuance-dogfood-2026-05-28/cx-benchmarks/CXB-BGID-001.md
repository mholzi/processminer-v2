## The benchmark
Best-in-class digital trade finance transactions now complete in approximately 24 hours. A fully digital documentary collection that previously took 15 days — requiring six courier trips between India and the UK — was completed by Lloyds Bank in just over 24 hours once paper was eliminated.

## Relevance
Bank guarantee issuance involving physical documentation and manual approval loops routinely takes 3–5 working days. The 24-hour bar set by digital trade-document workflows is the reference point corporate clients increasingly apply to all trade finance instruments, including guarantees.

## Evidence
Lloyds Bank/WaveBL case study, April 2024: a previous trade took 15 days and required couriering documentation six times between India and the UK; the digital equivalent was completed in just over 24 hours.
