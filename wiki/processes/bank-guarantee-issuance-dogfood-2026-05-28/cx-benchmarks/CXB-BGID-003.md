## The benchmark
38% of trade banks have made front-office portal modernisation a top investment priority — an 11-point jump year-on-year — while 39% are simultaneously upgrading back-office platforms. The industry consensus is that digital self-service portals are now a competitive necessity, not a differentiator.

## Relevance
Corporate clients applying for bank guarantees through a portal expect the same friction-free digital experience they receive in payments and FX. The 11-point acceleration in front-office investment signals that banks failing to modernise their guarantee application and status interfaces risk losing mandates to digitally-enabled competitors.

## Evidence
CGI / BAFT Trade Technology Survey 2025 (fifth annual edition): 38% of banks prioritising front-office modernisation (up 11 points); 55% investing in intelligent process automation; 39% upgrading back-office platforms.
