## The benchmark
Paper-based trade finance processes add up to 20% to transaction costs and cause average cross-border trade to take 10–14 additional days due to manual document processing and approvals. A single trade transaction can involve up to 240 pages of documentation across 27 parties.

## Relevance
Bank guarantee issuance remains heavily paper-dependent — application forms, board resolutions, indemnity agreements and SWIFT confirmations are often couriered or scanned. The cost and delay benchmarks quantify what corporate clients effectively absorb when a bank has not digitised its guarantee workflow end-to-end.
