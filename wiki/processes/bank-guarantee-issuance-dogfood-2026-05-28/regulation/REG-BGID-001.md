## What it requires
Every beneficiary and beneficiary country must be screened against applicable sanctions lists before a guarantee is issued.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantees involve the bank creating a direct payment obligation to a third-party beneficiary, which creates exposure to sanctions evasion and potential misuse for financial crime. EU sanctions regulations and AML directives apply to all cross-border and domestic trade finance instruments.

## How it is met
The Compliance Analyst screens every beneficiary via the Sanctions Screening Tool before issuance (PS-BGID-004 / CP-BGID-002). A screening hit suspends the application pending a Compliance Operations investigation.
