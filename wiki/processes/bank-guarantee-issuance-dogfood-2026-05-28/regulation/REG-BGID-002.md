## What it requires
URDG 758 requires the guarantee to state the amount, expiry (fixed date or defined event), presentation requirements, and demand-compliance terms. The bank's payment obligation is independent of the underlying commercial contract, triggered by a compliant demand.

## Why it applies
Applies when the guarantee text incorporates URDG 758 by reference — the standard template does this by default. If parties substitute domestic law or ISP98, URDG 758 does not apply. Covers all URDG-incorporated demand guarantees, not all guarantees universally.

## How it is met
The standard wording template incorporates URDG 758 by reference; the Trade Finance System's wording library is the authoritative source. Legal verifies the incorporation clause is present when reviewing bespoke wording at PS-BGID-003.
