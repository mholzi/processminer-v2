## The trend
AI-powered intelligent document processing and agentic automation are achieving over 96% straight-through processing rates on structured trade finance instruments, with processing times reduced from days to minutes. GenAI is increasingly applied to unstructured guarantee wording to automate classification and compliance checks without manual intervention.

## Relevance
Both documented pain points involve manual hand-offs — facility shortfall routing to Credit (PP-BGID-001) and bespoke wording to Legal (PP-BGID-002). AI-driven pre-screening and automated wording classification could eliminate or shorten these stall points and improve the 85% SLA attainment rate.

## Evidence
Agentic trade finance systems report 96%+ STP rates and five-times cost reduction versus OCR and BPO platforms, per Klearstack's 2026 guide.
