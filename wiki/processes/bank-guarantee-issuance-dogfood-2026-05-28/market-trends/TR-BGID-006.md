## The trend
At Sibos 2025, Finastra unveiled Trade Innovation Nexus, a REST API integration layer enabling banks to migrate from on-premise Trade Innovation deployments to cloud-ready, interoperable architectures with real-time processing and open API connectivity for guarantee and LC workflows.

## Relevance
The process runs on an on-premise Finastra Trade Innovation instance. Nexus is Finastra's strategic cloud direction; as peer banks migrate, on-premise will receive less investment. A migration path would re-integrate the Corporate Portal, Fircosoft and SWIFT connections and creates an opportunity to re-engineer the manual steps driving PP-BGID-001 and PP-BGID-002.

## Evidence
BNI migrated nine international Finastra Trade Innovation locations to a single Nexus instance in January 2026, demonstrating viability of large-scale consolidation on the cloud platform.
