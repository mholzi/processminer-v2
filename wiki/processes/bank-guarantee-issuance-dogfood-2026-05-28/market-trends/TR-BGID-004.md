## The trend
SWIFT's ISO 20022 MX migration continues with a November 2026 hard deadline requiring all financial institutions to receive Enquiry and Investigation messages in MX format. Trade finance MT categories, including MT760, face progressive displacement by structured MX equivalents over the migration runway.

## Relevance
The process delivers guarantees via SWIFT MT760. Although MT760 has no published sunset date yet, the ISO 20022 roadmap item noted in the process creates an active migration dependency requiring TFS upgrade planning and system readiness work before deadlines tighten further.

## Evidence
By November 2025, 97% of SWIFT payment instructions used ISO 20022. November 2026 deadline tightens structured-address requirements further across all message categories.
