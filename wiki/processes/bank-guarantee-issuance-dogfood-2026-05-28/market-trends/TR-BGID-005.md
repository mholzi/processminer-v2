## The trend
DORA entered into full application across EU financial institutions on 17 January 2025, mandating documented ICT risk management, binding contractual controls over all third-party technology providers, and tested incident-response plans. Nineteen critical ICT providers were placed under direct supervisory oversight by November 2025.

## Relevance
The process depends on four named systems: Corporate Portal (in-house), Finastra Trade Innovation (on-prem vendor), Fircosoft (sanctions screening vendor) and SWIFT. DORA requires binding contractual resilience terms with each vendor and tested recovery within the documented RTO/RPO bands.

## Evidence
DORA applies from 17 January 2025; 19 critical ICT providers came under direct EU oversight November 2025, with on-premise vendor relationships also subject to third-party obligations.
