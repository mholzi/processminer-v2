## The trend
EBA Guidelines EBA/GL/2024/14 came into force 30 December 2025, imposing for the first time harmonised EU standards on sanctions screening governance for all financial institutions, including mandatory annual system calibration, evidenced list-update frequency testing, and documented escalation procedures for positive matches.

## Relevance
The process uses Fircosoft for hybrid automated-plus-manual sanctions screening at step 4. The new calibration and annual-testing obligation requires the bank to formally evidence that Fircosoft's matching parameters are optimally tuned — a gap likely to surface at the next supervisory review.

## Evidence
EBA/GL/2024/14 effective 30 December 2025 mandates annual review of screening systems and immediate reporting of significant weaknesses — the first EU-wide sanctions screening standard.
