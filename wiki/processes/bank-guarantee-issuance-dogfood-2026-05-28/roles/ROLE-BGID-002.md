## Responsibility
Reviews the assembled guarantee package and approves issuance, satisfying the four-eyes control requirement.

## In this process
Performs and is accountable for Issuance Approval (PS-BGID-005) for all guarantees, recording the approval in the Trade Finance System. Is the control owner of the four-eyes issuance approval control (CP-BGID-001).
