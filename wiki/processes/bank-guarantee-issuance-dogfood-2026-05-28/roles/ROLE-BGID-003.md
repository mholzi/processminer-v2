Provides additional sign-off for guarantees above EUR 5 million.

Accountable for the additional sign-off at Issuance Approval (PS-BGID-005) when the guarantee amount exceeds EUR 5 million. Sign-off is mandatory in those cases and is recorded in the Trade Finance System.
