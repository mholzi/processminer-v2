## Responsibility
Screens the beneficiary and beneficiary's country against the sanctions list for every guarantee application; a screening hit suspends the application and triggers a hand-off to Compliance Operations for investigation.

## In this process
Performs and is accountable for Sanctions and Compliance Screening (PS-BGID-004) using the Sanctions Screening Tool; attaches the screening result to the application. On a hit, hands the suspended application off to Compliance Operations who own the investigation. Is the control owner of the sanctions screening control (CP-BGID-002).
