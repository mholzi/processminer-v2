## Responsibility
Handles application intake and completeness checks, wording review routing, and guarantee generation and delivery across the core steps of the process.

## In this process
Performs Application Intake (PS-BGID-001), verifying application completeness. Runs Credit and Facility Check (PS-BGID-002), confirming available limit. Routes Wording Review (PS-BGID-003) — standard wording passes directly to screening, bespoke wording is handed off to Legal for review and sign-off. Carries out Guarantee Generation and Delivery (PS-BGID-006) in the Trade Finance System, with the guarantee transmitted to the beneficiary's bank via SWIFT.
