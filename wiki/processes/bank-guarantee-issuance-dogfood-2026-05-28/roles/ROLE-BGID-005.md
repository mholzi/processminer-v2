## Responsibility
Decides the response to insufficient-facility applications routed from Credit and Facility Check: approves a temporary limit uplift via Credit Risk Committee, requires additional client collateral, or declines the increase if the credit profile no longer supports it.

## In this process
Responsible at PS-BGID-002 when the facility limit is insufficient: approves a temporary uplift via Credit Risk Committee (same-day within delegated authority, 1–3 business days otherwise), requires additional client collateral arranged by the relationship manager, or declines. Does not amend the underlying facility — that is a separate process. On sign-off, the application re-enters PS-BGID-002.
