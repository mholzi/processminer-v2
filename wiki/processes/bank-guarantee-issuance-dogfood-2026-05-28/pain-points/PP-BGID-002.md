## Description
When a client requests bespoke guarantee wording, the draft must be sent to the Legal team for review and sign-off before issuance can continue. This Legal review step has no committed turnaround time.

## Impact
The absence of a Legal turnaround SLA introduces unpredictable delay into the issuance process. The 3-business-day SLA target is measured only for standard-wording guarantees. When Legal overruns the informal 2-business-day expectation, there is no formal escalation path to unblock the application.

## Root cause
No service-level agreement exists between Trade Finance and the Legal team for wording reviews, and there is no escalation trigger when reviews take longer than expected.
