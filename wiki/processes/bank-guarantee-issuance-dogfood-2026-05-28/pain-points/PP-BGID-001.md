## Description
Applications stall at the credit and facility check when the client has not pre-arranged sufficient guarantee facility limit. The Trade Finance Officer must park the application and route it to the Credit team before issuance can proceed.

## Impact
Affects approximately 15–20% of applications — the single biggest delay cause by volume. It suspends progress and extends turnaround time beyond the 3-business-day SLA target.

## Root cause
Clients do not proactively manage their guarantee facility headroom, and there is no early-warning mechanism to alert them when limit is approaching capacity before they submit an application.
