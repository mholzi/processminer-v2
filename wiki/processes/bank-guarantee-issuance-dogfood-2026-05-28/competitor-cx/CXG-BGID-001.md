## The journey
Corporate clients apply for demand guarantees through HSBC Trade Solutions, a redesigned portal that pre-populates application data and automates validation checks end to end. Straight-through processing with zero human intervention reduces issuance from three to four days down to minutes; the guarantee is transmitted to the beneficiary in real time.

## Relevance
The speed gap is stark: a process peer banks still run in three-to-four business days HSBC can now deliver in minutes for pre-approved clients. Any bank still relying on manual review and SWIFT batching will lose mandates from time-sensitive tendering and contracting corporates.

## Evidence
First live STP transactions were a Tender Bond for KPMG Qatar and Advance Payment guarantee for Russell Finex Ltd, completed December 2023 — ‘something that can take three to four days was done in a matter of minutes’.
