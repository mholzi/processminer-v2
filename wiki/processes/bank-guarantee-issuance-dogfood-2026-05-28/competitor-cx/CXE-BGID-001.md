## The journey
Corporate clients access guarantee issuance through Deutsche Bank’s Autobahn App Market, a single digital gateway housing Trade Finance alongside cash management. The Trade Finance app offers a dedicated fast-track channel for guarantee and standby letter of credit business, allowing clients to submit, track and manage guarantees within the portal without switching systems.

## Relevance
The fast-track guarantee channel inside a unified corporate portal sets a benchmark for straight-through digital submission with no paper or email steps. Any process lacking a comparable self-service application channel creates friction against this experience standard, particularly for repeat corporate issuers.

## Evidence
Deutsche Bank Autobahn product page states the Trade Finance app is designed to 'fast track your guarantee and standby letter of credit business'; confirmed by the YouTube video 'flow – Guarantee Business – Autobahn Trade Finance App' (youtube.com/watch?v=97lr8psQ6bY).
