## The journey
Corporate clients initiate bank guarantees via API from their ERP or treasury systems into Standard Chartered’s Straight2Bank platform, bypassing manual portal entry. The bank is among the first globally to operationalise ICC-SWIFT API standards for guarantees in a live client workflow, eliminating paper handling end to end and providing real-time lifecycle visibility to all counterparties.

## Relevance
Clients who operate multi-bank treasury platforms can instruct guarantee issuance without logging into a bank portal at all. Banks that require manual portal submission impose an integration friction that best-in-class API connectivity removes, creating a switching cost for corporates with high guarantee volumes.

## Evidence
First live transaction was completed via Komgo's Konsole for a global energy company — 'This integration automates the receipt of instructions and accelerates the issuance of digitally initiated bank guarantees, offering clients greater speed, transparency and reliability.'
