## The journey
RIVO is a white-label SaaS portal that banks deploy for corporate clients; the corporate submits a guarantee application entirely online, tracks progress in real time, receives the instrument digitally, and manages amendments or extensions — all through the bank's branded interface connected to the bank's back office via API.

## Relevance
Banks such as LBBW, BBVA, and DZ Bank have gone live with RIVO, meaning their corporate clients now expect fully automated, end-to-end digital guarantee issuance with real-time status transparency — a standard that positions banks without a comparable portal as operationally inferior.

## Evidence
LBBW went live with RIVO in May 2026 to provide a 'fully automated, centralized solution for the initiation, processing and management' of guarantees and LCs; BBVA adopted RIVO in November 2025 for multi-bank trade finance digitisation.
