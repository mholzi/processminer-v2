## The journey
Corporate clients submit guarantee requests and manage the full lifecycle through UniCredit's Trade Finance Gate (UC TFG) via the UniCredit Corporate Portal. The platform consolidates domestic and international guarantees, provides real-time status monitoring with push notifications, and supports multi-bank EBICS data exchange for clients spanning multiple institutions.

## Relevance
Consolidating domestic and international guarantees into one real-time portal with push notifications removes the need for clients to chase status updates by phone or email. This transparency benchmark exposes any process where clients lack a live view of their guarantee pipeline from application through SWIFT transmission.

## Evidence
UniCredit UC TFG Quick Guide describes Trade Finance operations in a 'time-saving, forward-looking and fully digitalized environment'; clients can 'monitor the status of their deals in real time, receive notifications for changes and developments of existing transactions'.
