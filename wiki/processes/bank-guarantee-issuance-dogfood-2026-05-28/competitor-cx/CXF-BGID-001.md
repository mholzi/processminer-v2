## The journey
Komgo's GTK lets a corporate treasurer submit guarantee applications, track status in real time, and manage the full instrument lifecycle — amendments, expiries, claims — across all banking relationships from a single web interface, connected via ICC-Swift API standards, Konsole APIs, and SWIFT channels simultaneously.

## Relevance
A corporate that banks with multiple institutions no longer needs to log in to each bank's separate portal or dispatch paper applications; GTK collapses that fragmentation into one structured workflow, raising the bar for what corporate clients expect from a bank's own digital guarantee channel.

## Evidence
Standard Chartered completed the first live ICC-Swift API bank guarantee transaction through Komgo Konsole in January 2026; Euromoney named Komgo the world's most innovative trade-finance software provider for 2025.
