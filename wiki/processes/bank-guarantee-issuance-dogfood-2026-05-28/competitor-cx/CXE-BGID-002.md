## The journey
Corporate clients apply for, amend, and track bank guarantees through ING's InsideBusiness Trade (IBT) portal, available 24/7. Clients initiate requests online, set up real-time progress alerts, and monitor their full guarantee portfolio in a single overview backed by API-driven back-end integration that eliminates media breaks between client and bank.

## Relevance
IBT's always-on self-service application and amendment capability — backed by API-driven back-end integration that removes manual re-keying — raises the bar on speed and transparency. Processes still reliant on email or paper instructions for amendments suffer a visible experience gap relative to this model.

## Evidence
ING product page states IBT is 'available 24/7 for viewing, applying, and making amendments to trade products'; Komgo case study quotes ING Product Manager: 'The platform has significantly enhanced the digital client experience by streamlining processes and reducing turnaround times.'
