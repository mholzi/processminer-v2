## Purpose
Client-facing web portal that captures bank guarantee applications submitted by corporate customers or their relationship managers.

## Role in this process
Used in PS-BGID-001 to receive guarantee applications. Clients enter beneficiary details, guarantee amount, currency, wording type, validity period and the commercial contract reference. Relationship managers may also key applications on behalf of clients. If unavailable, RMs can fall back to direct Trade Finance System keying.
