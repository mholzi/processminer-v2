## Definition
Elapsed business days from receipt of a complete application to delivery of the executed guarantee, measured for standard-wording guarantees with no screening hit.

## Current reading
~85% of standard-wording guarantees delivered within the 3-business-day target (TFS ops dashboard). The 15% miss rate correlates strongly with facility shortfalls. Trend: flat over the last 6 months.

## Why it matters
Turnaround time is the primary client-facing SLA for this process. Misses risk client relationship damage and repeat business loss — corporate clients compare guarantee turnaround across banks. Facility shortfalls and bespoke wording are the known pressure points.
