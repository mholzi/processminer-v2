## What happens
The Trade Finance Officer receives the guarantee application and checks it is complete. The completeness check covers beneficiary details, guarantee amount, currency, wording type (standard or bespoke), validity period and the underlying commercial contract reference.

## Inputs
- Guarantee application submitted via the Corporate Portal
- Beneficiary details
- Guarantee amount and currency
- Wording type (standard or bespoke)
- Validity period
- Underlying commercial contract reference

## Outputs
- Validated, complete application record ready for credit and facility check
- Confirmed wording type routing flag (standard or bespoke)

## Why it matters
Catching incomplete applications at intake prevents rework downstream and ensures the credit, compliance and legal teams receive a package with all the information they need to proceed without back-and-forth.
