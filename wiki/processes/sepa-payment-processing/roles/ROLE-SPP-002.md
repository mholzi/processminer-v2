## Responsibility
Runs the payment through validation, funds checking, clearing submission and customer confirmation, performs end-of-day reconciliation against CSM settlement, and is accountable for handling R-transactions.

## In this process
Responsible for validating instructions, funds checks, releasing bulk files, submitting payments to the clearing mechanism and confirming execution to the customer; runs end-of-day reconciliation against CSM settlement reports and investigates any break; consulted on sanctions, AML and fraud screening; accountable and responsible for R-transaction handling.
