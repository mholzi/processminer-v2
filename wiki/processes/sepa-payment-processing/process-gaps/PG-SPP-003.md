## The gap
It is not documented who approves a bulk payment file when it exceeds the corporate daily aggregate limit.

## Impact
The approval path for high-value bulk files is unclear, leaving a possible control gap over large aggregate exposures.

## Next step
Confirm with the SME who approves bulk files above the corporate daily aggregate limit.
