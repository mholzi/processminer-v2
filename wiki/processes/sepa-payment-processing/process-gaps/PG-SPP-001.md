## The gap
It is not documented whether the EUR 100,000 SCT Inst limit applied at the routing decision is a bank-set limit or the SEPA scheme maximum.

## Impact
Without knowing the basis of the limit, the bank cannot tell whether it has room to raise the instant threshold in the target design.

## Next step
Confirm with the SME whether the EUR 100,000 SCT Inst limit is a bank-set limit or the scheme maximum.
