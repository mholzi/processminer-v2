## Definition
The end-to-end time for an SCT Inst payment, from instruction to funds being available to the beneficiary.

## Current reading
The target is funds available to the beneficiary within 10 seconds; this document records no measured actual.

## Why it matters
The 10-second SCT Inst rule is the scheme promise to the customer; meeting it is what makes a payment genuinely instant.
