## Definition
The daily deadline by which a standard SCT must be ready to make the current processing cycle.

## Current reading
The cut-off is set at 16:00 CET; this document records no measured actual.

## Why it matters
Payments ready after the cut-off roll to the next cycle or business day, moving the customer's expected execution date.
