## What happens
The hub generates a pacs.008 message and submits it to the clearing and settlement mechanism: the instant gateway (RT1) for SCT Inst payments, or the batch gateway (STEP2) for standard SCT payments.

## Inputs
- Funded payment with its rail selection
- pacs.008 message generated by the hub

## Outputs
- pacs.008 submission lodged with the CSM
- Payment handed to RT1 or STEP2 for settlement

## Why it matters
Submission to the CSM hands the payment to the SEPA scheme for clearing and settlement; the gateway used must match the rail chosen at routing.
