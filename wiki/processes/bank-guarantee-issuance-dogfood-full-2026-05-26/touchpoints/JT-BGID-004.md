## What the client does
The client receives a portal notification that their bank guarantee application has been formally approved and is advancing to generation and delivery.

## What the bank does
A portal notification is triggered automatically once the four-eyes issuance approval is recorded in the Trade Finance System; no manual notification action is required from the trade-finance officer.

## Experience
The notification is welcomed but typically brief; clients appreciate the confirmation of approval yet commonly lack any indication of the expected issuance date or whether further action is needed from them.
