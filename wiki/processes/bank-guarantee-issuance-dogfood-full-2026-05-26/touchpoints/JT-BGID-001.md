## What the client does
The client logs into the Corporate Portal and submits a bank guarantee application, filling in beneficiary details, guarantee amount, currency, wording type, validity period and the underlying commercial contract reference.

## What the bank does
The Trade Finance Officer receives the submission, validates completeness and returns any incomplete application via the portal with a checklist identifying the missing fields.

## Experience
The portal provides a structured form, but clients commonly discover missing fields only after submission. The resulting back-and-forth feels slow and error-prone, particularly for applicants without an experienced relationship manager guiding them.
