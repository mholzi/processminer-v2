## What the client does
The client awaits the bank's decision on their proposed guarantee wording — receiving either confirmation of the approved text or a redlined counter-proposal from Legal for their review and acceptance.

## What the bank does
The trade-finance desk emails the Legal review outcome — an approval confirmation or revised wording draft — and requests the client's sign-off before advancing the application to issuance approval.

## Experience
This is the highest-anxiety touchpoint for clients with bespoke wording requirements: the wait is undefined, no interim status updates are provided, and the client cannot estimate the issuance date for their underlying commercial transaction.
