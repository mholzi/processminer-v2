## What the client does
The client receives confirmation that the bank guarantee has been issued and the MT760 transmitted to the beneficiary's bank; they notify their counterparty that the guarantee is in place.

## What the bank does
The trade-finance officer notifies the client of the MT760 transmission, providing the guarantee reference number, value date and the beneficiary bank's SWIFT BIC as confirmation of delivery.

## Experience
This is the resolution touchpoint — clients feel relief and can advance their commercial transaction. However, they commonly experience a silent gap: no confirmation that the beneficiary's bank has acknowledged the MT760 arrives, leaving the final link unverified.
