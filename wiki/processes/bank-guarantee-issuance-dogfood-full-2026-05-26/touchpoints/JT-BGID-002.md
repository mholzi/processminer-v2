## What the client does
The client speaks with their relationship manager to clarify the guarantee request, confirm guarantee type and structure, or hand over the application for portal entry on their behalf.

## What the bank does
The RM acknowledges the request, validates the guarantee type and required documentation, and — where the client has not used the portal — keys the application into the Trade Finance System on the client's behalf.

## Experience
For clients with an established RM relationship the call is brief and confidence-building; those without a dedicated RM find this channel less accessible and may fall back to unsupported email communication with the trade-finance desk.
