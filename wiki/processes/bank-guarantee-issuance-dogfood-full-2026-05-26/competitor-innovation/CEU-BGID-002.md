## The move
BNP Paribas deployed an AI and OCR platform across its Trade Finance operations — covering guarantees, documentary credit, and short-term financing — now live in 15 countries and processing close to one million printed documents a year, accelerating data capture and AML screening.

## Relevance
Automated first-pass document reading compresses the wording-review and compliance-check cycle that causes bespoke-guarantee delays (PP-BGID-002) and lack of committed turnaround (FP-BGID-001), setting a competitive benchmark for AI-assisted guarantee processing speed.

## Evidence
BNP Paribas Fortis Head of Global Trade Solutions confirmed the deployment in May 2024; the programme spans guarantees as one of three core Trade Finance domains across 15 countries.
