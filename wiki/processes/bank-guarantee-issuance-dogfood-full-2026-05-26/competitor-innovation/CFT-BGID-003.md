## The move
Traydstream's TraydCheck uses OCR, NLP, and ML trained on over 300,000 trade-rule permutations to scrutinise guarantee documents against regulatory and policy rules, flagging discrepancies and routing exceptions — compressing a four-to-ten-hour manual check to under three minutes for compliant transactions.

## Relevance
Attacks FP-BGID-001 and PP-BGID-002 by making sub-SLA document scrutiny mechanically enforceable; also catches CG-BGID-001 and PP-BGID-003 completeness gaps at the document-check stage before they reach credit approval.

## Evidence
TraydCheck product page confirms sub-3-minute scrutiny; Erste Group partnership announced at Sibos October 2024; EUR 9.3M funding closed August 2025 to scale ML capabilities globally.
