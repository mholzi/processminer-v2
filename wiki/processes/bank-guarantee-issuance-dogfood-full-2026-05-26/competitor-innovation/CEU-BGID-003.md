## The move
ABN AMRO went live on CBA's IBAS Global Trade Finance Factory, routing all trade finance payments — including counter-guarantees and standby LCs — through a central Payment Hub fully compliant with ISO 20022 CBPR+, with RESTful APIs enabling structured client onboarding and transaction submission.

## Relevance
Structured API-driven application submission directly targets the incomplete-intake problem (PP-BGID-003, CG-BGID-001); ISO 20022 compliance and automated routing address facility-utilisation tracking delays linked to PP-BGID-001.

## Evidence
ABN AMRO confirmed the IBAS GTF go-live in July 2023 via FF News; deployment covers counter-guarantees and standby LC processing as named capability areas.
