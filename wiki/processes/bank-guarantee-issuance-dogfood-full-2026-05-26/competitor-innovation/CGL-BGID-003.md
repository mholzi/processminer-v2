## The move
Barclays announced in September 2025 integration of CGI Trade360 with Komgo's Konsole so that corporate client instructions flow directly into Barclays' back-office systems, eliminating manual re-keying and reducing operating risk across the trade finance lifecycle.

## Relevance
Eliminating manual intake re-keying closes the structural gap of CG-BGID-001 and PP-BGID-003 for incomplete applications; automated instruction flow also shortens the facility pre-check cycle relevant to PP-BGID-001 and reduces the queue ahead of wording review (FP-BGID-001).
