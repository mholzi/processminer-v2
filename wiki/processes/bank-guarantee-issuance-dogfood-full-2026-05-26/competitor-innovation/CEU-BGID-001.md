## The move
Commerzbank, in partnership with Digital Vault Services GmbH, launched the Guarantee Vault Platform — a centralised portal where corporate clients with multiple banking relationships submit, amend, and monitor guarantee orders digitally, with real-time exposure visibility and 24/7 self-service access.

## Relevance
The platform enforces structured digital order submission, eliminating the incomplete-application problem (CG-BGID-001) and creating an auditable issuance workflow that implicitly commits to processing timelines, closing the SLA gap documented as FP-BGID-001.

## Evidence
Commerzbank's product page describes self-service issuance, modification, and real-time visibility; Digital Vault Services claims it is the first and only fully end-to-end digital corporate guarantee platform.
