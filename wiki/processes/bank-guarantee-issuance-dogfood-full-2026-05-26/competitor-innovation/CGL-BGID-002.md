## The move
HSBC joined Microsoft, ANZ, and Lloyds in a generative-AI proof of concept — demonstrated at Sibos 2025 and published April 2026 — where an AI agent auto-parses trade finance instruments, extracts structured data fields, cross-checks them against client ERP data, and flags discrepancies before bank submission.

## Relevance
Automating pre-submission document cross-checking directly addresses CG-BGID-001 (no intake completeness enforcement) and FP-BGID-001 (no wording-review SLA); catching discrepancies at intake also shrinks the bespoke legal review cycle driving PP-BGID-002 delays.

## Evidence
Microsoft Cloud Blog, 20 April 2026: HSBC PoC uses AI to parse trade documents, cross-check ERP data, and flag discrepancies, aligned to ICC KTDDE standards; demonstrated at Sibos 2025.
