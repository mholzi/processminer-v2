## The move
Komgo's GTK platform embeds AI to extract clauses from guarantee requests, auto-populate draft wording, compare clauses against bank policy libraries, and flag discrepancies in real time — compressing manual wording review from days to minutes across a multi-bank, API-connected workflow.

## Relevance
Directly attacks PP-BGID-002 and FP-BGID-001: AI-assisted clause checking eliminates the unstructured legal-team queue that stalls guarantee issuance, while intake field flagging addresses CG-BGID-001 by catching missing fields at submission.

## Evidence
Komgo GTK launched April 2025; TK Elevator case study reports 70% of a 12,000-guarantee annual portfolio now processed via GTK automation; Euromoney named Komgo world's most innovative trade finance software provider 2025.
