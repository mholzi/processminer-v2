## The move
Surecomp's RIVO AI Email Reader auto-ingests guarantee applications submitted by email, parses PDF attachments, extracts all required fields — applicant, beneficiary, amount, expiry, legal terms — and creates a complete transaction record without manual re-keying, targeting a 30% reduction in processing time.

## Relevance
Directly addresses PP-BGID-003 and CG-BGID-001: automated field extraction enforces completeness before the application enters the bank's queue, removing the rework loop that delays issuance start and eliminating the missing-contract-reference round-trip.

## Evidence
Surecomp committed to 30% processing-time reduction in November 2024; RIVO AI Email Reader is a live feature; Barmag selected RIVO in December 2025 citing elimination of manual Excel-and-email workflows.
