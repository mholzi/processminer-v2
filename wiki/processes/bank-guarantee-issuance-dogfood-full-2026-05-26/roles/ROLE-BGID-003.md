## Responsibility
Provides additional approval authority for high-value guarantees above EUR 5 million.

## In this process
The Head of Trade Finance is consulted at Step 5 (Issuance Approval) for any guarantee above EUR 5 million, providing a mandatory additional sign-off before the guarantee can be issued.
