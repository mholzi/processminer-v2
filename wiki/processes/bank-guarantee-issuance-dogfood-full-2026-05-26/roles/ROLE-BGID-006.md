## Responsibility
Reviews and signs off bespoke guarantee wording before the guarantee is issued.

## In this process
The Legal Team is responsible and accountable for Step 3 (Wording Review) when the guarantee uses bespoke (non-standard) wording. Standard-wording guarantees bypass the Legal Team entirely. Legal review has no committed turnaround, which introduces unpredictable delay for bespoke applications.
