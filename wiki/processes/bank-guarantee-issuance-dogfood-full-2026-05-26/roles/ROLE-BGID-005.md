## Responsibility
Handles cases where a client's approved guarantee facility is insufficient to cover a new application.

## In this process
The Credit Team is consulted at Step 2 (Credit and Facility Check) when the client does not hold sufficient available limit to cover the guarantee amount. The application is parked and routed to the Credit Team — the most common reason for delay in this process.
