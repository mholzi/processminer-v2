## Responsibility
Executes the operational steps of the guarantee issuance process — intake, completeness check, wording review coordination (including Legal liaison for bespoke guarantees), collateral confirmation for partially-secured guarantees, guarantee generation and delivery.

## In this process
The Trade Finance Officer owns Steps 1 (Application Intake), 2 (Credit and Facility Check), 3 (Wording Review), the new Collateral Confirmation step, and Step 7 (Guarantee Generation and Delivery). The executed guarantee is transmitted via SWIFT and the client's facility utilisation is updated.
