## Responsibility
Screens beneficiaries against sanctions lists and owns compliance checks before guarantee issuance.

## In this process
The Compliance Analyst is responsible and accountable for Step 4 (Sanctions and Compliance Screening), running the beneficiary and beneficiary country through the Sanctions Screening Tool and attaching the result to the application. A screening hit suspends the application pending Compliance investigation.
