## Responsibility
Reviews and approves guarantee issuance, exercising the four-eyes control for every guarantee.

## In this process
The Trade Finance Manager is accountable and responsible for Step 5 (Issuance Approval), reviewing the assembled application package and recording the approval decision in the Trade Finance System before issuance can proceed.
