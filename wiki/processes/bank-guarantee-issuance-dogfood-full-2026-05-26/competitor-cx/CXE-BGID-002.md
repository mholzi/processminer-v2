## The journey
Commerzbank corporates submit guarantee orders digitally through the Guarantee Vault platform, manage domestic and foreign guarantees centrally across multiple banks, and receive issued certificates via the portal with 24/7 access. Domestic guarantees can be delivered digitally to beneficiaries, eliminating paper certificates.

## Relevance
The multi-bank, always-on digital vault model raises expectations for guarantee transparency and self-service. Clients managing guarantees across lenders now expect real-time exposure dashboards and portal delivery — not paper certificates or email PDFs from a single relationship bank.

## Evidence
Commerzbank's product page and Digital Vault Services' announcement confirm fully end-to-end digital corporate guarantee management; DVS claims it is "the first (and only) platform" offering this capability.
