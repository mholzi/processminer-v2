## The journey
Citi's CitiDirect gives corporate clients a 360-degree view of trade, cash and loans in one interface with AI-driven form pre-fill and data extraction. CitiConnect APIs let corporates trigger guarantee transactions directly from SAP or Oracle ERP, with integration time cut to under one day via Citi Integrator.

## Relevance
Citi's ERP-embedded model means guarantee applications can originate inside the corporate's own systems, with no separate portal entry. A process still requiring manual portal upload will feel archaic to treasury teams already running this model.

## Evidence
Citi's July 2025 release confirms CitiDirect live in 8 markets covering 57% of its commercial banking base with 2.3M+ client sessions; CitiConnect reached 10 billion API calls in October 2024.
