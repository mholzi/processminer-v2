## The journey
Corporate clients initiate, amend, and track guarantee applications across multiple banking partners from one RIVO portal with real-time status updates, replacing email threads and per-bank log-ins. Guarantee and LC lifecycles are managed end-to-end digitally with structured data flows between corporate treasury systems and issuing banks.

## Relevance
RIVO's single-portal model exposes the experience gap where clients must juggle separate bank portals and manual status chasing for each guarantee. Banks deploying RIVO — including LBBW and BBVA — can offer a materially superior CX that challengers are already delivering as table stakes.

## Evidence
LBBW went live with RIVO in May 2026; BBVA adopted it in November 2025, both citing transparency and turnaround time improvements for corporate guarantee and LC clients.
