## The journey
UniCredit corporate clients apply for and manage bank guarantees end-to-end through UC Trade Finance Gate, accessed via single-sign-on from the UniCredit Corporate Portal. The platform covers issuance, amendments, payment, and monitoring of incoming and outgoing domestic and international guarantees in one fully digital environment.

## Relevance
A single-portal, SSO-integrated guarantee workflow eliminates context-switching and manual hand-offs common in less mature implementations. For large European corporates banking across the UniCredit group, this digital-first experience is already the baseline expectation.

## Evidence
UniCredit's Trade Finance Gate product pages confirm full-lifecycle digital guarantee management. Euromoney named UniCredit a standout performer in its 2024 Trade Finance Survey, citing digital platform capabilities.
