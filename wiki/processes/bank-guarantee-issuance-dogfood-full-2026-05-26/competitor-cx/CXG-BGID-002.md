## The journey
J.P. Morgan's Trade Channel lets corporate clients initiate, track and manage bank guarantees and SBLCs through a single web platform in 60+ markets and 10 languages. Local issuance and reissuance is handled without requiring clients to hold separate facilities per jurisdiction.

## Relevance
JPMorgan's cross-border, single-platform guarantee model raises the bar for multi-market corporates. A process requiring separate in-country touchpoints or manual jurisdiction-by-jurisdiction coordination appears fragmented against this benchmark.

## Evidence
JPMorgan ranked #1 in Trade in the 2024 Coalition Greenwich Digital Transformation Benchmarking Study and received Best Digital Asset Capabilities for Trade Finance by Global Finance 2025.
