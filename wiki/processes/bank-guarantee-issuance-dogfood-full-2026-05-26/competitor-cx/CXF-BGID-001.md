## The journey
Corporate clients apply for, track, and amend guarantees across 30+ banking partners through a single GTK portal. ICC-SWIFT API standards connect the corporate's ERP directly to the issuing bank, automating instruction receipt and accelerating issuance in near real-time — replacing email chains and per-bank portals.

## Relevance
GTK eliminates the fragmented, bank-by-bank experience that corporate treasury teams face today. The API-native model compresses the instruction-to-issuance cycle and provides a unified audit trail — a gap traditional banks cannot close with portal-only approaches.

## Evidence
ANDRITZ went live on GTK in November 2025 across 40 countries; Standard Chartered executed the first ICC-SWIFT API-standard bank guarantee via Komgo in January 2026.
