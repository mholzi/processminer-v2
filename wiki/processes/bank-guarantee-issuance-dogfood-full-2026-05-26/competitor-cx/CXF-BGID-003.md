## The journey
Principals apply for surety bonds through a digital portal; pre-configured underwriting rules evaluate eligibility and issue the instrument instantly when criteria are met. Brokers can request and process bonds remotely, with same-day issuance for repeat business — no credit analyst queue.

## Relevance
Tinubu's automated underwriting model sets a speed benchmark — instant to same-day issuance — that bank guarantee teams cannot match with manual credit assessment. Clients accustomed to instant surety decisions will find multi-day guarantee turnarounds increasingly unacceptable. Note: Tinubu focuses primarily on surety bonds, not bank guarantees.

## Evidence
Tinubu's 2025 Surety Outlook reports that brokers expect same-day issuance for repeat business, yet fewer than 40% of carriers consistently deliver. Confidence set to low as Tinubu's core product is surety, not bank guarantees.
