## The journey
BNP Paribas piloted SWIFT-ICC API-based guarantee issuance via Surecomp's Rivo platform in November 2024, processing a live guarantee in real time without manual intervention. Corporate applicants submit requests through an API connection, bypassing SWIFT messaging costs and manual keying entirely.

## Relevance
Real-time, zero-touch issuance resets the speed expectation for corporate clients. Banks still relying on manual intake or back-office keying will face a growing experience gap as API-native guarantee flows become the competitive baseline for digital-first corporates.

## Evidence
GTR reported the live pilot on 20 November 2024, quoting BNP Paribas calling it "the beginning of a new and very promising journey in the digitalisation of bank guarantees."
