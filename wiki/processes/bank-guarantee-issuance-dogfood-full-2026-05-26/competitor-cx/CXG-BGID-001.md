## The journey
HSBC Trade Solutions offers high-volume standard-wording guarantee types as digital templates. Clients select a template; pre-populated data flows automatically through validation checks with zero human intervention, issuing the guarantee in minutes rather than three to four days.

## Relevance
HSBC's STP guarantee issuance sets a concrete speed benchmark against which manual or semi-digital processes will be measured. Banks still routing standard guarantees through manual review risk an experience gap that corporates will notice when comparing providers.

## Evidence
GTR reported two live STP deals in December 2023 — a tender bond for KPMG Qatar and an advance payment guarantee for Russell Finex UK — confirming production-grade capability.
