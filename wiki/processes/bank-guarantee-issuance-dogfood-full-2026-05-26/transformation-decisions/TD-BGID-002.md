## The decision
The bank will deploy an AI wording pre-screener classifying guarantee wording as standard (auto-approved) or bespoke (Legal review), paired with a published maximum SLA of five business days for bespoke review and client-facing milestone notifications throughout.

## Options considered
- **AI pre-screener + SLA commitment** — chosen: eliminates Legal queue for standard cases; makes the bespoke wait predictable and visible; addresses PP-BGID-002, FP-BGID-001 and FP-BGID-002 together
- **SLA commitment without AI** — addresses the visibility problem but leaves the manual wording classification burden on TFOs and Legal; queue depth unchanged
- **Template-only approach** — forces all wording into bank templates; reduces Legal involvement but removes client flexibility and may conflict with URDG 758 custom terms
- **Status quo** — accepted risk; bespoke wording delay is the highest-rated client pain point and cannot be left unaddressed

## Rationale
The AI pre-screener removes Legal from standard-wording cases, freeing Legal capacity for the genuinely bespoke subset; the SLA converts the most trust-eroding wait in the process into a committed, visible timeline. Together they address the two highest-rated client friction points without restricting the bank's ability to issue custom wording.
