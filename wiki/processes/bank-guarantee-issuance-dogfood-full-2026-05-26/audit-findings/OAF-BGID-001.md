## Finding
In a Q1 2024 sample of 25 issued bank guarantees, internal audit identified one case (4%) where the Trade Finance Officer who submitted the guarantee application also recorded the issuance approval in TFS, breaching the four-eyes segregation requirement of CP-BGID-001. The TFS workflow did not prevent the same user from performing both actions at that time.

## Recommendation
Implement a TFS workflow control preventing the submitting officer's user ID from recording the issuance approval on the same application. Remediation target: Q3 2024. Control introduced as a TFS role-based workflow constraint; finding closed on verification in Q3 2024.
