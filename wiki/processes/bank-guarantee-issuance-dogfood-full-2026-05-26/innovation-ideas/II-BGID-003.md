## The idea
Expose real-time guarantee facility utilisation and remaining headroom to corporate clients via the Corporate Portal and an optional API feed to their treasury management systems. Clients self-serve pre-qualify before submitting an application, preventing the stall at the Credit and Facility Check step.

## Expected benefit
Eliminates facility-shortfall-caused stalls at PS-BGID-002, reducing cycle time and Credit team queue. Clients submit pre-qualified applications, accelerating the end-to-end SLA and reducing relationship manager escalation load.

## Feasibility
Value MEDIUM — eliminates PP-BGID-001 stalls; effort MEDIUM — real-time credit-system API feed required; feasibility MEDIUM — dependent on credit system integration maturity and a data-privacy review for headroom disclosure. Rank 5.
