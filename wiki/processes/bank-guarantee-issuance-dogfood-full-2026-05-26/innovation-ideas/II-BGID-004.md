## The idea
Implement an ICC-SWIFT API-compliant guarantee application channel, allowing large corporates to submit structured guarantee requests directly from their ERP or treasury management systems. The structured schema enforces field completeness by design, eliminating manual portal entry for high-volume clients.

## Expected benefit
High-volume corporate clients gain a touchless application experience with zero re-keying. Intake completeness is guaranteed by the structured schema, removing the most common source of application rejection and SLA delay.

## Feasibility
Value HIGH — eliminates PP-BGID-003 and CG-BGID-001 for high-volume clients; effort HIGH — API gateway, ICC-SWIFT adoption, ERP integration; feasibility MEDIUM — standard not universally adopted, phased rollout recommended. Rank 3.
