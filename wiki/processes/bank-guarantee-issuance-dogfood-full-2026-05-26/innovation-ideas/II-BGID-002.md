## The idea
Upgrade the Corporate Portal's guarantee application form to enforce mandatory fields — including commercial contract reference — at submission, blocking incomplete applications before they enter the bank's queue. Inline help text explains each field's purpose and where to find the required reference.

## Expected benefit
Eliminates the client round-trip caused by missing contract references, allowing the end-to-end SLA clock to start from first submission and removing the manual completeness-checking burden from Trade Finance Officers at intake.

## Feasibility
Value HIGH — removes CG-BGID-001 and PP-BGID-003 via portal enforcement; effort MEDIUM — UI change, no back-end re-architecture; feasibility HIGH — no regulatory blockers, within portal product team scope. Rank 1.
