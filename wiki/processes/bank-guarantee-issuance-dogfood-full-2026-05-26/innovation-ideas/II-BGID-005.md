## The idea
When the treasury team records that cash collateral has been received and blocked for a partially-secured guarantee, an automated notification is sent to the client confirming the block, the blocked amount and the value date — removing the need to call the trade-finance desk to check whether collateral is committed and issuance is proceeding.

## Expected benefit
Corporate clients with time-sensitive underlying contracts gain real-time visibility into the collateral step, reducing inbound queries to the trade-finance desk and eliminating the uncertainty that currently delays contract execution when issuance timing is unclear.
