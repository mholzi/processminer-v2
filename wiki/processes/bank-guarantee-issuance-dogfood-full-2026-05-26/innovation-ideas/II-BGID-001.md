## The idea
Deploy an LLM-based clause-screening tool that checks bespoke guarantee wording against standard policy templates before the application reaches the Legal team. Non-conforming clauses are flagged instantly with suggested corrections; Legal reviews only the flagged delta under a committed SLA, rather than conducting a full open-ended read.

## Expected benefit
Bespoke wording applications gain a predictable, shortened review timeline, enabling relationship managers to give corporate clients a reliable issuance date and eliminating the open-ended delay documented in PP-BGID-002 and FP-BGID-001.

## Feasibility
Value HIGH — eliminates PP-BGID-002 and FP-BGID-001/002; effort HIGH — LLM integration plus Legal sign-off on screening logic; feasibility MEDIUM — AI accuracy risk requires human-in-the-loop escalation design. Rank 2.
