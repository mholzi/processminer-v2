## What happens
For guarantees not fully covered by an approved facility, the Trade Finance Officer confirms that cash collateral has been received and blocked before issuance can proceed. This step is mandatory for every partially-secured guarantee.

## Inputs
- Application package flagged as partially secured
- Collateral receipt confirmation
- Blocked-funds confirmation record

## Outputs
- Collateral block confirmation recorded
- Application cleared to proceed to Issuance Approval

## Why it matters
Prevents the bank from issuing a guarantee without the required cash collateral in place for partially-secured facilities.
