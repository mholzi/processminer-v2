## What happens
Standard-wording guarantees proceed directly to Sanctions and Compliance Screening, using approved templates maintained in the Trade Finance System and version-controlled by Legal. Bespoke wording guarantees are sent to the Legal team for review and sign-off before issuance can proceed. The Trade Finance Officer remains accountable throughout — chasing Legal and managing client expectations — with Legal acting as consulted rather than accountable. Legal review operates against an informal 2-business-day desk convention, though this target is not committed to the client, not tracked in TFS, and carries no escalation trigger when breached.

## Inputs
- Validated application with identified wording type
- Approved standard wording template from the Trade Finance System (standard wording)
- Bespoke wording request (bespoke applications)

## Outputs
- Approved guarantee wording ready for compliance screening
- Legal sign-off obtained (bespoke only)

## Why it matters
Ensures the guarantee instrument's wording is legally sound and acceptable to the beneficiary, reducing the risk of disputes or instrument rejection at delivery.
