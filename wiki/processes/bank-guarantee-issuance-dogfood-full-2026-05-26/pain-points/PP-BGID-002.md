## Description
Guarantees with bespoke wording must be reviewed and signed off by the Legal team before issuance. Legal review has no committed turnaround, making the delay unpredictable.

## Impact
Bespoke wording cases cannot be given a reliable timeline. The document does not quantify the impact on client experience or relationship manager commitments.

## Root cause
The Legal team operates without a committed turnaround for wording review.
