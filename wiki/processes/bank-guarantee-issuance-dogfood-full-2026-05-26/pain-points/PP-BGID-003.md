## Description
The commercial contract reference — a mandatory intake field — is the most frequently missing item when clients submit guarantee applications. Officers must return the application via the portal before intake can complete.

## Impact
Delays the start of credit and compliance review, adding at least one client round-trip before the 3-day end-to-end SLA clock can effectively begin.

## Root cause
Clients may not have the contract reference readily available at submission, or may not know it is mandatory; the portal does not block submission on this field.
