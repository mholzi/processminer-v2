## Description
Applications frequently stall at the credit and facility check when the client has not pre-arranged enough limit.

## Impact
Stalled applications are routed to the Credit team for limit increases. Processing halts pending Credit team review, adding cycle time.

## Root cause
Clients submit guarantee applications without first pre-arranging sufficient approved facility limit, leaving insufficient headroom at the credit and facility check.
