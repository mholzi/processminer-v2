## Description
The client's designated relationship manager by telephone — a direct, assisted channel for initiating, discussing or escalating a bank guarantee request.

## Usage
Used for initial triage of guarantee applications, particularly where the RM keys the application into the portal on the client's behalf, and for escalation when digital channels stall or the client needs guidance on guarantee structure.
