## Description
The SWIFT messaging network used for bank-to-bank guarantee communication — MT-799 pre-advises the beneficiary's bank and MT760 constitutes the formally issued guarantee instrument.

## Usage
Used when the beneficiary's bank requires SWIFT-delivered instruments; MT-799 signals the bank's willingness to issue and MT760 delivers the guarantee text, triggering the beneficiary bank's acceptance process.
