## Description
The bank's online corporate portal — a web-based self-service platform through which corporate clients initiate and track bank guarantee applications.

## Usage
Clients use the portal to submit guarantee applications and receive decision notifications; it is the primary digital intake channel for standard guarantee requests and handles the majority of application volume.
