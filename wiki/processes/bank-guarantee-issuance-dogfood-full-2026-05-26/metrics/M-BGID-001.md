## Definition
Time elapsed from receipt of a complete application to delivery of the executed guarantee to the beneficiary, measured for standard-wording guarantees with no sanctions screening hit.

No measured actual is recorded. The SLA target is 3 business days.

## Why it matters
Turnaround time is the primary service-level commitment made to corporate clients. Tracking actuals against the 3-day target would expose where delays accumulate — facility checks and legal wording review — and enable improvement.
