## Definition
Percentage of guarantee applications triggering at least one documented exception path (EX-BGID-001 through EX-BGID-006) in a given month. Sourced from the exception log. Unit: %; owner: Trade Finance Manager; frequency: monthly.

## Current reading
Not currently measured; target <15%. Thresholds: green ≤15%, amber 15–25%, red >25%.

## Why it matters
Exception rate aggregates the six documented categories and gives the Trade Finance Manager early warning of process breakdown before SLA overruns appear. Rising rates at intake (EX-BGID-002) or wording review (EX-BGID-003) directly predict turnaround time deterioration.
