## Definition
Percentage of guarantee applications processed from PS-BGID-001 through PS-BGID-006 without triggering an exception flag or manual rework step. Sourced from workflow audit trail. Unit: %; owner: Head of Trade Finance Operations; frequency: monthly.

## Current reading
Approximately 40% (per PP-BGID-002 and FP-BGID-001 analysis); target 65%. Thresholds: green ≥60%, amber 40–59%, red <40%.

## Why it matters
STP rate is the composite health indicator for the manual-handoff steps at PS-BGID-002 and PS-BGID-003. A sustained amber or red reading signals rising pressure on Legal and Credit teams and predicts end-to-end SLA deterioration.
