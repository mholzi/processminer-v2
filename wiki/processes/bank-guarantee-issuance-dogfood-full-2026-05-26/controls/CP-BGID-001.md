## What it checks
Every guarantee issuance requires a Trade Finance Manager's approval before the guarantee is released, with an additional sign-off required for guarantees above EUR 5 million.

## Control activity
A Trade Finance Manager reviews the assembled guarantee package and records approval in the Trade Finance System. For guarantees exceeding EUR 5 million, the Head of Trade Finance must also provide sign-off before issuance proceeds.

## Risk addressed
Without this control, a single officer could issue a guarantee unilaterally, increasing the risk of unauthorised issuance, fraud or error going undetected before the instrument reaches the beneficiary.

## Timing
The control runs at the Issuance Approval step, once per guarantee application, before the instrument is generated and transmitted.
