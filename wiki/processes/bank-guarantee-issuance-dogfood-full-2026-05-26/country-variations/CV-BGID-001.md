## Description
In Germany the regulator requires a senior signing officer's sign-off before issuance of any guarantee above EUR 5 million.

## Variation detail
PS-BGID-005 issuance approval gains an extra mandatory step: a board-empowered Trade Finance director must counter-sign in the system. Below threshold the standard four-eyes flow applies; above threshold the system blocks issuance without the second sign-off.

## Reason
BaFin MaRisk module BTO 2.2 and internal Group Risk policy require a senior approval for material guarantee commitments above EUR 5 million in the German entity.
