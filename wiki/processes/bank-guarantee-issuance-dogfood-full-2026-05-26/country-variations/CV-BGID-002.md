## Description
French guarantees in favour of French beneficiaries must carry their legally binding wording in French.

## Variation detail
PS-BGID-003 wording review for French applications runs against a French-language template library; the Legal team also signs off a French version when the underlying contract is multilingual. Standard English-only wording is rejected at this step.

## Reason
ACPR and French commercial-code requirements mandate that obligations enforceable in French courts be issued in French.
