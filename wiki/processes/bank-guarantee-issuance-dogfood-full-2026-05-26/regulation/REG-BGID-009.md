## What it requires
Financial entities must maintain a documented ICT risk management framework and implement security controls ensuring continuous availability, integrity and confidentiality of ICT systems supporting critical or important functions, including trade finance processing platforms.

## Why it applies
DORA applies to EU credit institutions from 17 January 2025. The Trade Finance System underpins every step of guarantee issuance; system unavailability or data integrity failure would prevent timely issuance and create counterparty and legal risk.

## How it is met
The Trade Finance System must be in the bank's DORA ICT risk management framework covering resilience testing and business continuity under Articles 6 and 11. CP-BGID-001 and CP-BGID-003 depend on system availability for execution.
