## What it requires
Retain copies of all CDD documents and transaction records — including guarantee application files, beneficial-owner identification and screening results — for at least five years after relationship end or transaction date, in machine-readable format (AMLR Article 77; AMLD5 Article 40).

## Why it applies
Each guarantee issuance is a transaction creating CDD and document retention obligations. AMLD5 Article 40 and AMLR Article 77 impose specific document-type, format and duration requirements that supplement the AML compliance obligations in REG-BGID-001.

## How it is met
Application package, screening result and KYC file archived in Trade Finance System after issuance at PS-BGID-006. Machine-readable retention and 5-year purge schedule to be confirmed with Compliance and Records Management.
