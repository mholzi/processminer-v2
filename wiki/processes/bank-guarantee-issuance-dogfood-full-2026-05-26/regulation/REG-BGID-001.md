## What it requires
Conduct customer due diligence (CDD) on guarantee applicants — verify identity, beneficial ownership (25%+ threshold) and the purpose of the transaction. Screen the beneficiary and related parties against EU and UN sanctions lists, and PEP registers. Retain transaction records for five years.

## Why it applies
The bank is an obliged entity under AMLD5 and the forthcoming AMLR; each bank guarantee is a financial product issued to a corporate client, creating an obligation to apply CDD and sanctions screening before the guarantee is transmitted.

## How it is met
CP-BGID-002 (Sanctions Screening) screens the beneficiary and country at PS-BGID-004. CDD on the guarantee applicant is maintained as part of the client's KYC file, documented outside this process.
