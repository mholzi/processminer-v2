## What it requires
The guarantee must be issued in writing; it is independent of the underlying commercial transaction and the bank must honour compliant demands without reference to any underlying dispute. Demand presentation, expiry and amendment terms are governed by the rules.

## Why it applies
The process issues demand guarantees; where the guarantee instrument incorporates URDG 758 by reference, the ICC rules govern the form of issuance, presentation of demands, amendments and the bank's payment obligations.

## How it is met
Standard wording templates in the Trade Finance System (PS-BGID-003) are version-controlled by Legal to ensure URDG 758 compliance. Bespoke wording is reviewed and signed off by Legal before issuance.
