## What it requires
Personal data of corporate representatives collected during KYC and guarantee applications must have a lawful basis (Article 6(1)(b)/(c)), be kept no longer than necessary (Article 5(1)(e)), and may only be withheld from erasure requests where AML retention law applies (Article 17(3)(b)).

## Why it applies
Guarantee applications collect personal data of corporate signatories and beneficial owners. The bank is a data controller under GDPR; Articles 5 and 6 govern every processing step from application intake through post-issuance record retention.

## How it is met
Lawful basis documented in privacy notices. AML-mandated retention overrides erasure requests during the 5-year window. Data minimisation applied to application forms collected at PS-BGID-001.
