## What it requires
Institutions must implement appropriate segregation of duties as a structural internal-control measure to mitigate conflicts of interest. Incompatible functions — originating and independently approving a credit commitment — must be assigned to different individuals or organisational units.

## Why it applies
EBA/GL/2021/05 applies to all EU credit institutions under CRD. Bank guarantee issuance combines client-facing origination with credit approval; the guidelines require structural separation as a governance obligation, not merely a best practice.

## How it is met
CP-BGID-001 structurally separates proposal (Trade Finance Officer) from approval (Trade Finance Manager / Head of Trade Finance), satisfying the EBA internal-governance segregation requirement at the operational-process level.
