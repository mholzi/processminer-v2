## What it requires
Banks must report issued guarantees as off-balance-sheet credit exposures in COREP template C 07.00 under Regulation (EU) No 680/2014, applying the correct credit conversion factor per CRR Article 111 and submitting data to supervisory authorities at prescribed frequencies.

## Why it applies
Every guarantee issued creates a contingent credit exposure. Regulation (EU) No 680/2014 mandates institutions report these exposures in COREP, enabling supervisors to assess capital adequacy against the bank's off-balance-sheet portfolio.

## How it is met
Facility utilisation updated in Trade Finance System at PS-BGID-006 (consistent with CP-BGID-003). COREP C 07.00 population from the Trade Finance System feed to be confirmed with Finance and Regulatory Reporting.
