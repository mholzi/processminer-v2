## What it requires
Obliged entities must assess ML/TF risk factors specific to trade finance products, including country risk, counterparty risk and product risk. Enhanced due diligence applies for cross-border transactions and any correspondent banking relationships used in international guarantee issuance.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantee issuance involves cross-border beneficiaries and potentially complex ownership structures. EBA supervisory guidance requires a product-specific ML/TF risk assessment for trade finance guarantees, supplementing the AML directive obligations.

## How it is met
Beneficiary screening at PS-BGID-004 via CP-BGID-002 addresses sanctions-related risk factors. Broader ML/TF risk assessment for individual guarantee applications is to be confirmed with the SME.
