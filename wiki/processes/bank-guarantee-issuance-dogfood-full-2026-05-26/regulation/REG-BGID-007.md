## What it requires
Institutions must organisationally separate the front office (Markt) from the back office (Marktfolge) up to management board level in credit and contingent-liability business. Risk-relevant decisions require an independent second sign-off (four-eyes principle) before commitment.

## Why it applies
Bank guarantee issuance creates a contingent credit liability falling within MaRisk credit-business modules. The process combines client-facing origination and independent credit approval, directly engaging BTO 1.2 segregation and four-eyes requirements.

## How it is met
CP-BGID-001 enforces the mandatory second sign-off by the Trade Finance Manager before release, with Head of Trade Finance escalation above EUR 5 million. CP-BGID-003 provides the automated pre-approval facility check.
