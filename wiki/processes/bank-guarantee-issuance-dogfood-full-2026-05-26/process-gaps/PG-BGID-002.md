## The gap
Each handoff between the Trade Finance Officer, Legal and Trade Finance Manager is executed by email; no workflow system enforces ownership transfer, records handoff time or makes the request's current status visible to the originating TFO.

## Impact
Handoff delays are invisible to TFS and to management reporting. The TFO has no system-driven escalation trigger and the client has no status update; this is the systemic root cause of FP-BGID-001, FP-BGID-002 and directly amplifies PP-BGID-002.

## Next step
Introduce TFS-enforced workflow tasks for each inter-function handoff with mandatory acknowledgement, elapsed-time stamping and an automatic escalation trigger when a task exceeds its sub-SLA threshold.
