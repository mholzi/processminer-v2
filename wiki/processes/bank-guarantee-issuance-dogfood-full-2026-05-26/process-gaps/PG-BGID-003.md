## The gap
The seven-step guarantee issuance process is documented but not instrumented. STP rate, exception escalation rate and rework rate are not measured, and no process-wide quality KPI exists. Management cannot determine whether process performance is improving or degrading between periods.

## Impact
Without measurement, the desk has no objective basis for prioritising improvement investment, cannot evidence process maturity to internal audit or regulators, and cannot quantify the benefit of target-state changes (TS-BGID-001 through TS-BGID-003) after implementation.

## Next step
Define a minimum KPI set — STP rate, exception rate, rework rate, end-to-end elapsed time — instrument them in TFS and assign ownership to the Head of Trade Finance with a monthly reporting cadence.
