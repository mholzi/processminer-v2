## Description
Clients with bespoke wording requests cannot see where their file sits in Legal's review queue; they receive no status updates and no estimated completion date while the review is in progress.

## Root cause
The Legal team operates an informal, untracked review queue with no client-facing status interface and no service-level commitment published to the trade-finance desk or the client.

## Client impact
Clients cannot plan the underlying commercial transaction around an unknown issuance date; relationship managers absorb the uncertainty through escalation calls, adding invisible overhead to every bespoke guarantee application.
