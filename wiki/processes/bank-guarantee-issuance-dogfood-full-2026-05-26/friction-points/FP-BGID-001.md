## Description
At the Wording Review step, clients awaiting approval of bespoke guarantee wording enter a legal review queue with no disclosed turnaround time. The client receives no proactive updates while Legal deliberates.

## Root cause
The Legal team operates without a committed or communicated SLA for bespoke wording review, and no systemic notification mechanism exists to keep the client informed.

## Client impact
The client cannot plan the underlying transaction around an unknown issuance date. Relationship managers are unable to give a reliable completion estimate, eroding trust and prompting escalation calls that add invisible overhead to every bespoke guarantee.
