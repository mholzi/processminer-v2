## Description
For partially-secured guarantees, the client fails to transfer the required cash collateral to the bank's collateral account by the agreed posting deadline, preventing the Trade Finance Officer from confirming collateral receipt and advancing the application to issuance approval.

## Handling
The Trade Finance Officer contacts the client and RM to notify them of the posting shortfall and requests immediate transfer. A one-business-day grace period is extended; if collateral is not received, the application is suspended and the client is informed that issuance cannot proceed without collateral confirmation via CP-BGID-004.

## Impact
Blocks guarantee issuance entirely; creates high risk to the client's commercial deadline when the underlying contract requires the guarantee by a specific date. Repeat shortfalls indicate a client liquidity risk the RM should monitor.
