## Description
The beneficiary's bank receives the MT760 via SWIFT but formally rejects the guarantee wording, typically because the text does not conform to a locally required standard form or the beneficiary's contractual specification. This is distinct from a SWIFT network transmission failure (EX-BGID-001).

## Handling
The Trade Finance Officer contacts the RM and client to advise the rejection and its stated reason. Legal reviews the wording objection and drafts amended guarantee text meeting the beneficiary bank's requirements. The amended guarantee is re-approved under CP-BGID-001 and a replacement MT760 is transmitted; the original guarantee is marked void in TFS.

## Impact
Delays guarantee delivery by two to five business days for a Legal amendment cycle; may breach the client's commercial obligation deadline and triggers a second sanctions screening to ensure the amended instrument remains compliant.
