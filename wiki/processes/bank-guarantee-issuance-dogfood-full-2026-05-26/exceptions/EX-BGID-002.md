## Description
A guarantee application submitted through the Corporate Portal or via an RM is returned to the client because one or more mandatory fields — typically the commercial contract reference, beneficiary address or guarantee validity period — are missing or carry invalid values.

## Handling
The Trade Finance Officer returns the application via the Corporate Portal with a checklist identifying each missing or invalid field, requesting resubmission. The application is placed on hold in TFS and does not advance to the credit and facility check until a complete resubmission is received. No SLA clock is suspended during the client correction period.

## Impact
Delays the application by one to two business days on average; repeated incomplete submissions cause visible client frustration and increase the TFO's workload through manual chasing and re-validation.
