## Description
A guarantee application requiring bespoke wording review spends more than five business days in the Legal team's queue without an approved wording outcome — typically because Legal's workload exceeds capacity or the wording requires multiple review cycles between Legal and the client.

## Handling
The Trade Finance Officer escalates to the Legal team's manager and notifies the relationship manager and client of the delay. If the delay will breach the client's underlying contract deadline, the Head of Trade Finance is informed and a client communication is issued. The application remains on hold in TFS pending Legal's determination.

## Impact
Increases total cycle time, potentially breaching the client's beneficiary submission deadline; the RM is drawn into escalation management, displacing revenue-generating activity and eroding client trust in the bank's turnaround reliability.
