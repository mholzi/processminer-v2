## The risk
The AI pre-screener may be classified as a high-risk AI system under the EU AI Act, given its role in a credit-adjacent bank-guarantee decision workflow, triggering mandatory conformity assessment, transparency and audit-trail obligations before deployment.

## Likelihood & impact
Moderate likelihood given EBA guidance on AI Act scoping for trade-finance automation; MEDIUM impact — adds compliance overhead and extends the deployment timeline but does not block the initiative if planned for in the architecture phase.

## Mitigation
Commission an EU AI Act scoping assessment before procurement; design human-oversight mechanisms and a full audit trail into the initial architecture to ensure conformity regardless of the final high-risk determination.
