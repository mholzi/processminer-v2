## The risk
Not all correspondent banks have adopted the ICC-SWIFT API standard, and corporate clients face material ERP integration cost and project overhead to connect their treasury systems, limiting the reachable client base at launch.

## Likelihood & impact
Moderate likelihood for mid-tier clients and emerging-market correspondents; MEDIUM impact — constrains the addressable client base in the near term and may delay intake-completeness benefits to a subset of high-volume clients.

## Mitigation
Prioritise the 20 highest-volume corporate clients with existing SWIFT connectivity for the first rollout wave; maintain the smart intake portal (II-BGID-002) as the concurrent channel so the API path enhances rather than replaces it.
