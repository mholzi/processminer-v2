## The risk
Corporate clients opt out of automated collateral notifications, or the notification is delivered to the wrong contact within the client organisation, leaving the intended treasury recipient uninformed about the collateral block status.

## Likelihood & impact
Low likelihood given opt-in design means only engaged clients are enrolled; LOW impact — the existing manual process remains unchanged for non-opted-in clients, so no degradation occurs.

## Mitigation
Opt-in enrolment with contact-address validation at registration; delivery-failure fallback to RM outreach; phased rollout limited to clients already receiving approval and issuance notifications via the portal.
