## The risk
Corporate clients accustomed to submitting via email or through relationship managers resist the enforced portal form, increasing application drop-off and RM escalation calls during rollout before the mandatory field-enforcement benefits are realised.

## Likelihood & impact
Moderate likelihood for clients with established email-based workflows; MEDIUM impact — does not block issuance but delays realisation of the completeness benefits and adds RM workload during parallel-channel operation.

## Mitigation
Phased rollout beginning with digitally-engaged clients; co-design mandatory field set with top-20 corporates before go-live; 90-day parallel-path option for complex accounts; RM coaching programme aligned to launch.
