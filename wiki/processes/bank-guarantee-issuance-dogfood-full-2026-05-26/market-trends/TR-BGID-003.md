## The trend
Treasury management platforms now offer real-time guarantee facility utilisation dashboards that aggregate outstanding guarantees across group entities, currencies, and banks, giving treasury teams live headroom visibility before submitting new applications to the issuing bank.

## Relevance
Facility limit shortfalls (PP-BGID-001) stall applications at the Credit and Facility Check step (PS-BGID-002) because clients are unaware of remaining headroom. Real-time utilisation monitoring shifts this check upstream to the client's treasury team, enabling pre-qualified submissions.

## Evidence
Nomentia's guarantee management platform surfaces live facility headroom across all group banks and currencies, supporting pre-submission credit checks for organisations managing hundreds to thousands of guarantees annually.
