## The trend
The UNCITRAL Model Law on Electronic Transferable Records (MLETR) is reaching a tipping point. Twelve economies — including Singapore, Germany, UK, and France — have adopted or aligned with it, creating a growing legal basis for issuing demand guarantees as electronic instruments without paper originals.

## Relevance
As MLETR-aligned jurisdictions grow, corporates will demand fully electronic guarantee instruments and e-delivery to beneficiaries, pressuring the current paper-based wording and courier workflow (PS-BGID-003, PS-BGID-006) and making a digital delivery capability competitively necessary.

## Evidence
The five largest MLETR adopters represent nearly 37% of global GDP; 12 economies in total had adopted or aligned as of mid-2024.
