## The trend
Leading trade banks are deploying purpose-built digital guarantee portals with standardised intake forms that enforce mandatory field completion before submission, replacing fragmented email and document workflows. HSBC's FI Digital Network is an early example, now expanded to its full trade product suite.

## Relevance
Clients submitting incomplete applications without commercial contract references (PP-BGID-003) and the absence of system-enforced completeness checks at intake (CG-BGID-001, PS-BGID-001) are directly addressed by structured portals that block submission until required fields are populated.

## Evidence
HSBC's guarantee digital portal, live since 2022, provides real-time transaction status and automated reporting; the bank has since extended the platform beyond guarantees to its full trade product suite.
