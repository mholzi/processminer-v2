## The trend
DORA entered application across the EU on 17 January 2025, requiring banks to maintain robust ICT risk management, audit third-party digital providers, and test business continuity annually. Paired with eIDAS 2.0, it is accelerating adoption of qualified electronic signatures for formal banking documents.

## Relevance
Guarantee issuance and e-delivery (PS-BGID-003, PS-BGID-006) and completeness controls (CG-BGID-001) rely on third-party portals and e-signature tooling. DORA's mandatory supplier oversight compels banks to formalise and audit these digital touchpoints, driving standardisation of e-delivery workflows.

## Evidence
DORA applies to more than 20 types of financial institutions and mandates ICT incident reporting within 24 hours; entered into application 17 January 2025 with ongoing EBA supervisory oversight.
