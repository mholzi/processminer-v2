## The trend
Large language models are moving from pilots into production for trade finance document review. Specialist AI platforms now read, interpret, and flag non-standard wording in guarantees and standby letters of credit, automating steps previously reserved for legal teams.

## Relevance
Bespoke guarantee wording review (PP-BGID-002, FP-BGID-001) has no committed turnaround. LLM-based pre-screening could flag non-standard clauses before the wording reaches counsel, compressing legal review time and enabling a committed SLA on the reduced-scope review.

## Evidence
GTR Risk 2025 reports up to 40% reduction in trade document processing time from LLM automation, depending on document complexity.
