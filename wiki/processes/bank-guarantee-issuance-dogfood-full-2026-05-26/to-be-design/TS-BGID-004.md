## Target description
The Sanctions and Compliance Screening step is unchanged in the target state. The hybrid automated and manual screening model already satisfies AMLD5, AMLR and EU Sanctions Regulation obligations; no innovation idea targets this step and no control gap exists. The step is retained as-is within the transformation scope.

## What changes
- No structural change — the hybrid automated and manual screening model is retained in the target state
- Ongoing maintenance of sanctions list feeds and regulatory calibration remain the Compliance team's responsibility and are out of scope for this transformation

## Rationale
The existing hybrid model meets regulatory obligations and no documented problem or innovation idea targets this step; scarce transformation capacity is directed at the three steps with material client and operational improvement potential.
