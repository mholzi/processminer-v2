## The benchmark
90% of banks globally are investing in digital customer-experience initiatives for trade finance, driven by corporate clients who now prioritise leading service and digital capabilities more than any other selection criterion.

## Relevance
Bank guarantee issuance sits squarely in scope: a paper-heavy, manually driven process is out of step with the digital-experience level the market now treats as table stakes for retaining corporate trade-finance mandates.

## Evidence
ICC Trade Register 2024 (ICC / BCG / Global Credit Data, October 2024) via Trade Finance Global — "90% of banks are investing in digital customer experience initiatives to meet client expectations."
