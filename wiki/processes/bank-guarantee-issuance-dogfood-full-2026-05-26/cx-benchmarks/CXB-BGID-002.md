## The benchmark
83% of corporate clients consider real-time transaction visibility a top priority, yet only 31% of banks regard it as high value — a 52-point expectation gap that drives dissatisfaction and wallet-share loss even when overall satisfaction scores appear healthy.

## Relevance
Bank guarantee issuance typically offers clients no real-time status between application submission and instrument delivery. This process sits squarely inside the visibility gap and exposes the bank to switching risk from clients who can benchmark it against digitally superior alternatives.

## Evidence
CGI Transaction Banking Survey 2026 (356 corporates, 357 banks, published February 2026) — "only 31% of banks see real-time forecasting as high value, compared to 83% of corporates."
