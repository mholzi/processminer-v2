## The benchmark
Automated trade-finance systems reduce document-examination turnaround from 4.2 business days to 1.1 business days, and deliver same-day issuance for 78% of standard applications. Bank guarantee processing capacity increases from 15 to 45 transactions per day with automation.

## Relevance
Bank guarantee issuance follows the same document-examination and credit-check sequence: without automation, the 3–5 day manual baseline is the effective floor. Digitally advanced peers are already compressing this to same-day for standard guarantee structures.

## Evidence
Finantrix industry analysis, March 2025 — directionally consistent with other sources but no primary survey named by publisher; confidence set to low accordingly.
