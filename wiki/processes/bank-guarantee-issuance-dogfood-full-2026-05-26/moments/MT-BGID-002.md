## The moment
After an undefined number of days, the revised bespoke wording arrives with no prior indication of how long the review took or where their file stood in Legal's queue.

## Why it matters
The unexplained wait erodes trust disproportionately to its actual duration: clients unable to monitor progress perceive the bank as opaque and unresponsive. Relationship managers field escalation calls that displace higher-value activity, amplifying the internal cost of every bespoke guarantee application.

## Design implication
A published SLA range for bespoke wording review — even an indicative three-to-five business days — combined with a milestone notification when Legal begins the review, neutralises most negative sentiment before it accumulates.
