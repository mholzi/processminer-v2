## The moment
The client reads the draft guarantee text the bank is prepared to issue and assesses whether its wording satisfies the beneficiary's contractual requirements.

## Why it matters
If the wording does not match, the entire timeline resets — a second Legal review, renewed client–beneficiary negotiation and further delay. If it matches, trust is confirmed and issuance proceeds immediately. A single yes-or-no reading determines whether the process completes on schedule.

## Design implication
Present the draft text alongside a structured checklist mapping each beneficiary requirement to the relevant clause; provide a structured feedback channel so any mismatch is captured and resolved in one exchange rather than an open email thread.
