## The idea
Define a time-bound reversal window (e.g. T+5 business days) and a digital reversal workflow in the Client Lifecycle Workflow Tool that allows a Closure Approver to reinstate a closed account, trigger re-credit of any disbursed balance, and generate an audit trail — closing the current procedural gap where no reversal route exists.

## Expected benefit
Removes the documented risk of client harm and operational confusion when an account is closed in error. A defined digital workflow replaces ad-hoc escalation and gives both the client and the bank a clear recovery path with a complete audit record.

## Feasibility
Low-to-medium complexity — requires a policy decision on the reversal window and a workflow configuration in the Client Lifecycle Workflow Tool and Core Banking System. Main risk is the re-credit of disbursed balances, which may require client cooperation to return funds.
