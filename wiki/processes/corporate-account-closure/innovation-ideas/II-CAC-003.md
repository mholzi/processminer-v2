## The idea
Define the closed-account retention period and archive location in policy, then automate retention enforcement in the Records Archive: the system flags files reaching the end of their retention window, prevents premature deletion, and generates an audit trail that satisfies AMLA supervisory retrieval requirements coming into force from July 2027.

## Expected benefit
Closes the documented gap that retention period and archive location are undefined. Consistent, automated retention enforcement removes the risk of premature deletion or non-retrieval during a regulatory inspection, directly addressing the quarterly manual review's current inability to confirm compliance.

## Feasibility
Medium complexity — requires a policy decision on retention period, then Records Archive configuration. Key risk is legacy archive coverage: older files may sit outside the system. AMLA Technical Standards due July 2026 create urgency.
