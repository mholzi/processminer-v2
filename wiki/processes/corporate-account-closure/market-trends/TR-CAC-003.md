## The trend
Banks are replacing periodic batch sanctions screening with continuous, AI-driven real-time checks that flag matches at the moment of activity rather than overnight. AI-powered fuzzy matching and NLP cut false-positive alert volumes and enable explainable risk scoring that satisfies audit and regulatory expectations.

## Relevance
The compliance and sanctions check at step 4 already triggers an automated query, but regulators increasingly expect real-time screening with explainable, auditable outputs rather than a single point-in-time check. An AI-enhanced screen would reduce Compliance review time on the file and lower the rate of false-positive blocks that delay closures.

## Evidence
Temenos FCM AI Agent live deployments achieve less than 2% false positive rates in real-time sanctions screening, launched May 2025.
