## The trend
Banks are moving agentic AI from pilot to production in 2026, deploying autonomous AI agents that handle multi-step compliance tasks, client due diligence, and operations workflows under human oversight. These systems reason across systems and act without step-by-step human instruction.

## Relevance
The corporate account closure process relies heavily on manual sequential steps — mandate verification, Finance obligations confirmation (2-business-day turnaround), and the 4-eyes approval gate. Each of these is a target for agentic orchestration, with AI agents able to gather, cross-check and route the file while a human approves the outcome.

## Evidence
Lloyds Banking Group expects agentic AI to add £100 million in value in 2026 through fraud and complaints automation. BCG projects AI agents can increase bank profitability 30% and reduce costs 30–40% by 2030.
