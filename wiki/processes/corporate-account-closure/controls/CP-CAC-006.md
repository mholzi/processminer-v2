## What it checks
That all accounts closed during the period show zero balances and no residual positions after closure.

## Control activity
A daily reconciliation of recently closed account balances is performed to detect any residual positions or erroneous postings.

## Risk addressed
Undetected residual balances or erroneous postings on closed accounts, leading to financial exposure or reconciliation breaks.

## Timing
Performed daily, as a detective control run after closures have been executed.
