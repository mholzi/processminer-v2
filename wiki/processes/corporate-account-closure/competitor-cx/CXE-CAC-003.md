## The journey
For eligible accounts Commerzbank provides an in-portal closure path: clients navigate to Accounts & Cards → Administration → Manage Account → Closing Account in online banking. The bank checks digital eligibility and notifies the client on outcome. Ineligible account types require a paper closure form sent by post. Pre-conditions include zero balance and repaid overdraft.

## Relevance
As Deutsche Bank's closest German corporate peer, Commerzbank's in-portal closure option for eligible accounts—even if not universal—sets a minimum digital self-service expectation in the German market. Deutsche Bank's current process has no equivalent client-facing digital entry point, meaning Commerzbank already offers the German corporate client a more direct closure experience.

## Evidence
Wise's guide to closing a Commerzbank account describes the online banking closure path and notes that accounts ineligible for digital closure still require a mailed paper form, confirming the hybrid model.
