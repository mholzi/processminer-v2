## The journey
Monzo Business account closure is self-service in the mobile app: switch to Lite plan, zero the account balance, then tap Close business account in settings. Monzo confirms closure by email, attaching a password-protected final statement; the password is delivered by SMS. No documentation submission is required. A 30-day cooling-off period applies before reapplication.

## Relevance
Monzo's automated email confirmation with attached final statement collapses the post-closure record-handover step—separate in Deutsche Bank's process—into a single automated action at point of closure. The SMS-password delivery also demonstrates that secure document handover does not require staff effort or multi-day postal dispatch.

## Evidence
Monzo's public help page confirms the in-app self-service closure steps, the automated email with password-protected statement, and the SMS-delivered password, all without staff involvement.
