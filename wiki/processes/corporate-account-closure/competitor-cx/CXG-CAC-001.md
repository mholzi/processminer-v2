## The journey
HSBC UK Business Banking offers multi-channel corporate account closure: an OTP-authenticated digital form via Business Internet Banking (Account Services → Account Closure); a downloadable PDF for postal submission; and a branch option. Simple mandates can complete the digital path fully online. Complex multi-signatory mandates fall back to the paper route, with all authorised signatories required.

## Relevance
HSBC's layered model—digital for simpler mandates, paper for complex ones—shows that even a global universal bank retains paper dependency for multi-signatory corporate clients. The OTP-authenticated digital form for simpler cases is a step ahead of Deutsche Bank's current journey, where no client-facing digital closure channel exists regardless of mandate complexity.

## Evidence
HSBC UK's published business account closure page confirms the Business Internet Banking digital form option, SMS OTP authentication, postal requirement for multi-signatory mandates, and five years of post-closure transaction history.
