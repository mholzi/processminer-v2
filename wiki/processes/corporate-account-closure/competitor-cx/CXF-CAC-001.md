## The journey
Revolut Business account closure is fully self-service in the app: profile icon → Account & documents → red Close Business account button → confirm. Pre-conditions are enforced in-app before the button is reachable: clients must downgrade to the free plan and zero all sub-account balances across all currencies. No staff interaction is required.

## Relevance
Revolut's instant, staff-free closure sets the fintech bar for corporate offboarding: no paper, no workflow handoff, no multi-day wait. The contrast with Deutsche Bank's multi-step, staff-mediated process is sharpest around immediacy and the total absence of a client-facing status layer—the client either completes or is blocked by a pre-condition, with no grey zone.

## Evidence
Heropay's 2026 guide describes the complete Revolut Business in-app closure flow, the mandatory balance-zeroing and plan-downgrade pre-conditions, and the irreversibility of closure once confirmed by the account owner.
