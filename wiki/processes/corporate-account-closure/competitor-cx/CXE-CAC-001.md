## The journey
Corporate clients initiate closure through My ING, ING's digital self-service platform, by completing an integrated online closure form. The system prompts clients to download statements, clear card balances, and arrange fund transfer to a new eurozone account. Accounts remain accessible until the transfer to the new bank is confirmed. No fees apply for euro-denominated transfers.

## Relevance
ING's fully digital self-service closure—no branch visit, no paper form for most business clients—demonstrates that European corporate banking peers can deliver an end-to-end digital offboarding journey. The absence of a comparable client-facing digital closure channel at Deutsche Bank means corporate clients currently cannot initiate or track their closure without staff mediation.

## Evidence
ING Luxembourg's public FAQ page for business account closure confirms the My ING digital form option, the guided document-download and balance-transfer steps, and the no-fee euro transfer policy for business clients.
