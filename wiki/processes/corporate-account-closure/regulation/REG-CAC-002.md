## What it requires
CDD documents and transaction records must be retained for at least five years from the end of the calendar year in which the business relationship ends. Records may be kept for up to ten years where a risk-based assessment requires it.

## Why it applies
Account closure marks the end of the business relationship under GwG. The five-year retention clock starts from year-end following closure. Deutsche Bank must archive KYC files, UBO declarations, and transaction records in retrievable form for FIU and BaFin inspection.

## How it is met
CP-CAC-007 (record-retention completeness review) verifies quarterly that closed-account files are present in the Records Archive and the retention schedule has been correctly applied. Specific GwG start date and maximum period to be confirmed with the SME.
