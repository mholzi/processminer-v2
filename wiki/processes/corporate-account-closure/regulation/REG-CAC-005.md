## What it requires
Material banking decisions must be reviewed and authorised by at least two independent individuals. No single person may both prepare and authorise the same operation (four-eyes principle, Vier-Augen-Prinzip) in client lifecycle management.

## Why it applies
Account closure is an irreversible, material operation with financial and regulatory consequences. BaFin requires that operational risk in client lifecycle management is controlled through independent authorisation; a single analyst preparing and approving the same closure creates unacceptable operational risk.

## How it is met
CP-CAC-005 (segregation of duties — 4-eyes closure approval) mandates that the Closure Approver is independent of the Closure Analyst who prepared the file; the approver must not be the analyst who performed steps 2 through 5.
