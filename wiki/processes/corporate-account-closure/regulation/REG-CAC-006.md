## What it requires
Business correspondence and accounting vouchers must be retained for 6 years (correspondence) and 10 years (booking records and annual statements) from year-end. Under AO § 147 the same periods apply for tax purposes, independent of and in addition to AML retention.

## Why it applies
Account closure generates accounting entries, payment confirmations, and business correspondence that are business records subject to HGB § 257 and tax records subject to AO § 147. Deutsche Bank as a Kaufmann and taxpayer must retain these documents regardless of whether the client relationship continues.

## How it is met
CP-CAC-007 (record-retention completeness review) verifies the archive is complete and the retention schedule correctly applied. The schedule should reconcile HGB, AO, and GwG requirements by applying the longest applicable period; this mapping is to be confirmed with the SME.
