## What it requires
If any fact indicates a suspicion that assets are connected to money laundering or terrorist financing, Deutsche Bank must immediately report this to the FIU (Zentralstelle für Finanztransaktionsuntersuchungen), regardless of the amount involved.

## Why it applies
The compliance screening at closure may surface indicators of financial crime. A closure request could itself be a suspicious indicator if the client seeks to exit before a pending compliance case is resolved. Deutsche Bank must assess and report before disbursing funds.

## How it is met
CP-CAC-003 blocks closure when an open compliance case is present and routes it to Compliance for adjudication. Whether a formal documented SAR assessment is completed for every closure case is to be confirmed with the SME.
