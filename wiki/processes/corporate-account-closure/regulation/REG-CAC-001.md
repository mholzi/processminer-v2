## What it requires
If a bank cannot comply with customer due diligence requirements, the business relationship must not be established or, for existing relationships, must be terminated. The bank must not process closure in a way that bypasses an unresolved CDD deficiency.

## Why it applies
Deutsche Bank is an obliged entity under GwG. Closure is the formal termination of the business relationship; if an open compliance case reflects a CDD failure, the closure procedure is blocked until Compliance clears it.

## How it is met
CP-CAC-003 automatically screens for open compliance cases at the start of the closure procedure and blocks any case where Compliance has not cleared the client. The CDD exit assessment is adjudicated by Compliance before closure can proceed.
