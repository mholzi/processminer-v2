## The gap
The reconciled multi-regime retention schedule — GwG (5 years), HGB (6/10 years), AO (10 years), GDPR storage limitation — has not been defined or approved (PG-CAC-003). The Records Archive automation in TS-CAC-003 has no schedule to enforce until the policy exists.

## Resolution
A Legal/Compliance policy workstream must produce and approve the retention schedule before TD-CAC-004 can be implemented. This is a sequencing dependency: the policy decision must precede the technical automation. The AMLA Technical Standards due July 2026 may introduce additional requirements the schedule must accommodate.

## Status
Open. A blocking dependency for TS-CAC-003; the AMLA Technical Standards due July 2026 act as a forcing function.
