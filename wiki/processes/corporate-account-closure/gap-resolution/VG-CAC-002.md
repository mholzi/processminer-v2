## The gap
No agentic AI orchestration capability exists in the corporate account closure process or its confirmed systems landscape. The obligations check, compliance file assembly, and post-closure reconciliation all rely on sequential manual steps. Realising TS-CAC-001 requires a capability the bank does not currently have in this process.

## Resolution
Adoption of an agentic AI orchestration platform (TD-CAC-001) closes this gap. The resolution depends on a bank-level platform selection decision and a deployment timeline aligned with the broader agentic AI programme. Fiserv agentOS is widely available August 2026.

## Status
Open. The agentic AI market is maturing rapidly; delayed commitment risks widening the efficiency gap against peers (ING, Fiserv agentOS adopters).
