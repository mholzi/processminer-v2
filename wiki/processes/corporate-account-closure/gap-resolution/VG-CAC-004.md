## The gap
GwG § 43 requires immediate FIU reporting when facts indicate a suspicion of money laundering or terrorist financing. The compliance and sanctions check (step 4, CP-CAC-003) blocks closure on a hit but does not evidence a formal SAR assessment for every closure case, leaving the SAR obligation undocumented.

## Resolution
A Compliance policy decision is required on when a SAR assessment is triggered within the closure workflow, how it is documented, and how the SAR reporting timeline interacts with the closure SLA. Once defined, the agentic AI workflow (TS-CAC-001) should include a SAR assessment step as part of the automated compliance file assembly.

## Status
Open. A compliance design gap requiring Compliance input before TS-CAC-001 can be fully specified.
