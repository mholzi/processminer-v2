## The gap
The current Client Lifecycle Workflow Tool has no client-facing module. Corporate clients cannot initiate closure digitally, track status, or receive automated confirmation — the capability required by TS-CAC-002 does not exist in the current architecture.

## Resolution
To realise TS-CAC-002, the bank must extend the existing tool with a client portal module or adopt a CLM platform with native client-facing lifecycle capabilities. TD-CAC-002 defines the build-or-buy decision that resolves this gap.

## Status
Open. Dependent on the TD-CAC-002 platform decision and a product or engineering commitment.
