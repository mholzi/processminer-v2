## Responsibility
Serves as the primary client contact and raises or receives corporate account closure requests on behalf of the bank.

## In this process
Accountable for ensuring the corporate account closure request is raised and routed to the Client Lifecycle Workflow Tool (step 1), and accountable for the final client confirmation and archiving (step 8). Informed across all intermediate steps. When signatory authority cannot be confirmed, the request is returned to the Relationship Manager to obtain correct authorisation from the client.
