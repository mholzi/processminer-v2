## Responsibility
Performs sanctions and compliance-case screening; resolves live sanctions hits and open internal compliance cases that block account closures.

## In this process
Consulted on mandate and authority verification (step 2); accountable and responsible for the compliance and sanctions check (step 4), with the Closure Analyst triggering the automated screen and Compliance adjudicating all results. Any live sanctions hit or open internal compliance case routes to Compliance for resolution before closure can resume.
