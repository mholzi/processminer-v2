## Responsibility
Performs sanctions and compliance-case screening and resolves open internal compliance cases that block account closures.

## In this process
Consulted on mandate and authority verification (step 2); accountable and responsible for the compliance and sanctions check (step 4), with the Closure Analyst triggering the automated screen and Compliance reviewing and adjudicating results. When a live sanctions hit is identified, the case routes to Financial Crime; when an internal compliance case is identified, Compliance owns resolution before closure can resume.
