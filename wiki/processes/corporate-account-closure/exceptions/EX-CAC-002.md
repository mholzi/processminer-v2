## Description
The account has unsettled items — pending payments, uncleared cheques, fees due, or linked products with a balance — that prevent closure.

## Handling
Closure is suspended and the client is notified of the outstanding items to settle. The closure process resumes once all items are cleared.

## Impact
Closure is suspended and delayed until all outstanding obligations are settled.
