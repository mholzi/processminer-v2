## Description
The residual credit balance cannot be returned to the client's nominated account.

## Handling
The balance is moved to a suspense account and treated as unclaimed funds in accordance with the bank's policy.

## Impact
The client does not receive the residual balance; the funds are held in suspense pending policy-driven disposition.
