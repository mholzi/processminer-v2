## The move
Citi launched a Digital Servicing Hub within its CitiDirect commercial banking platform in August 2025, giving corporate clients a self-service portal for document submission, status tracking, and account queries. KYC renewals are handled via a 'one field, one-time' digital process with pre-populated data and automated notifications.

## Relevance
Once a closure request is logged, the client has no visibility into the bank-side steps: obligations clearance, compliance screening, and final confirmation all proceed without client status updates. Citi's self-service hub shows a global peer setting an expectation of real-time status and document submission that this process does not meet.

## Evidence
CitiDirect Digital Servicing Hub live August 2025; 57% of Citi's commercial banking clients onboard; ERP integration reduced from 7 weeks to under 1 day; 2.3 million active user sessions as of July 2025.
