## The move
Fiserv launched agentOS on 14 May 2026 — an enterprise operating system that lets banks deploy, govern, and scale AI agents natively across their core, payments, and servicing platforms. Initial agents include an Agentic AML Triage Analysis module and a reconciliation use case, with nine marketplace partners covering financial crimes compliance.

## Relevance
Banks adopting agentOS can deploy a governed AML Triage agent that handles the sanctions-screening adjudication and post-closure reconciliation steps that today require Compliance staff and daily Finance review. Banks that integrate this platform will run automated, auditable closure compliance checks in the time it currently takes Compliance to manually review a single case.
