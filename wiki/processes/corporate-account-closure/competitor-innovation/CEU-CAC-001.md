## The move
ING is redesigning all client due diligence workflows using agentic AI agents that work from existing data rather than manual analyst steps. All manual labor in KYC and client lifecycle checks is being rebuilt around AI-first models, with agentic AI driving live origination from 2026.

## Relevance
The mandate verification and obligations check in this process are both entirely manual and sequential. ING's move to AI-driven CDD shows a European peer removing the equivalent manual steps from its client lifecycle. If Deutsche Bank does not follow, the efficiency gap in multi-step manual closure checks will widen.

## Evidence
Computer Weekly reports ING is redesigning all manual KYC workflows with agentic AI in 2026, with ING allocating 15% of operating expenses to technology. Agentic AI mortgages begin in 2026.
