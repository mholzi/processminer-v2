## What happens
A closure confirmation is sent to the client. The account file is archived in the Records Archive and retained in accordance with the record-retention schedule.

## Inputs
- Executed closure from step 7
- Complete closure case file

## Outputs
- Closure confirmation sent to the client
- Account file archived in the Records Archive per the retention schedule

## Why it matters
Notifies the client that their account is closed and ensures the complete file is retained for regulatory and audit purposes.
