## The gap
Whether a closure can be reversed once executed, and within what time window, is not addressed in this document.

## Impact
Without a defined reversal procedure, there is no guidance for teams if an account is closed in error, increasing the risk of client harm and operational confusion.

## Next step
Determine with the process owner whether closure reversal is possible, and if so, document the procedure and reversal window.
